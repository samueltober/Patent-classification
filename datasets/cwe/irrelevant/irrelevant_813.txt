 WOA A cover assembly is disclosed The cover assembly may be installed on an axle assembly which comprises an axle rotating by using power received from a power source a housing surrounding one end portion of the axle and a bearing disposed between the housing and the one end portion of the axle so as to support the axle with respect to the housing so that the axle may rotate By integrally forming a generator capable of generating electricity using the rotation of the axle on the axle assembly power may be supplied for data detection signal conversion and wireless communication Accordingly sufficient data for frequency analysis may be secured and abnormalities of the axle assembly may be diagnosed in real time
