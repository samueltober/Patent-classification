 USA A sealing device for bearing units is disclosed herein The sealing device may comprise a static component and a dynamic component The static component is provided with a shaped metal support with a plurality of noncontacting sealing lips and with two contacting lips The dynamic component defines a labyrinth seal with a second sealing lip of said noncontacting sealing lips a third lip preferably noncontacting being positioned along the labyrinth seal and having a full angular extension so as to form a full seal against any ingress of contaminants into the bearing unit
