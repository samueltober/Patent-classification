 USA Embodiments are directed to a modular multilevel DCDC power electronic converter for transferring power from or between a highervoltage DC network and a lowervoltage DC network The power electronic converter features a series connection of lowvoltage voltage source modules VSM and a current source module CSM The series connection of the submodule elements forms a string The highervoltage DC network is interfaced to the converter by connecting across the outer terminals of the string The lowervoltage DC network is interfaced to the converter through the CSM
