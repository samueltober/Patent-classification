 CNA The invention provides a hub coating method and a hub The hub coating method includes the steps that a hub base body to be coated is pretreated base powder spraying is conducted on the pretreated hub base body according to the preset film thickness and curing treatment is conducted under the first curing condition colored paint spraying is conducted on the hub base body sprayed with base powder and curing treatment is conducted under the second curing condition the hub base body sprayed with colored paint is subjected to varnish spraying curing treatment is carried out under the third curing condition and a coating process is carried out on the surface of the hub base body so that the obtained hub coating has good corrosion resistance and coating stability According to the hub coating method and the hub compared with an existing production process the prepared hub coating prepared by the hub coating method can meet the requirement of corrosion resistance during hub operation in a severe environment
