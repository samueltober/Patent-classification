 CNU The utility model provides a suspension support structure The suspension support structure comprises an upper support body for supporting the upper end part of a piston rod of the shock absorber anupper spring seat for supporting the upper end part of a spiral spring and a bearing arranged between the upper support body and the upper spring seat the bearing is axially inclined to the axial direction of the shock absorber and the suspension support structure is also provided with a rubber pad arranged between the upper support body and the bearing Bearing made of resin Based on the structure the durability of the resin bearing can be prevented from being reduced
