 CNA The invention provides a reconfigurable modular robot system which comprises module assemblies and connecting assemblies the connecting assemblies are fixedly connected with the module assemblies and the multiple module assemblies are connected in series through the connecting assemblies Each module assembly comprises a driving assembly a base an active suspension assembly a passive suspension assembly and a control system the driving assembly is fixedly connected with the base through the active suspension assembly and the passive suspension assembly and the control system is arrangedin the base The control systems are electrically connected with the driving assemblies and the active suspension assemblies the multiple module assemblies are arranged connection of the multiple modules is achieved through the connecting assemblies the bearing capacity endurance capacity and passing capacity higher than those of a single module assembly are achieved the combination of the active suspension and the passive suspension is adopted the field basic passing capacity and maneuverability of the single module are improved and the bearing capacity is improved
