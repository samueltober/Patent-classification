The invention discloses a testing device for a multipressurelevel steam extraction heat supply steam turbine generator unit The device comprises an electric power tester a current converter a voltage converter and a data collection and control module wherein the current converter converts current outputted by a generator stator and then transmits the converted current to the electric powertester the voltage converter converts voltage outputted by the generator stator and then transmits the converted voltage to the electric power tester and a power directcurrent voltage signal outputted by the electric power tester is transmitted to the data acquisition and control module According to the testing device provided by the embodiment of the invention the electric power is monitoredin real time and peakvalley adjustment is performed according to requirements so unnecessary energy loss is reduced and the cost is saved
