 CNU The utility model discloses a wearresistant bearing of an automobile hub relates to the technical field of automobile hubs and aims to solve the problems that an existing automobile hub bearing is not stable enough in installation performance not high enough in protection performance in the using process of the bearing and large in abrasion along with the increase of using time A connecting fixing sleeve is installed at one end of the wearresisting bearing body the connecting fixing sleeve and the wearresisting bearing body are of an integrated structure a fixing installation hole is formed in the connecting fixing sleeve an outer fixing bearing sleeve is installed on the outer side of the wearresisting bearing body and an inner fixing bearing sleeve is installed on the inner side of the wearresisting bearing body A rotating fixing groove is formed between the inner fixing bearing sleeve and the protective fixing cover and the protective fixing cover is installed on the outer side of the rotating fixing groove
