 TWA The present invention discloses a power conversion apparatus including a power supply circuit a load switch and a control circuit The power supply circuit generates a first power source The load switch controls the power supplying of the power supply circuit to a load The control circuit generates a switching control signal to control the conducting level of the load switch according to an enabling signal The control circuit controls the level of the switching control signal to soft start from first level to second level Among the soft start period the switching control signal has plural waveforms including first waveform and second waveform The change speed of the first waveform level is higher than that of the second waveform level The first waveform level occurs earlier than the second waveform level
