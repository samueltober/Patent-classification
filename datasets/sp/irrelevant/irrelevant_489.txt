 WOA The invention relates to a suspension element   of a vehicle comprising a coil spring   and a bearing part   provided with a main stopper   the spring   being mounted on the bearing part   such that a first end   of the spring   bears against the main stopper   According to the invention the bearing part  comprises a secondary stopper  which is placed in front of the main stopper  and against which the first end  of the spring  bears during a method for assembling the suspension element  the secondary stopper  being capable of retracting under the effect of pressure exerted by the spring  on the secondary stopper  during a step of compressing the spring  after the spring  has pivoted about its axis of revolution during a step of straightening the latter to cause a second end  of the spring  to interact with a centring element  in order to bear against the main stopper
