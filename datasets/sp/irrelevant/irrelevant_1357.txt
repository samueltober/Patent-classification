 CNA The invention provides a spring seat installation reinforcing structure and the structure comprises a horizontallyarranged spring seat plate connected with the lower surface of a longitudinal beamwherein the lower plate face of the spring seat plate is used for installing a spring seat part of a plate body of the spring seat plate is suspended and a side reinforcing plate is connected between the suspended part of the spring seat plate and the side wall of the longitudinal beam According to the scheme the load of the spring seat can be effectively transmitted to the side face of the longitudinal beam through the side reinforcing plates the spring seat plate is prevented from bearing large load in the running process of an automobile and meanwhile the bending resistance of the spring seat plate is improved through the side reinforcing plates
