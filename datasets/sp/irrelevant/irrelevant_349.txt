 WOA The invention relates inter alia to a rigid axle  as the rear axle of a motor vehicle  having an axle tube  made from a steel and extending in the transverse direction of the vehicle Y direction on which at both ends a respective wheel mounting  made of a steel is arranged Advantageously the wheel mountings  are formed as forgings and on the end face the axle tube  is connected cohesively by welding directly to the wheel mounting
