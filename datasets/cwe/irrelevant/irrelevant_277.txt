 CNA The invention relates to the technical field of intelligent driving equipment and particularly relates to a wheel type fulldrive chassis A driving module of the wheel type fulldrive chassis comprises two driving module groups Each driving module group comprises two driving wheels and a driving motor for providing a driving force for the two driving wheels the driving motor is located betweenthe two driving wheels each driving wheel is connected with a first synchronous wheel the driving motor is connected with a second synchronous wheel and the second synchronous wheels are connectedwith the two first synchronous wheels on the two sides through transmission devices According to the scheme the four driving wheels are adopted and two driving motors are used for driving Therefore the chassis with the structure has high stability in a moving process smooth and stable operation of mobile equipment is achieved phenomena of slipping drifting and motion control instability in the moving process of the chassis are eradicated and production cost is low
