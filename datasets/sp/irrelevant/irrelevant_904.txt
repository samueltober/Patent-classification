 CNA The invention belongs to the technical field of automobile chassis suspensions A watt link device capable of being adjusted in a radial rotation mode and a torsion beam assembly are provided The watt link device comprise a center swing arm a left link and a right link wherein the left link and the right link are hinged to the two ends of the center swing arm respectively and hinge parts are arranged at the outer ends of the left link and the right link respectively Each of the left link and the right link comprises a first link body a second link body and an adjusting unit wherein thefirst link body and the second link body are connected through the adjusting unit the first link body can rotate in the radial direction relative to the second link body the axial direction of thefirst link body and the axial direction of the second link body are relatively fixed and a watt link assembly is arranged between a middle cross beam of the torsion beam assembly and the axis of a wheel installation base The link structure is reasonable in overall structural design the link structure can achieve radial rotation and axial adjustment functions twisting force brought to the linkwhen the torsion beam moves up and down is effectively eliminated and then the service lives of connecting rubber sleeves at the two ends of the link are effectively prolonged
