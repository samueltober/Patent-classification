 CNU The invention provides an instantaneous daily difference measuring device and relates to the technical field of stopwatch detection the measuring device comprises an electric field sensor and a signal measuring circuit and the electric field sensor is physically connected with the signal measuring circuit the electric field sensor is used for converting an electric field signal generated by an internal crystal oscillator of the electronic stopwatch to be tested into a voltage signal the signal measuring circuit is used for collecting the frequency value of the voltage signal output by the electric field sensor and converting the frequency value into the daily difference value of the electronic stopwatch to be measured In this way the electric field sensor and the signal measuring circuit can be used for accurately measuring the tobemeasured electronic stopwatch made of metal materials the measuring speed is increased and the actual measuring requirement is met
