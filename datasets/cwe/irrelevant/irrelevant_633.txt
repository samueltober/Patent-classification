 CNA The invention discloses a laser additive hub bearing unit and a treatment method The laser additive hub bearing unit mainly comprises a bearing inner ring and the like wherein the bearing inner ring is installed at one end of a flange plate an outer side roller path is arranged at the root of a shaft neck of the flange plate the outer side roller path and an inner side roller path of the bearing inner ring form double rows of roller paths of the hub bearing unit and a bearing rolling body is installed between the double rows of roller paths and the bearing rolling body is kept fixed by a retainer the bearing outer ring is installed at the outer side of the bearing rolling body and sealing structures are arranged among the bearing outer ring the bearing inner ring and the flange plate According to the invention a laser additive manufacturing technology is utilized laser cladding is carried out on the surface of a base body with the strength requirement in the hub bearing with cast iron as a base body material iron powder and other reinforcing materials are clad to corresponding positions the using strength requirement of the hub bearing unit is guaranteed and meanwhile the effect of saving cost is achieved and meanwhile the additional characteristics of corrosion resistance wear resistance heat resistance and the like are obtained through regulation and control by different cladding materials
