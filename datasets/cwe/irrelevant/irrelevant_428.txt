 KRA According to the present invention disclosed is an axle device for a vehicle The disclosed axle device for a vehicle includes a wheel disk unit that comes in contact with a wheel a wheel bearing unit that comes in contact with the wheel disk unit and is coupled to a knuckle a coupling unit that is coupled to the wheel disk unit in a direction perpendicular to the axial direction of the wheel disk unit and removes a gap between the wheel disk unit and the wheel bearing unit by pressing the wheel bearing unit and a wheel coupling unit for coupling the wheel disk unit the wheel bearing unit and the wheel
