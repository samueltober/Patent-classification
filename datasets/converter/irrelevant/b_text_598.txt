 WOA This document presents a modular power supply system  for providing electric power to electronic devices The system  comprises a power module  comprising a first housing  a display screen  a first output  a first connecting unit  and a power converter unit  for converting electrical power to have suitable characteristics for supplying to the an electronic device The system  also comprises a main battery unit  comprising a second housing  slots  for receiving batteries  and battery connectors  arranged into the slots  for connecting with the batteries  and connected in parallel with each other a control unit  configured for controlling charging andor discharging of the batteries  and a second connecting unit  The first connecting unit  and the second connecting unit  are arranged to be connected removably to each other
