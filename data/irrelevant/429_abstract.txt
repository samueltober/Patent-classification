The invention discloses a modular parallel energy storage system and a parallel scheduling management method The modular parallel energy storage system comprises energy storage converter modules storage battery modules a master control module and an energy management module and the plurality of storage battery modules are respectively connected with the plurality of energy storage converter modules each energy storage converter module is connected to the general control module the general control module is connected with the energy management module and the energy management module is responsible for dispatching and managing the general control module and the energy storage converter modules According to the invention the energy storage converter in the modular parallel energy storage system is scheduled in turn and the energy storage converter modules work in turn during nonfullpower operation so that the service life of the energy storage converter is effectively prolonged
