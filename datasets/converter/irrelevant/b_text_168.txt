 CNB The embodiment of the invention provides a display driving circuit a control method thereof and a display device is used for reducing the unstable voltage of a pullup node The display driving circuit comprises a time sequence controller a power management integration subcircuit and a grid driving subcircuit The time sequence controller provides time sequence signals to the power management integration subcircuit and the gate driving subcircuit respectively The power management integration subcircuit under the control of a time sequence signal output by the time sequence controller provides signals for a clock signal end and a first control signal end of a firstlevel shift register in the grid driving subcircuit respectively The voltage amplitude of the effective signal received by the clock signal end is different from the voltage amplitude of the effective signal received by the first control signal end
