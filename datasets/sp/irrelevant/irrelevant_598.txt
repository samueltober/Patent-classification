 CNA The invention discloses an active suspension control system and method for complex road conditions wherein the system comprises an environment sensing module which comprises a speed sensor an infrared structured light assembly and a monocular camera a realtime processing control module which comprises a BP neural network and is used for receiving the data from the speed sensor and the infrared structured light assembly outputting the damping and rigidity values suitable for the current road condition and obtaining the corrected damping and rigidity values in combination with the data outputted by a convolutional neural network and an adjusting module which is used for transmitting the corrected damping and rigidity values to an active suspension and adjusting the damping and rigidity of the active suspension On the premise of ensuring the realtime performance and the precision the cost of acquiring the road surface information is reduced and the adaptability to the road surface is improved
