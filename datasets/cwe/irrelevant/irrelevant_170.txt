 WOA According to one embodiment of the present invention a fault diagnosis device mounted on a wheel bearing for diagnosing whether there is a fault or malfunction of the wheel bearing is provided A fault diagnosis device according to one embodiment of the present invention may comprise a detection unit for detecting an operating state of the wheel bearing and a diagnosis unit for diagnosing whether there is a fault or malfunction of the wheel bearing by using signal information detected by the detection unit According to one embodiment of the present invention the detection unit may comprise an acceleration sensor for collecting acceleration information of a wheel bearing and the diagnosis unit may be configured to diagnose whether there is a fault or malfunction of the wheel bearing by frequency analyzing acceleration signal information collected from the acceleration sensor According to one embodiment of the present invention the diagnosis unit may be configured to collect event occurrence information by analyzing acceleration signal information collected from the acceleration sensor
