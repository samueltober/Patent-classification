 USB The disclosure relates to a check rail for a wheel suspension of a wheel in a vehicle wherein the wheel suspension may include a wheel carrier configured to support a wheel of a vehicle the wheel carrier may include an elastic check rail body that may include a fiberreinforced composite material with a first check rail end and a second check rail end wherein the first check rail end is configured to be fastened to a structural body component of the vehicle wherein the first check rail end is constructed without a bearing and a pivot bearing on the second check rail end and configured to be rotatably fastened on the wheel carrier
