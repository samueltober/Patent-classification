 CNA The invention discloses a wireless charging device and electronic equipment which belong to the technical field of electronics The wireless charging device comprises a wireless charging chip and a multistage voltage reduction module wherein the input end of the wireless charging chip is connected with alternating current so as to convert alternating current voltage into direct current voltageand the wireless charging chip is connected to a processor of electronic equipment the output end of the previous stage of the multistage voltage reduction module is connected to the input end of the next stage of the multistage voltage reduction module the input end of the first stage of the multistage voltage reduction module is connected to the first output end of the wireless charging chip the output end of the last stage of the multistage voltage reduction module is connected to a charging management module of the electronic equipment and the control ends of all stages of the multistage voltage reduction module are connected to the second output end of the wireless charging chip
