 CNU The utility model discloses an antideviation wheel for stabilizing an automobile body during automobile tire burst The antideviation wheel comprises a tire body  an air storage bin  an inflation nozzle  a hub  tire patterns  a shared tire wall  an air pressure balance valve  a movable block  a shared toe opening  and a toe opening groove  According to theutility model the vehicle direction the vehicle posture the lane keeping and the like can be controlled during vehicle tire burst and after tire burst so that the vehicle body is balanced and stably runs for a certain distance and then measures are taken and traffic accidents caused by improper operation of a driver to the vehicle during tire burst and after tire burst are prevented
