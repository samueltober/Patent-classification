The invention provides a light source driving circuit a lamp panel a light source driving device and a display device The light source driving circuit comprises a first power supply conversion circuit and a second power supply conversion circuit connected with the first power supply conversion circuit The first power supply conversion circuit converts and outputs a target current signal to thesecond power supply conversion circuit according to the input working power supply and a data signal the second power supply conversion circuit receives the target current signal and outputs a current driving signal with a corresponding magnitude to a light source connected with a driving pin when receiving a line scanning signal so as to drive the light source the first power supply conversioncircuit and the second power supply conversion circuit are arranged in an integrated mode the structure is simple when the light source driving circuit and the light source are soldered to the lamppanel the soldering reject ratio can be reduced the production yield of the lamp panel is increased and then the reliability of the whole lamp panel is improved
