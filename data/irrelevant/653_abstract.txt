The invention discloses an energy storage configuration and operation strategy considering dynamic characteristics for stabilizing system power fluctuation and provides an active power distribution network energy storage operation strategy for stabilizing system power fluctuation and considering charging and discharging dynamic characteristics of an allvanadium redox flow battery according to acharging and discharging power modeling result of a battery energy storage system An energy storage configuration model taking fixed cost operation cost direct economic benefit environmental benefit and the like of the battery energy storage system in the life cycle of the energy storage system as optimization objectives is constructed energy storage installation capacity power and installation position are taken as decision variables and the model is solved by utilizing a dynamic programming algorithm On the basis of a typical solarwind output curve an optimal energy storage configuration result is obtained and system tie line power and a dynamic characteristic curve of an energy storage system before and after the energy storage device is installed are obtained at the same time
