 DEA The invention relates to a wheel bearing unit for arrangement on a wheel hub  with a wheel bearing flange  comprising a wheel bearing  the wheel bearing  comprising an inner ring  and an outer ring  between which a plurality of rolling elements  are arranged a first sealing plate  being attached to the inner ring  or a component connected nonrotatably to the inner ring  with between an end face of the wheel bearing flange  and the wheel bearing  a gap  is formed It is provided that a centrifugal element  is arranged in the gap  and rests against the first sealing plate  or is connected to the first sealing plate  the centrifugal element  entering the gap   promotes penetrated water when the wheel hub  rotates from the gap
