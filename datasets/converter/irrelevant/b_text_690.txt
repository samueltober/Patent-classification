 CNA The invention relates to a singlephase multilevel cascaded Hbridge converter device and a control method which belong to the field of power electronics The device comprises an inductor a threestage cascaded singlephase Hbridge converter a directcurrent side voltage signal acquisition module an alternatingcurrent side current signal acquisition module a carrier phase shift module anda control module The control method is an alternating voltage sensorfree control method based on a natural coordinate frame the defects of a traditional control method are overcome coordinate transformation and zerocrossing detection are not needed an alternating voltage sensor and a phaselocked loop are omitted through a virtual flux linkage estimation algorithm and highfrequency interference is eliminated the method is simple and easy to implement the device cost is reduced and the control of the multilevel converter is simpler more reliable and more economical
