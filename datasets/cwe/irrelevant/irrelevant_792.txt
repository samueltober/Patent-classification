 CNU     The utility model discloses a labyrinth type wheel hub bearing unit sealing structure which relates to the field of wheel hub bearings It includes an inner frame and an outer frame The inner frame is closely attached to the inner diameter of the outer ring The outer circumference of the inner frame is covered with inner rubber Gluing and adhesion are integrated and the inner rubber is provided with at most two contact lips for matching with the outer frame the head of the outer frame is covered with outer rubber and the two are glued and adhered as a whole and the outer rubber is far away from the outer frame One side of the ring is in close contact with the upper end of the flange the side of the outer frame that is not covered by the outer rubber is in close contact with the lower end of the flange and the tail of the outer frame is in close contact with the outer diameter of the flange the outer rubber is close to One side of the outer ring is provided with a number of lips for matching with the outer ring The utility model has high sealing performance meets the resistance to the intrusion of external muddy water under various working conditions has strong antileakage ability to the lubricating grease inside the bearing adopts a twolip contact structure which greatly reduces the friction level of the bearing
