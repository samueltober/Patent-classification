The utility model provides a power supply management system supporting multipath power supply input automatic switching relates to the technical field of power supply management and can be used ina vehiclemounted system a distributed direct current power supply management system an industrial control system and a highpower battery power supply system Power supply to various loads is realized through automatic switching of multipath power supply input and power supply conversion According to the invention the multipath power supply input can be directly connected to the corresponding input pins of the two LTC chips respectively according to the power supply priority requirement of the system and as long as one path of power supply input is effective the system load can operate normally the utility model provides a power supply management system supporting multipath power supply input automatic switching and aims to reduce complexity of a power supply circuit reduce size set power supply priority according to actual needs realize automatic switching among multipath power supplies and ensure stable operation of a load
