 KRA The present invention relates to a hub bearing coating apparatus for preventing coating by masking a connection portion of a hub bearing and performing coating by exposing the remaining portions The coating apparatus of the present invention has an upper housing which can move up and down to prevent separation of the hub bearing during rotation and at the same time mask a lower end portion by clamping a lower side of the hub bearing thereby preventing coating In addition since the coating apparatus of the present invention performs spray metal coating after simultaneously masking the upper and lower connection portions of the hub bearing the coating of the hub bearing can be completed within minutes
