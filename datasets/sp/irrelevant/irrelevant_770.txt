 DEA     Embodiments of the present disclosure relate to a method and system for controlling active suspension of a moving vehicle In one embodiment the system collects road surface conditions sensed by lead vehicles as read ahead data RAD for use by the moving sameway vehicle to control an active suspension control system of the moving vehicleThe system determines one or more correction adjustments to be made based on the determination of a deviation in the detection of the RAD based on the vehicle conditions of the moving vehicle Furthermore the system determines false positive errors based on contradicting RAD and the actual road surface conditions of the path and applies the false positive error correction which enables the system to be used appropriately to control the active suspension of the target vehicle to avoid further obstacles and thus the driving quality of the vehicles and the comfort for the occupants is improved
