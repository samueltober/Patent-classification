 KRA The present invention relates to a suspension thrust bearing device  which is to be used with a suspension spring  in a vehicle suspension strut The suspension thrust bearing device of the present invention comprises a bearing including upper and lower annular bearing members     and a damping element  An axial reinforcing insert  in a hub  of the lower annular bearing member  includes a lower end part  curved toward the inside of the suspension thrust bearing device  Also the lower annular bearing member  includes at least one narrow groove  opened radially outward and having a bottom end part in an axial downward direction and the damping element  includes a part  fitted into the narrow groove
