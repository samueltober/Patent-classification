 USB The disclosure relates generally to methods systems and apparatuses for automated or assisted driving and more particularly relates to identification localization and navigation with respect to bollard receivers A method for detecting bollard receivers includes receiving perception data from one or more perception sensors of a vehicle The method includes determining based on the perception data a location of one or more bollard receivers in relation to a body of the vehicle The method also includes providing an indication of the location of the one or more bollard receivers to one or more of a driver and component or system that makes driving maneuver decisions
