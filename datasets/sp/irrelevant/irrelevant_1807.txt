 CNA The invention discloses a moving mechanism which comprises a frame and a power system a driving system a suspension system an adjusting system and wheels are mounted on the frame The adjusting system is installed on the frame through the suspension system the wheels are installed on the suspension system and the power system directly drives the wheels to rotate through the driving system According to the suspension system designed by the moving mechanism the change of the ground clearance of the frame is realized through the extension and retraction of the first hydraulic cylinder so that the frame can be lifted when crossing a larger obstacle the overall passing capacity and obstacle crossing capacity of the frame are further improved and the moving mechanism is suitable for being used in a special road surface environment under the cooperation of the suspension system the adjusting system and the driving system when the frame ascends and descends rotation of the wheels can still be achieved and the passing performance is further enhanced
