 CNB The present invention discloses a hydraulic interconnection suspension pipeline connection mode switching device and a control method A sensor unit comprises a vehicle speed sensor and four range finders and the vehicle speed sensor and the four range finders are responsible for inputting the realtime vehicle speed and the realtime length of four suspensions of a vehicle into an electronic control unit ECU respectively the ECU of a processing unit is used for calculating the realtime data of the vehicle by combining the data stored in an memory unit and an electromagnetic directional valve A and an electromagnetic directional valve B of an execution unit are used for controlling a hydraulic oil pipeline to be connected and disconnected and thus switching among different connection modes of the hydraulic interconnection suspensions is realized By applying the method the running state of the vehicle can be judged appropriate hydraulic interconnection suspension pipeline connection modes are thus selected the vehicle body posture is controlled and the riding comfort of the vehicle is improved
