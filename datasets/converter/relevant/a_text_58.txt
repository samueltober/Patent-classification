 WOA A power inverter for converting a DC voltage from a DC voltage source to AC voltage for a load eg a motor or power grid includes an inverter input terminal for connection to the DC voltage source an inverter output terminal for connection to the load at least two inverter legs connected in parallel with the inverter input terminal and a coupled inductor assembly The coupled inductor assembly includes at least one magnetic core and for each one of the inverter legs an associated pair of windings around the at least one magnetic core Each associated pair of windings includes a first winding in series connection with the inverter leg output terminal of the one of the inverter legs and a second winding in series opposing type connection with the first winding and in series connection with the inverter output terminal between the first winding and the inverter output terminal
