 KRA A wheel hub used in a wheel bearing assembly is provided The wheel hub comprises a cylindrical portion having an annular shape around an axial direction a hub flange extending radially outward from the outer circumferential surface of the cylindrical portion and a guide portion extending axially outward from the tip of the cylindrical portion The guide portion includes a first guide portion extending from the cylindrical portion to have a first axial length a second guide portion extending from the cylindrical portion to have a second axial length shorter than the first axial length and a guide division portion disposed between the first guide portion and the second guide portion based on the circumferential direction of the cylindrical portion and having a third axial length shorter than the second axial length
