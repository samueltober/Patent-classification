 CNU The utility model relates to a steelplastic composite sliding column upper support structure and belongs to the technical field of automobile design and manufacturing The upper support structure ofthe steelplastic composite sliding column comprises an upper plate a pressfitting bolt an upper cover a rubber bushing an inner framework a plastic reinforcing layer a spring upper seat a needle roller bearing and a buffer block mounting seat According to the upper support structure of the steelplastic composite sliding column the weight can be effectively reduced on the premise thatrigidity and strength are guaranteed the number of parts of the front sliding column is reduced assembling efficiency can be improved and the possibility of relative sliding between the independentsliding column bearing and the upper support of the sliding column is avoided
