 FRA     A wheel hub  has a onepiece outer ring  and a centering piece  The outer ring  has at least one outer race turned radially inward and defining a reference axis of the wheel hub  and a tubular cavity  and a flange  provided with holes  for fixing a wheel rim  The centering piece  is integral with the outer ring  and has at least one mounting zone   inserted in the outer ring  and a first centering zone  projecting axially outwardly with respect to the outer ring  and having a first cylindrical centering bearing surface  facing radially outwards
