 WOA Disclosed are a traveling system of a trolleybus and a trolleybus The traveling system is configured to be arranged below a trolleybus body in the trolleybus and the traveling system comprises an axle  extending in a transverse direction wherein two ends of the axle are connected to trolleybus wheels  by means of steering shafts  and a traction suspension device which is arranged on the axle wherein the traction suspension device comprises a leaf spring  and a suspension assembly one end of the leaf spring is connected to the trolleybus body by means of a trolleybus body connecting member  and the other end of the leaf spring is connected to the trolleybus body by means of the suspension assembly The traveling system of a trolleybus and the trolleybus have a good vibration reduction effect
