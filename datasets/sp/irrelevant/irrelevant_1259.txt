 CNU The utility model relates to a stepcrossing walking assembly and a robot and belongs to the technical field of intelligent robots The utility model provides a stepcrossing walking assembly which comprises a main supporting plate and the main supporting plate is used for being installed on a robot body The middle of the swing arm is rotationally connected with the main supporting plate the two hub motors are installed at the two ends of the swing arm respectively According to the stepcrossing walking assembly the two ends of the swing arm are each provided with one hub motor one of the two hub motors is made to grab the ground through swing of the swing arm and therefore driving of the stepcrossing walking assembly is guaranteed The utility model further provides a robot thetwo stepcrossing walking assemblies are used as the two front driving wheels the reliable stepcrossing effect is achieved steering can be conducted conveniently and rapidly and the robot is suitable for complex stepclimbing terrains
