 KRA According to an embodiment of the present invention provided is an encoder ring used to measure the rotational speed of a rotating element According to an embodiment of the present invention an encoder ring comprises a ring member mounted on a rotating element and an encoder provided on one side of the ring member The ring member may be configured to include an axial extension unit extending along an axial direction and a radial extension unit extending radially from the axial extension unit According to an embodiment of the present invention the ring member and the encoder may be formed of a plastic material and the ring member and the encoder may be integrally formed
