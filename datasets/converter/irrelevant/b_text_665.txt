 CNA The invention discloses a singlephase threephase integrated distribution transformer antishortcircuit bearing capacity test device which belongs to the field of distribution transformer antishortcircuit bearing capacity test detection and comprises an incoming line unit a phaseshifting transformer a frequency converter and an output filter the incoming line unit is connected with the phaseshifting transformer and the phaseshifting transformer is connected with the frequency converter The frequency converter is connected with an output filter which outputs a variablefrequency power supply According to the invention through cooperation of the wire inlet unit the phaseshifting transformer the frequency converter and the output filter wiring is automatically changed through the output highvoltage contactor when a test object is different and switching between a threephase output mode and a singlephase output mode is completed integration of singlephase and threephase tests is realized and the device has a good application prospect
