 CNA The invention provides a transverse stabilizer bar for achieving stepless rigidity adjustment of an FSAE formula racing car The transverse stabilizer bar serves as a transverse stabilizer bar in a rear suspension system of the racing car The transverse stabilizer bar is located above the rear axle and is easy to maintain and adjust The rigidity of the transverse stabilizing rod is adjusted by rotating the bent rod in threaded fit and stepless adjustment of the rigidity within the design range can be achieved
