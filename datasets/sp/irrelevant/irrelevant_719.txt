 CNU The utility model discloses a singlepoint height valve control air suspension control system which comprises an air cylinder a height valve a lifting valve a drive axle bearing air bag a matchingvalve and a followup bearing air bag Wherein an air outlet of the air cylinder is respectively communicated with an air inlet of the altitude valve and an air inlet of the lifting valve an air outlet of the altitude valve is communicated with two output ports of the lifting valve the other two output ports of the lifting valve are respectively communicated with an air inlet of the drive axlebearing air bag and an air inlet of the matching valve and an air outlet of the matching valve is communicated with the followup bearing air bag The cost of the valve control system is equivalent to that of a traditional valve control system and the valve control system further has the functions of height manual adjustment and axle load adjustment of an electric control air suspension system
