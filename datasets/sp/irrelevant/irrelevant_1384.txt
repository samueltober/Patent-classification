 CNA The invention relates to a front axle assembly with a shock absorber assisting in absorbing side impact energy The front axle assembly comprises a front axle a front cross beam and two longitudinalbumpers the two ends of the front cross beam are supported at the two ends of the front axle through the shock absorbers the two longitudinal bumpers are distributed on the two sides of the front cross beam in the length direction and the longitudinal bumpers are connected with the front cross beam through front axle part side impact energy eliminating devices The automobile side impact energyabsorption device has the advantages that side impact energy can be absorbed without crumpling deformation of the automobile frame when side impact occurs and the shock absorber supporting the automobile frame participates in energy absorption so that the problem of poor safety caused by the fact that an automobile body needs to crumple to absorb energy during side impact of an existing automobile is solved
