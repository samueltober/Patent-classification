 KRB The present invention relates to a vehicle drive axle with a sealing performance improving function and particularly to a vehicle drive axle with a sealing performance improving function which can seal a sensor portion without adding other separated components to block inflow of external impurities or foreign substance and prevent sensing performance degradation The vehicle drive axle includes a drive shaft a knuckle supporting a wheel bearing and a seal and boots wherein the boots has a sealed part with one end formed to be extended long in the radial direction of the wheel bearing and the wheel bearing is formed to have the extended length so that one cross section of an outer ring comes in contact with the sealed part of the boots
