 KRA The present invention relates to a vehicle driving support device capable of providing safety and convenience to a user a system including the same and a method thereof According to an embodiment of the present invention a vehicle driving support device can comprise a processor which determines a current gradient of a vehicle and a users steering intention using a vehicle internal signal and calculates a stroke which is a suspension control amount based on the current gradient of the vehicle and a users steering intention determination result and a storage unit for storing the stroke calculated by the processor and the vehicle internal signal
