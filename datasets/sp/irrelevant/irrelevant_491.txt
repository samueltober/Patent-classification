 DEA     A method for producing an active roll stabilizer  for a motor vehicle comprises the following steps Providing at least one stabilizer section b with bearing  mounted thereon for connecting the stabilizer section b to a vehicle body of the motor vehicle providing an actuator  orienting the stabilizer section b relative to the actuator  such that an actuatorside end  of the stabilizer section b faces a connection region  of the actuator  temporarily attaching a heat conducting device  to the stabilizer section b and  or to the actuator connecting stabilizer section b and actuator  with application of heat in particular by means of welding wherein the heat supplied during the connection at least to such an extent the heat conducting device  is discharged that overheating of the bearing  is avoided Removing the heat conducting device  from the stabilizer section b and  or actuator after completion of the connection process
