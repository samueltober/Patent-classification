 CNA The invention provides a wheel hub assembly  for a vehicle The assembly  has a central axis X and comprises the components of an outer ring  provided with with a tubular mounting portion  and a cover  which is used for closing an outer ring  and has a keying portion  that is inserted inside the tubular mounting portion  The cover  is made of plastic material the keying portion  comprises an annular seat  which is made of plastic and has a preset axial length and a sealing element  which is made of rubbery elastic material and is defined bya rotation member mounted inside the annular seat  over a whole of an axial length of the annular seat so as to project radially outside of the annular seat  to make contact with the tubularmounting portion  of the outer ring  thereby sealingly closing the wheel hub assembly
