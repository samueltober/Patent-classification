 KRB According to the present invention a thrust bearing assembly for a suspension includes an upper cap inserted into an insulator bushing having a bushing flange on the outside in a radial direction a lower cap installed in the upper cap to come in contact with the top of a coil spring a bearing installed between the upper and lower caps a first sealing member having an upper part combined with the upper cap and installed in a radial outer opening part formed between the upper and lower caps and a second sealing member installed in a radial inner opening part formed between the upper and lower caps The upper cap has an outer flange inserted into the bushing flange on the outside in a radial direction and at least one coupling member coming in contact with the inner surface of the bushing flange is installed on the outer edge surface of the outer flange The coupling member is made of a material which has lower hardness than a material of the upper cap
