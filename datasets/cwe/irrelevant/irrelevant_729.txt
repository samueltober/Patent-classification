 CNU The utility model provides an installation structure of a heavy truck front axle hub The installation structure is characterized by comprising a hub a steering knuckle a bearing a space ring an oil seal and an ABS gear ring The steering knuckle is sleeved with the hub the space ring and the bearing are both sleeved on the steering knuckle the oil seal is sleeved on the space ring the ABS gear ring is sleeved in a hole of the hub and the inner diameter of the oil seal is larger than the outer diameter of the bearing By adopting the structure the oil seal of the heavy truck front axle hub is timesaving and laborsaving to replace and the production efficiency is improved
