 CNA The invention relates to an air spring strut with a plastic air spring cover made of thermoplastic The air spring strut  for a motor vehicle comprises an air spring  and a shock damper  and is used for the springcushioning and damping of oscillations of a motor vehicle chassis wherein the air spring  comprises an air spring cover   and a rolling piston  wherein a rolling bellows  ofelastomer material is clamped in an airtight manner between the air spring cover   and the rolling piston  The air spring cover   comprises a damper bearing receptacle  in which a damper bearing of the shock damper is arranged and the air spring cover comprises a clamping base  to which a first end of the rolling bellows  is attached At least the damper bearing receptacle  of the air spring cover  is produced completely from a plastic material and the plastic material is a thermoplastic
