 USB A hub bearing for motor vehicles includes a rotatable hub and a bearing unit The bearing unit has a radially outer ring provided with respective radially outer raceways a radially inner ring provided with respective radially inner raceways and a plurality of rolling bodies positioned between the corresponding inner and outer raceways The hub bearing has an optical reading device for measuring the rotational speed of one of the hub or the radially inner ring The optical reading device is mounted on the radially outer ring
