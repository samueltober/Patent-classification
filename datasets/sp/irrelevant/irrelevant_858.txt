 FRA         The invention relates to a suspension support  for a motor vehicle comprising a first convex bearing surface  a second bearing surface  located in a central region  of the first bearing surface  a peripheral frame  which delimits the first bearing surface  and a strapping member  which extends at least in part around the first bearing surface  In the suspension support  according to the first aspect of the invention the first bearing surface  is intended to be brought to bear against a head of a supporting element of a suspension of the motor vehicle and the second bearing surface  is intended to be brought to bear against a shock absorber wedge  of the suspension of the motor vehicle    Figure to be published with the abstract Fig
