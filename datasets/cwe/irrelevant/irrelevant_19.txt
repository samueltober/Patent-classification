 WOA An aluminum alloy wheel hub and a method for coating a surface thereof The aluminum alloy wheel hub has an aluminum alloy matrix  and a coating  attached to a surface of the aluminum alloy matrix  The coating  which is attached to the surface of the aluminum alloy matrix  sequentially comprises a precoat layer  formed from a nickel coated aluminum powder or an aluminum coated nickel powder a CrC layer  and a varnish layer  In the invention a surface of an aluminum alloy wheel hub is spray coated with a CrC layer  and thus has better corrosion resistance
