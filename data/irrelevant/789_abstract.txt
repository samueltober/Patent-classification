The invention discloses a spectrum acquisition device Thespectrum acquisition device comprises a control module which is used for generating a corresponding driving signal according to a control signal sent by an upper computer and sending the driving signal to an acquisition module and an analogtodigital conversion module the acquisition module which comprises a plurality of CMOS acquisitioncircuits and is used for acquiring spectral analog signals according to the driving signals and sending the spectral analog signals to the analogtodigital conversion module the analogtodigital conversion module which converts the spectral analog signal into a spectral digital signal according to the driving signal and sends the spectral digital signal to an upper computer through the controlmodule and a power supply module which supplies power to the control module the acquisition module and the analogtodigital conversion module A plurality of CMOS acquisition circuits are arrangedto acquire light waves with different wavelengths so that the wavelength range of the acquired spectrum is increased and the power consumption and the cost are reduced the control module drives the acquisition module and the analogtodigital conversion module and through simple digital driving the stability of the whole acquisition device is stronger
