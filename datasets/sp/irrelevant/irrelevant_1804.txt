 WOA The present invention relates to a selfpowergenerating drone flying vehicle for air and land deployment in which a circular riding spherical body  is formed as a common usage space in which a person rides to fly transport operate and control a drone wing device  which is a flight device is mounted above the riding spherical body and a ground travel device which travels on the ground is mounted below the riding spherical body thus enabling both flight and ground travel and an intermediate connection frame device  for attachment and detachment is formed in the middle to allow the flight device and the ground travel device to be disconnected and connected as needed
