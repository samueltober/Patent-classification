B The present invention provides a modular signal conversion apparatus that can be combined with a data processing apparatus a data processing apparatus that can be combined with a modular signal conversion apparatus and a data processing system The modular signal conversion apparatus receives frequency data and control signal from the data processing apparatus generates and transmits clock signals thereto and receives multimedia signals and frequency data which are synchronized to the clock signals from the data processing apparatus The data processing apparatus receives clock signals from a clock generation unit of the modular signal conversion apparatus synchronizes previously stored multimedia signals to the clock signals and transmits synchronized multimedia signals to the modular signal conversion apparatus
