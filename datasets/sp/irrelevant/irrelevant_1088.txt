 KRA The invention relates to a suspension thrust bearing device  for use with a suspension spring  on a vehicle suspension strut of a vehicle The device  includes upper annular bearing members   and lower annular bearing members   that rotate relatively wherein the lower annular bearing members   have a body  provided with a builtin reinforcing insert  The device further comprises a damping element  made of an elastic material and interposed between the lower annular bearing members   and the suspension spring  wherein the damping element  and the lower bearing members   comprise the mechanical holding means    via the insert
