 USB A suspension thrust bearing for use with a suspension spring in an automotive suspension strut of a vehicle The suspension thrust bearing provides a bearing having upper and lower annular bearing members in relative rotation to each other and having upper and lower bearing surfaces respectively disposed in confronting alignment with each other The lower bearing surface has a contour shaped to axially receive an upper end of the suspension spring The upper bearing surface is shaped to be axially received by a support block attached to a body of the vehicle The suspension thrust bearing includes an upper isolator made of resilient material and fixed to the upper bearing surface such that the upper isolator fits between the upper annular bearing member and support block
