 CNA     In one aspect a lock nut is provided which includes a washer a threaded body and an actuator that are different from each other The washer is configured to form a nonrotatable connection with the main shaft of the vehicle The threaded body has threads to engage the threads of the vehicle main shaft The actuator has a rotation drive structure and is configured to rotate in the tightening direction to rotate the threaded body relative to the washer in the tightening direction The lock nut includes a lock operatively coupled to the actuator and configured to prevent rotation of the threaded body relative to the washer in the loosening direction The actuator is configured to rotate relative to the threaded body in the loosening direction to disengage the lock and allow the threaded body to rotate relative to the washer in the loosening direction
