 WOA The invention relates to a wheel module for a motor vehicle comprising a wheel  which is rotatable through a steering angle and can be driven at least indirectly The wheel  is connected at least indirectly via a suspension strut  to a body  of the motor vehicle and the suspension strut  has a fluidconducting element  having a first element which is fixed in position and a second element which is rotatable relative to the first element The first element is connected at least indirectly to the body  of the motor vehicle and the second element is connected at least indirectly to the wheel  and the two elements have throughholes   which are interconnected and are designed to supply fluid to the wheel hub drive  The invention further relates to a suspension strut  for such a wheel module and to a motor vehicle
