 DEA     A steerable twist link axle  for a motor vehicle with a twistable cross member  which is connected at the end to a longitudinal link   extending in the longitudinal direction x of the vehicle and pivotable about a steering axle   wheel carrier  mounted opposite each of the trailing arms   for receiving a wheel   a connecting rod  extending in the vehicle longitudinal direction x adjoining the wheel carrier which is coupled to an actuator  in such a way that steering movements of the wheel carrier  about the steering axis   can be carried out by actuating the actuator  is characterized in that there is a passage in the wheel carrier   for carrying out a drive shaft  for driving the wheel   is formed In addition a trailing arm   and a motor vehicle with a steerable torsion beam axle  are specified
