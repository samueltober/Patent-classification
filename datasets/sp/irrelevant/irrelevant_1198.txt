 CNU The utility model discloses a driving wheel suspension damping mechanism and an AGV and relates to the field of robots The driving wheel suspension damping mechanism comprises a fixing plate a wheel support used for installing a driving wheel and a first damping unit Wherein the first damping unit comprises a first guide shaft a first linear bearing matched with the first guide shaft and an elastic piece arranged in the circumferential direction of the first guide shaft in a sleeving mode and the two ends of the elastic piece are connected with the wheel support and the fixing plate respectively the first linear bearing is fixedly arranged on the fixing plate the first guide shaft is provided with a first end and a second end which are opposite the first end is fixedly connected with the wheel support the second end penetrates through the fixing plate and the first linear bearing in a sliding mode and the second end is provided with a limiting part for limiting the first guide shaft to be separated from the linear bearing so that the wheel support can be hung on the fixing plate Rigid friction and abrasion between the driving wheel suspension damping mechanism and the rack are avoided during longterm load running so that the AGV is high in running route precision and long in service life
