 CNA The invention discloses a vehicle maintenancefree wheel end assembly The end assembly comprises a hub and a shaft tube wherein the hub is connected to the radial outer side of the shaft tube through a bearing and an oil seal used for preventing bearing lubricating oil from leaking to a brake disc is arranged between the hub and the shaft tube an oil guide plate used for blocking a gap between two parts is arranged between the axle tube and the hub a sealed oil storage chamber is defined by the oil guide plate the oil seal the hub and the axle tube and a hole communicated with the sealed oil storage chamber is formed in the axle tube According to the end assembly lubricating oil leaked from the hub can be effectively prevented from splashing to the brake disc
