 CNA     The present invention relates to the technical field of power converters and relates to a current ripple feedforward control system that improves the stability of cascaded power converters The system includes a source converter a load converter a source converter controller and a load converter controller The converter is electrically connected to the source converter controller and the load converter respectively and the load converter is electrically connected to the source converter controller and the load converter controller respectively the source converter and the load converter include an input device and a switch device  Filter device and output device source converter controller and load converter controller include and drive circuit RS flipflop clock comparator error amplifier reference voltage adder capacitance current sampling device voltage detection device and Current sampling device The invention aims to eliminate the influence of the feedforward current ripple of the load converter in the cascaded power system on the source converter and improve the stability of the cascaded power system
