 CNA The invention relates to the technical field of floating driving devices The invention discloses a novel driving device with a floating function Vehicle frame an upper connecting plate is fixedly mounted on the left side of the frame a lower connecting plate positioned below the upper connecting plate is fixedly mounted on the left side of the frame a bearing base is fixedly installed in theupper connecting plate through a first hexagon screw a bearing is fixedly installed in the bearing base a steering shaft pipe is fixedly installed in the bearing a floating spring located on the outer side of the steering shaft pipe is fixedly installed at the bottom of the bearing base and a round nut is in threaded connection with the outer side of the steering shaft pipe The novel drivingdevice with the floating structure has the advantages of being convenient to disassemble and the like and the problems that in the prior art a driving mechanism product is complex in structure fixing mode so that the product is inconvenient to disassemble during assembling and aftersales maintenance a driving mechanism of a floating structure is not arranged and the service life of the driving device is shortened are solved
