 CNA The invention belongs to the technical field of automobiles and particularly relates to an electric control hydraulic interconnection suspension system for a formula car and a control method thereof The system comprises a data processing system an actuator a hydraulic interconnection suspension mechanical device and a formula car suspension system the data processing system is connected withthe actuator the actuator is connected with the hydraulic interconnected suspension mechanical device and a hydraulic pipeline in the actuator is fixedly connected with the formula car suspension system An electric control antipitching hydraulic interconnected suspension system is introduced based on the doublewishbone independent suspension design of the formula car and interconnection suspension system arrangement and control method design are carried out on the formula car suspension structure so that the car body pitching posture control capacity of the formula car under an emergency brakingacceleration working condition is improved the influence on the actual performance of an aerodynamic device is reduced and the formula car control stability is improved
