 CNA The invention provides an active suspension system an automated guided vehicle and a pressure adjusting method of the automated guided vehicle belongs to the technical field of automated guided vehicles and can actively adjust the slipping or suspending situation possibly caused by uneven ground of an AGV wheel train so as to improve the motion stability of an AGV The active suspension systemcomprises a wheel train mounting plate used for being connected with a driving wheel train and a chassis mounting plate used for being connected with a vehicle body chassis and a linear driving device is arranged between the wheel train mounting plate and the chassis mounting plate and used for driving so that the relative positions of the wheel train mounting plate and the chassis mounting platecan be close to or away from each other
