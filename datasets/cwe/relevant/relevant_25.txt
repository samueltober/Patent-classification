 DEB The arrangement has a wheel bearing seal  and a bearing assembly  rotatably supported at a spindle  A spacer  is arranged at the spindle between the bearing assembly and a wheel carrier  An annular body is tightly held at the spindle between the spacer and the wheel carrier where the annular body includes an end section  in a direction of a wheel hub  The end section which is made of plastic or rubber forms an annular gap with the wheel hub The wheel carrier includes an inner body made of metal and an outer body made of plastic or rubber
