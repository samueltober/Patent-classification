 WOA The present invention relates to a ball joint in particular a chassis joint with a housing and with a ball socket which has a ball bearing section for receiving a joint ball and with an antirotation safeguard which blocks a movement of the ball socket within the housing In order to propose a ball joint in particular a chassis joint with increased operational performance it is provided according to the invention that in order to form the antirotation safeguard the housing and the ball socket have in each case at least one connecting element which connecting elements have corresponding holding profiles with the result that the connecting elements form a positively locking plugin connection in the assembled state
