 CNB The invention discloses a signal acquisition circuit for a grain loss sensor The circuit includes a chargevoltage conversion circuit an absolute value circuit and a bandpass filter circuit which are connected in series in order conditions charge signals output by the grain loss sensor to voltage signals suitable for transmission and processing and filters out noise signals except grain impact signals A DSP microcontroller controls two cascaded A D converters to synchronously collect output signals of an  sensing module conditioned by a signal conditioning unit thereby providing a reliable data source for the statistics of grain loss A power supply circuit supplies power for the signal conditioning unit and the signal acquisition circuit The signal acquisition circuit is compact in structure excellent in performance and high in cost performance and provides possibility for popularization and use of the grain loss sensor
