 CNB The invention relates to a highlowvoltage directcurrent dualoutput integrated threephase PWM rectifier converter which is cascaded through three switching tubes and simultaneously multiplexed into a bridge arm of the threephase PWM rectifier converter and a switching bridge arm of a BuckBoost DCDC converter so that the integration of a bidirectional BuckBoost DCDC converter and the threephase PWM rectifier converter is realized and the highlowvoltage directcurrent dualoutput capability is realized according to the the designed converter switching devices and driving circuitsare effectively reduced power switching devices are reduced the cost and switching loss are reduced while motor performance and charging performance are not influenced and the converter can also be suitable for load application occasions of various directcurrent power supply levels The converter has an important application prospect in electric vehicles new energy power generation and microgrid systems
