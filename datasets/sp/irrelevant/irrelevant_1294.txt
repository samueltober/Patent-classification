 CNA The invention discloses an air bag supporting system in a passenger car chassis The air bag supporting comprises a first reinforcing plate a second reinforcing plate an air bag mounting panel and asupport main body The first reinforcing plate the second reinforcing plate and the air bag mounting panel are welded to the support main body An outer side of a bottom edge of the first reinforcing plate and the edge of the air bag mounting panel are aligned and then welded together and an inner side of the first reinforcing plate is welded to an upper end surface of the support main body the second reinforcing plate is welded to the inner surface of the support main body an air bag installation hole is arranged in the center of the air bag mounting panel an air bag installation and maintenance hole is arranged in the support main body and wire passing holes are arranged in the vertical surfaces of the two sides A front air suspension air bag support is reinforced a bearing capacity of the air bag support in a normal direction of the air bag panel is improved a stress of the air bag mounting panel is dispersed and problems of bending deformation and cracking of the air bagmounting panel are solved
