 CNA The invention relates to a device for the horizontal adjustment of a vehicle body comprising an axially fixed spindle  that is antirelative rotation and a rotationally drivable nut  that can be moved axially along the spindle  said nut  being axially displaceable between a first axial end point and a second axial end point wherein the nut  is at least indirectly supported radially with respect to the spindle  on the end face a b thereof by means of a respective radial needle bearing a b in order to carry out an ascending or descending process of the vehicle body
