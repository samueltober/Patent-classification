 USA An air suspension strut for a motor vehicle comprises an air spring having an air spring cover and a rolling piston A shock damper is integrated with the air spring and has a damper tube and a piston rod receivable within the damper tube A rolling bellows is secured between the spring cover and the rolling piston thereby delimiting a variable volume pressure chamber filled with compressed air The rolling bellows rolls on the rolling piston with a rolling fold being formed A damper bearing is accommodated in a bearing socket of the air spring cover and connected to the piston rod The damper bearing comprises a supporting piece an elastomer body and a sleeve A closure element inserted in the bearing socket above the damper bearing An elastic element arranged between the sleeve and the closure element
