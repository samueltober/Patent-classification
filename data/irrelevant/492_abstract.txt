The invention relates to a locomotive power supply system in particular to a locomotive power supply system and a control method thereof The problems that an existing electric locomotive is lack ofredundancy in power supply and poor in power supply quality and reliability are solved The locomotive power supply system comprises a transformer a train power supply system a diesel generator power supply system and a storage battery Through the multilayer redundancy main circuit topology design and multilayer redundancy control switching the reliability and flexibility of the train powersupply system are greatly improved and the riding comfort and convenience of passengers are improved the system has the capability of providing longtime and shorttime power supply for the train power supply system and improves the flexibility and safety of locomotive power supply
