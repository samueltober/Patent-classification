 CNA The invention discloses a torsion bar suspension mechanism based on Mecanum wheels The torsion bar suspension mechanism based on the Mecanum wheels comprises a vehicle frame suspension units and wheel mechanisms The suspension units are arranged at intervals at the bottom of the vehicle frame each suspension unit comprises a torsion bar and two wheel connecting pieces wherein each torsion baris axially and fixedly arranged at the bottom of the vehicle frame through a shaft support assembly and the two wheel connecting pieces of each suspension unit are symmetrically and fixedly arrangedat both ends of the corresponding torsion bar The wheel mechanisms are arranged on both sides of the vehicle frame in pairs and are fixedly connected with wheel connecting pieces of the corresponding suspension units According to the torsion bar suspension mechanism based on the Mecanum wheels the fluctuation of the ground can be passively adapted so that the four Mecanum wheels simultaneously touch the ground the accuracy of omnidirectional movement is ensured the obstacle crossing performance is excellent the structure is simple and compact the control is simple and reliable economical efficiency and practicality are high the cost is low and the weight is light
