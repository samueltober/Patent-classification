 CNA     A modular power assembly and a wind power converter are provided The modular power assembly includes a capacitor pool module including a first laminated busbar and a plurality of capacitors electrically connected to the first laminated busbar a plurality of power modules Each power module includes at least two IGBT units connected in parallel and a second laminated busbar electrically connected to the at least two IGBT units wherein the second laminated busbars in each power module are electrically connected respectively To the first laminated busbar integrated control module respectively connects each power module and sends a control command to each power module to control the turnon and turnoff of the IGBT unit in each power module Based on the modular power assembly and the wind power converter of the exemplary embodiment of the present invention by adopting the modular design the interface design can be unified the quantity of materials can be reduced and the cost can be reduced
