 CNA The invention provides an allterrain vehicle A right rear suspension of the allterrain vehicle comprises a rocker arm an axle support a first pull rod a second pull rod and a third pull rod A connecting part is arranged at the front end of the rocker arm and connected with a frame the inner end of the first pull rod is connected to the frame and the outer end of the first pull rod is connected to a first hinge part the second pull rod is located above the first pull rod the inner end of the second pull rod is connected to the frame and the outer end of the second pull rod is connected to a second hinge part and the third pull rod is located in front of the first pull rod and the second pull rod the inner end of the third pull rod is connected to the frame and the outer end of the third pull rod is connected to the rocker arm The allterrain vehicle always keeps wheel positioning meeting the vehicle running requirement in a large wheel stroke so that the allterrain vehicle has a large ground clearance strong obstacle passing capacity and the capacity of buffering and absorbing impact vibration from a rugged ground
