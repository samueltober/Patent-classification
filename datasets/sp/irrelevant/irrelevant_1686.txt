 CNA The invention provides an obstacle crossing robot with an adjustable gravity center The obstacle crossing robot comprises a vehicle frame obstacle crossing assemblies a gravity center adjusting assembly a walking assembly and a vehiclemounted control system The walking assembly is arranged on the lower portion of the vehicle frame and the gravity center adjusting assembly and the vehiclemounted control system are fixedly arranged in the vehicle frame The obstacle crossing assemblies are arranged at the front end and the rear end of the vehicle frame and the obstacle crossing assemblies the gravity center adjusting assembly and the walking assembly are all in communication connection with the vehiclemounted control system According to the obstacle crossing robot with an adjustable gravity center the gravity center adjusting assembly is arranged to adjust the gravity center position of the robot in real time in the walking process and through cooperation of the walking assembly the passing performance and the bearing performance are effectively improved and by arranging the obstacle crossing assemblies the obstacle crossing performance of the robot is improved
