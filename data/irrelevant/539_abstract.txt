The invention discloses a permanent magnet reluctance cascade generator control system which comprises a PMG and an SRG which are coaxially connected in series and further comprises a rectifying circuit which selects a threephase uncontrollable rectifying circuit is connected with the PMG and is used for converting PMG output into direct current and outputting the direct current to a power converter the power converter adopts a separately excited asymmetric halfbridge structure is connected with the SRG is used for controlling the SRG to work and can further output electric energy atthe same time the DSP controller is connected with the rectifying circuit and the power converter and used for collecting corresponding voltage and current signals and outputting driving signals through the driving circuit to control the power converter to work the overall reliability of the system is improved the safety coefficient is increased the control mode is simple and operation is easy
