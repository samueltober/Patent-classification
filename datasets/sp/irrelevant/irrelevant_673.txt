 CNU The utility model discloses a suspension system which is characterized by comprising two torsion bar springs  one end of each torsion bar spring  is connected with a longitudinal arm  with awheel bracket the other end of each torsion bar spring  is connected with a frame and the two torsion bar springs  are arranged in parallel By the adoption of the arrangement the torsion rod spring is used for connecting the frame and the wheels and the torsion rod spring bears the vertical load namely the load of the vehicle body to the wheels Due to the fact that positioning and guiding are conducted only through the torsion rod springs and the wheels the structure can be simplified the cost can be reduced and meanwhile larger riding space can be provided for an automobileThe utility model further discloses an automobile with the suspension system
