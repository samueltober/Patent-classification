 DEA     The invention relates to a wheel bearing  for a motor vehicle  comprising at least the following components An inner ring  preferably made in two parts an outer ring  A plurality of rolling elements  which form a first row of bearings  and a second row of bearings  each row of bearings  preferably being guided in a bearing cage   The outer ring  forms a wheel flange  to which a vehicle wheel  can be detachably fastened preferably a wheel rim  can be screwed on directly The wheel bearing  is primarily characterized in that the wheel bearing  comprises a near field identification transponder  preferably an RFID tagWith the wheel bearing proposed here the marking of the wheel bearing can be read quickly and with little effort without the need for disassembly
