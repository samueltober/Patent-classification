 CNA The invention discloses a shock absorber and chassis system connecting structure The connecting structure comprises a piston rod assembly a mounting disc assembly and a rubber metal sleeve The piston rod assembly is installed in the piston cylinder the open end face of the piston cylinder is fixedly connected with the mountingdisc assembly the end away from the piston cylinder of the mountingdisc assembly is connected with a vehicle chassis and the rubber metal sleeve is installed in the mountingdisc assembly and arranged at one end of the piston rod assembly in a sleeving mode The mountingdisc assembly is in rigid connection with the vehicle body and the rubber metal sleeve can generate elastic deformation when bearing lateral force to achieve the flexible connection effect
