The invention discloses a universal PCB design motion control system for jig production and belongs to the technical field of jig production The universal PCB design motion control system for jig production comprises an STM minimum control system a power supply generation circuit a channel input circuit a channel output circuit an RS communication circuit a CAN communication circuit an RS communication circuit an SPIIIC cascade circuit and a motor control circuit and can achieve the poweron control of an electromagnetic valve so as to achieve the precise control of ajig cylinder and enable a motor driver to control the movement of a motor Input detection of a jig button a magnetic switch an optical grating an ion fan and the like can be achieved communication with an upper computer software network port a serial port and other interface modes can also be achieved CAN industrial bus expansion is achieved and farreaching significance is achieved for shortening a jig production period improving market competitiveness and reducing operation cost
