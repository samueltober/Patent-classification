 CNU The utility model discloses a rotary damping mechanism of an inspection robot The rotary damping mechanism comprises a supporting plate a damper and a swing arm which are connected with one anotherto form a triangular structure a rotating structure is arranged on the supporting plate one end of the swing arm is connected with the supporting plate through a first rotating shaft and the otherend of the swing arm is connected with a shock absorber and then connected with a second rotating shaft through a shaft clamping device The same sides of the first rotating shaft and the second rotating shaft are respectively connected with a synchronous wheel and the two synchronous wheels are connected through a synchronous belt a synchronous wheel center shaft and a fork arm swing shaft arecoaxially designed synchronous belt loosening caused by fork arm swing is eliminated and it is guaranteed that the damping function is complete and not affected while transmission is achieved
