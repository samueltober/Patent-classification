 CNU The utility model relates to a rear hub of an automobile The rear hub comprises a cylindrical body and a flange arranged on the outer circumference of the body the flange is provided with a plurality of through lightening grooves and a plurality of through holes and the lightening grooves are located between every two adjacent through holes And the flange is also provided with a stress part and the stress part is positioned between two adjacent through holes The number of the stress parts is two and the two stress parts are symmetrically arranged relative to the center of the flange And a penetrating top hole is formed in the stress part The automobile rear hub is integrally cast and machined and has the advantages of being light in weight good in strength low in manufacturingcost convenient to disassemble and assemble and the like
