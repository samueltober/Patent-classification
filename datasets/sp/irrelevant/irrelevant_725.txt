 DEA     According to the invention is a motor vehicle axle for a twolane vehicle with a stabilizer the respective end of each wheel side by means of a pendulum support in a fastening point on a wishbone on a ball joint on its support joint on a wheel carrier with its pivot bearing on an axle carrier via a connecting bolt is supported characterized that the connecting bolt is connected to the wishbone via a ball joint and to the pendulum support via a swivel joint
