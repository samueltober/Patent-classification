 CNU The utility model provides a suspension driving device and a mobile robot with the same The suspension driving device comprises a fixed mounting frame body a floating mounting plate a first elasticpiece a rotary driver and a driving wheel the floating mounting plate is vertically and movably arranged on the fixed mounting frame body the first elastic piece is vertically connected between the floating mounting plate and the fixed mounting frame body in an abutting mode and the first elastic piece constantly drives the floating mounting plate to vertically move downwards wherein the rotary driver is fixed on the floating mounting plate the driving wheel is pivoted on the floating mounting plate around a horizontal axis and the driving wheel is in transmission connection with an output shaft of the rotary driver The device has the advantages of being simple in structure small in size capable of guaranteeing good contact between the driving wheel and the ground capable of avoiding slipping stable in operation and capable of guaranteeing working precision
