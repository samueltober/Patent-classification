 KRA Suspension thrust bearing arrangement with seal A suspension thrust bearing arrangement comprises a lower support cap   an upper bearing cap  and at least one bearing  arranged between the caps The upper bearing cap  comprises an outer skirt c which radially surrounds the lower support cap   The suspension thrust bearing arrangement further comprises an outer seal  located at least partially radially between the bearing  and the outer skirt c of the upper bearing cap The outer seal  extends towards one of the lower support cap  and the upper bearing cap  and defines at least one circular outer chamber  facing at least the cap   It has sealing lips b c and at least one lubricant  is arranged in the outer chamber
