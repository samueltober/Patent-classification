 CNU     The utility model provides a mobile robot and its walking device The walking device includes a walking wheel a connecting body  and a shock absorber  The connecting body is used to connect the walking wheel and the chassis of the mobile robot The first position is hinged with the chassis the second position of the connecting body is hinged with the lower end of the shock absorber and the upper end of the shock absorber is hinged with the chassis The walking device provided by the utility model can make the mobile robot have good stability when walking
