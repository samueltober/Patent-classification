A method for fault handling in direct current DC power transmission between a first alternating current AC system and a second AC system is provided The method includes detecting an occurrence ofa phasetoground fault on an AC side of a voltagesource converter VSC connected to the second AC system and operated as an inverter The method further includes upon detecting the occurrence ofthe phasetoground fault firing a bypass valve pair of a currentsource converter CSC connected to the first AC system and operated as a rectifier A power transmission system is also provided
