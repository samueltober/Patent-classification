 CNU The utility model provides a balance shaft support which comprises a support body a longitudinal beam installation portion is arranged on the upper portion of the support body a thrust rod installation portion is arranged on the lower portion of the support body a hollow bearing hub installation portion is arranged in the middle of one side of the support body in the vertical direction and a cross beam installation portion is arranged on the other side of the support body One end of the connecting arm is provided with an end cover with a center hole the other end of the connecting arm isbent downwards to be provided with a mounting support and the mounting support is provided with a mounting hole A bolt installation hole is formed in the support body in the axis direction of the bearing hub installation part a plurality of threaded holes are formed in the thrust rod installation part the end cover is fixed to the support body through bolts and the other end of the connecting arm is fixed into the threaded holes of the thrust rod installation part Through the arrangement of the connecting arm one part of stress borne by the balance shaft assembly in the working processis located at the joint of the bearing hub installation part and the bearing hub the other part of stress is dispersed to the bottom of the support through the connecting arm the stress bearing capacity of the balance shaft assembly is improved and maintenance is convenient
