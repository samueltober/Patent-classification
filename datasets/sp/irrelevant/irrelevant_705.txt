 USB A damping system for damping rotary movements of a tailing arm The system includes a chassis a main shaft and a rotary damping mechanism The rotary damping mechanism includes a first externallythreaded gear attached fixedly to the main shaft a guide rail attached fixedly to the chassis a linear shock absorber an internallythreaded gear associated with the first externallythreaded gear and a first actuator configured to decouple the first externallythreaded gear from the linear shock absorber and couple the first externallythreaded gear with the linear shock absorber
