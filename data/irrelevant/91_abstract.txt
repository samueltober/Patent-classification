A power module for a converter in particular for a multilevel converter The power module includes a link circuit capacitor two connection terminals at least one halfbridge connected in parallel with the link circuit capacitor and having two semiconductor switches and for each halfbridge a bypass diode connected in parallel with a first semiconductor switch of the halfbridge and a loadrelief circuit group connected in parallel with the first semiconductor switch The loadrelief circuit group has a loadrelief thyristor and a loadrelief diode connected in series with the loadrelief thyristor
