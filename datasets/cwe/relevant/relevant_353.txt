 KRA According to an embodiment of the present invention a sealing device comprises inner and outer device units rotating relative to each other The inner device unit includes an inner frame fixed to an inner ring unit and an inner sealing member having an outer circumferential sealing unit for covering an outer radial end surface of the inner frame The outer device unit includes an outer frame fixed to an outer wheel unit and having an inner radial end surface which radially faces the outer circumferential sealing unit The inner sealing member is spaced apart from the outer device unit The inner sealing member includes a plurality of protrusions integrally formed with the outer circumferential sealing unit protruding in an outer radial direction from the outer circumferential sealing unit and arranged to be spaced apart from each other in a circumferential direction According to the present invention infiltration of foreign matters into a space where outer and inner wheel units are formed is prevented
