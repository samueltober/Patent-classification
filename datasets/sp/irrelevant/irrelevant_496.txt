 CNA The invention relates to the technical field of automobiles in particular to a Ushaped antiroll bar for a Formula Society of Automotive Engineers racecar The Ushaped antiroll bar comprises an antiroll bar torsion spring a first pull rod a second pull rod a first connection piece and a second connection piece wherein one end of the antiroll bar torsion spring is connected with the firstpull rod through the first connection piece and the other end of the antiroll bar torsion spring is connected with the second pull rod through a second connection piece According to the Ushaped antiroll bar the Ushaped antiroll bar torsion spring is simple in structure and light in weight and has certain rigidity so that rolling of the racecar can be resisted and the racecar is enabledto have better driveability on an uneven road surface
