 KRA Provided is a wheel assembly in which a motor assembly driving a wheel is detachably mounted outside the wheel According to the present invention the wheel assembly comprises a wheel with a tire a first gear provided on the wheel and configured to rotate the wheel around an axle connecting the wheel and a vehicle a motor assembly detachably mounted outside the wheel to rotate the wheel and a bearing unit rotatably connecting the wheel and the motor assembly and having the axle The motor assembly includes an inner cage detachably fixed to the axle an air cleaning unit mounted on the inner cage and a motor mounted on the inner cage and having a second gear rotating the first gear
