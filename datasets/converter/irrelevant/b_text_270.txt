 CNB The invention discloses a flexible directcurrent distribution network compact control protection host The host is characterized in that communication networks between plugins in protection controlhosts are designed a control LAN between the protection control hosts is designed and communication networks between the protection control hosts and equipment level controllers such as a flexible directcurrent conversion unit a directcurrent transformer unit an alternatingcurrent and directcurrent load a windsolar storage distributed power supply and the like are designed The flexibledirectcurrent distribution network compact control protection host is based on a realtime communication system framework and adopts an open modular and compact design principle the compact designof the structure and function of a protection and control device is realized the structure of control protection hardware is greatly simplified the system reliability is improved and the cost is reduced By means of a cooperative coordination method of control and protection functions rapid fault positioning fault removal and rapid postfault recovery capabilities of a directcurrent distribution network system are improved
