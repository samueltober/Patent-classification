 CNU The utility model discloses a wheel edge oil discharge structure for a drive axle The wheel edge oil discharge structure comprises a hub an oil baffle plate and an oil baffle cover an oil guide groove is formed in the hub and communicated with an oil guide hole and the oil guide hole is communicated with the outside The oil baffle plate is annular and the diameter of the inner ring of the oil baffle plate is smaller than that of the bearing oil drainage channel the outer diameter of the oil blocking cover is larger than the diameter of the plane where the oil guide hole and the oil drainage groove in the hub are located By means of the structure leaked oil generated by failure of the wheel edge oil seal is effectively guided out and the problems of brake failure wheel edge ignition and the like caused by wheel edge oil leakage are solved
