 CNA The invention relates to a distributed electric drive allterrain vehicle The vehicle comprises a vehicle body wheels used for driving the vehicle body to advance a power supply assembly used for driving the wheels to rotate and a control assembly The wheels are evenly distributed below the vehicle body each wheel comprises a hub motor and a tire arranged outside the hub motor in a sleeving mode a stator part of each hub motor is connected with the vehicle body a rotor part of each hub motor is connected with the corresponding tire and the hub motors are independent of one another andelectrically connected with the power supply assembly through the control assembly According to the arrangement a distributed hub motor driving system is adopted no gearbox no transfer case no transmission shaft and no half shaft are adopted that is no mechanical transmission power and torque are adopted a driving structure is simple and stable the number of parts is small and the damageis not likely to happen the power supply assembly supplies electric energy to the hub motors in all the wheels through the control assembly the hub motors directly drive the wheels to rotate and drive the vehicle body to run and running in various complex environments is facilitated
