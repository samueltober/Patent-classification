 CNA Certain aspects of the present disclosure generally relate to methods and apparatus for switching between modes of a multimode power supply circuit One example power supply circuit generally includes a switchedmode power supply SMPS circuit a voltage regulator circuit having an input coupled to an output of the SMPS circuit the voltage regulator circuit being configured to be selectively enabled a first voltage divider selectively coupled to the output of the SMPS circuit and selectively coupled to an output of the voltage regulator circuit and a second voltage divider coupled to the output of the voltage regulator circuit
