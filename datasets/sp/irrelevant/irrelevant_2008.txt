 CNA     The present invention provides an active suspension control method control device system and vehicle for prejudge avoidance wherein the method includes acquiring the type of the target vehicle detected by the intelligent driving assistance system the longitudinal distance between the target vehicle and the vehicle and Lateral distance when the absolute value of the first longitudinal distance of the first target vehicle is less than the corresponding first distance limit enter the preset preactive suspension control mode when the vehicle is working in preactive suspension control mode Next obtain speed information of the host vehicle and the first target vehicle adjust the damping force of the active suspension system according to at least one of the speed information the type of the first target vehicle and the first longitudinal distance That is when the vehicles are meeting by entering the preactive suspension control mode in advance the vehicle is always in the best shock absorption state ensuring the comfort of the occupants in the vehicle and improving the control stability of the vehicle
