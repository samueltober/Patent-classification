 KRA According to the present invention a vehicle includes a plurality of wheels a motor a battery and an air purification part prepared in the internal space of at least one of the plurality of wheels a wheel speed detection part detecting the speed of each of the plurality of wheels an acceleration detection part detecting acceleration and a control part determining a driving force application moment or a brake force application moment based on the detected acceleration and the detected speed of the plurality of wheels and then applying a driving force to at least one wheel by controlling the operation of the motor if the driving force application moment is determined whereas applying a brake force to at least one wheel if the brake force application moment is determined controlling the regenerative braking operation of the motor to perform a power generation operation for charging the battery and controlling the operation of the air purification part to perform an air purification operation with the electric power of the battery Therefore the vehicle is capable of automatically purifying air on a road through the rotation of its wheels
