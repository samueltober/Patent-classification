 WOA The invention relates to a vehicle component  for an offroad utility vehicle  comprising a chassis  and a power train  that can be accommodated on the chassis  The power train  comprises two rigid axle units  each having a torque tube frame  The torque tube frames  are connected to one another in articulated manner by means of a roll stabiliser device  fastened to the chassis  The roll stabiliser device  is assigned a damper device  for damping a roll movement of the vehicle  The damper element  comprises a controllable damper  The damper device  comprises a control device  which is suitable and configured for the adjustment of a damper parameter of the controllable damper  according to a characteristic variable that is stored andor can be detected by sensor
