 CNU The utility model discloses a wheel hub with high matching degree The lubricating oil has a good selflubricating effect according to the technical scheme the hub comprises a hub ring and a mounting disc a spoke is connected between the hub ring and the mounting disc a connecting column is arranged on the mounting disc shaft hole is formed in connecting column a selflubricating shaft sleeve and bearings arranged at the two ends of the selflubricating shaft sleeve are arranged in the shaft hole an oil storage groove is formed in the peripheral wall of the selflubricating shaft sleeve a plurality of micropores are formed in the selflubricating shaft sleeve the bearing comprises an inner ring an outer ring and a roller arranged between the inner ring and the outer ring and a sealing ring arranged between the inner ring and the outer ring is arranged on the side deviating from the selflubricating shaft sleeve of the bearing
