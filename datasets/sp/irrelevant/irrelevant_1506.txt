 CNA The invention discloses a vulcanizing device for producing and manufacturing transverse stabilizer bar assemblies The vulcanizing device comprises a rack a chain type conveying mechanism arranged onthe rack and a first placing mechanism a second placing mechanism a discharging welding mechanism a glue injection mechanism a heating heat preservation vulcanizing area and an accelerated cooling area which are sequentially arranged at the top of the rack in the conveying direction the chain type conveying mechanism is used for conveying the transverse stabilizer bar assemblies and multiple pairs of positioning parts are arranged on two bush fixing parts corresponding to the transverse stabilizer bar assemblies of the chain type conveying mechanism the first placing mechanism is used for sequentially grabbing lower brackets and lower rolling assemblies of the transverse stabilizer bar assemblies and correspondingly placing the lower brackets and the lower rolling assemblies onthe positioning parts of the chain type conveying mechanism and the second placing mechanism is used for grabbing upper rolling assemblies of the transverse stabilizer bar assemblies to be buckled with the lower rolling assemblies located on the positioning parts According to the vulcanizing device a chain type linear layout is formed so that the transverse stabilizer bar assemblies are mechanically produced and manufactured the production efficiency is high and the largebatch production of the transverse stabilizer bar assemblies is realized
