 CNA The invention discloses a driving suspension device of an automatic driving test vehicle The driving suspension device of the automatic driving test vehicle comprises a support a motor a swing arma shock absorber a driving wheel a main synchronous belt wheel a driven synchronous belt wheel and a synchronous belt A first through groove and a second through groove are vertically formed in the support in parallel the top end of the swing arm is located in the first through groove and hinged to the support through a first rotating shaft a shaft extension part of the motor horizontally penetrates through the side wall of the support and stretches into the second through groove the main synchronous belt wheel is arranged on the shaft extension part in a sleeving mode the shaft extension part of the motor is coaxial with the first rotating shaft the driving wheel is hinged to the bottom end of the swing arm through a second rotating shaft the driven synchronous belt wheel is arranged on the second rotating shaft in a sleeving mode the main synchronous belt wheel and the driven synchronous belt wheel are connected through the synchronous belt a first through hole is formedin the middle of the swing arm the bottom end of the shock absorber stretches into the first through hole and is fixedly connected with the lower portion of the inner wall of the first through holeand the top end of the shock absorber stretches into the first through groove and is hinged to the support trough a third rotating shaft The driving suspension device is designed into onegrade synchronous belt transmission the transmission efficiency is high and the synchronous wheel is not likely to loose and disengage
