 CNA The invention discloses an automobile damping continuously adjustable semiactive suspension electric control device and a method thereof which are used for controlling a shock absorber electromagnetic valve arranged at the position of an automobile body suspension The control device comprises an acquisition unit used for acquiring an automobile body vertical height signal and an automobile body vertical acceleration signal at the position of the suspension a control unit which is used for providing a control signal for the shock absorber electromagnetic valve according to the automobile body vertical height signal and the automobile body vertical acceleration signal of the position of the suspension an electromagnetic valve driving circuit through which the control unit controls the shock absorber electromagnetic valve and a plurality of current feedback circuits which are connected with the control unit through an AD conversion unit feed back the current of the shock absorber electromagnetic valve to the control unit and carry out error correction on the driving current of the shock absorber electromagnetic valve driving circuit According to the invention the technical problems between the riding comfort and the control stability of the automobile are solved
