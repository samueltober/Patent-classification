 CNA The invention discloses an independent steering driving system and a delivery robot The independent steering driving system comprises a hub motor component a shock absorbing component and a steeringcomponent wherein the hub motor component comprises a rotating shaft the rotating shaft is mounted at the lower end of the shock absorbing component the steering component is arranged above the shock absorbing component the steering component is provided with a mounting position connected with a chassis the steering component comprises a housing a steering driving piece a steering shaft asteering transmission component and a control board the steering driving piece and the control board are electrically connected with each other and are both arranged in the housing the steering driving piece is in transmission with the steering shaft through a steering transmission component and the steering shaft penetrates out from the interior of the housing and is fixedly connected with the shock absorbing component Therefore the independent steering driving system can realize integration of a driving function and a steering function and moreover the reliability of the whole structure is good the independent steering driving system can be directly mounted on the chassis and therefore the universality of the independent steering driving system can be enhanced
