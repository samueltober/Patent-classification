 CNU The utility model discloses a shock absorption type wheel assembly and a shuttle vehicle The shock absorption type wheel assembly comprises a wheel axle a mounting piece a driving motor and a speedreducer The wheel axle is connected with the speed reducer through the telescopic universal joint coupler the damping device is connected with the wheel and when the wheel vibrates the deformation of the telescopic universal joint coupler can prevent the vibration from being transmitted to the speed reducer and the driving motor so as to protect the speed reducer and the driving motor The shock absorption type wheel assembly is installed on the shuttle vehicle
