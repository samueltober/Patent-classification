 DEA     The invention relates to a suspension system of a motor vehicle designed as an active suspension system with a variable characteristic via which one a front axle and a rear axle enclosing an unsprung mass  forming the chassis is coupled to a a sprung mass  forming motor vehicle body the suspension system  comprising two springs arranged in parallel one main spring  and one secondary spring  as well as two dampers inserted in series a main damper  and a secondary damper  wherein a setting state of the secondary damper  by an adjusting device  can be fixed
