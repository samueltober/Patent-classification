 KRA The present invention relates to a stabilizer bar bush for a vehicle to prevent noise and improve durability in which a bearing member  is disposed between a core member  and a rubber bush  and a low friction member  is provided on an inner surface in contact with the core member  in the bearing member  Moreover a lubrication groove  for filling a lubricant is formed at a portion in contact with the bearing member  in the core member  and a body portion  of the core member  is formed in a bulge shape in which an outer circumferential surface thereof protrudes to be bludged outwards
