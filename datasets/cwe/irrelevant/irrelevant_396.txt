 KRA Disclosed is an inwheel driving device The inwheel driving device of the present invention includes a rim member on which a tire is installed along an outer periphery spokes connected to the rim member and rotated with the rim member a motor unit that is positioned inside a mounting space formed by the rim member and the spokes and generates rotational power by supplying power to rotate the spokes a knuckle unit connected to the motor unit and supporting a suspension unit and a hub unit in which one side is fixed to the rotation center axis of the spokes and the other side is connected to the knuckle unit to rotationally support the spokes
