 CNU     The utility model discloses a complete machine structure of a large vantype power station including main equipment of a generator set and is characterized in that the complete machine structure of the power station also includes at least three box units assembled in a modular manner one of which is a box body The units are used for air intake and one of the cabinet units is used for exhaust air The main equipment of the generator set is installed in the cabinet unit between the air intake and exhaust and adjacent cabinet units are assembled into a whole Compared with the prior art the utility model has the advantages of adopting a modular design facilitating production transportation and installation and also reducing risks in transportation
