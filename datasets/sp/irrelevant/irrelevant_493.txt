 CNU The utility model provides a fourrotation fourdrive mobile robot which comprises a suspension mechanism a steering mechanism a driving mechanism and a mobile robot vehicle body The suspension mechanism comprises wheels a flange shaft a steering knuckle a spherical pair an updown swing arm and a damping device The steering mechanism comprises a linear motion module a steering transmission rod and a spherical pair the driving mechanism comprises a motor a speed reducer and a telescopic universal joint The independent suspension mechanism is matched with the steering mechanism sothat the driving torque required by steering is reduced the steering efficiency of differential steering is improved and the turning flexibility is improved The terrain adaptability of the robot isenhanced the turning efficiency is improved and the robot can be widely applied to mobile robots under field road conditions
