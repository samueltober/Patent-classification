 CNA The invention relates to a gearing arrangement  for a device  for the level adjustment of a vehicle structure comprising a gear wheel  that is rotatably mounted on a stationary housing bolt via a bearing element  consisting of an inner ring  an outer ring  and rolling bodies  arranged spatially between same wherein a sheet metal ring  having multiple at least partially circumferentially arranged expansion sections  is arranged on the housing bolt  wherein the expansion sections  are provided to axially support the inner ring  in relation to the housing bolt  and to prevent an axial movement of the inner ring
