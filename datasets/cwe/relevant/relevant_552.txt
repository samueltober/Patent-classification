 CNU The utility model discloses a lightweight hub bearing which comprises a barrelshaped part and a flange plate arranged on the outer wall of the barrelshaped part a plurality of bolt holes are formed in the flange plate bolts are arranged in the bolt holes in a penetrating mode a plurality of grooves recessed inwards are formed in the outer edge of the flange plate and the grooves are located between the adjacent bolt holes Compared with an existing conventional flange plate the outer edge of the flange plate is in a petal shape due to the existence of the groove under the condition that conventional use is met the weight is lighter and the requirement for light weight of a hub bearing is met
