 CNA The invention provides an electric tractor fulllife electromagnetic compatibility testing system and method The test method comprises an EMC test of a sample piece and an EMC test of a tested piecethe EMC test of the sample piece needs initial EMC data the sample piece works for two years under natural stress and the EMC test is carried out at different time nodes of the two years the method comprises the following steps carrying out the EMC test on the tested piece before a life test then carrying out a doublestress accelerated life test completing the life test then carrying outthe EMC test establishing a degradation model carrying out EMC data comparison with the sample piece under natural stress and verifying the reliability of the model seen in the life test returningthe stress for doublestress accelerated life test adjustment until verification is completed establishing an electric tractor fulllife EMC test method in a laboratory environment and finally implementing the electric tractor fulllife EMC test method
