 CNU The utility model discloses a logistics wheel The logistics wheel comprises a hub a bearing is axially arranged in the center of the hub a shaft penetrates through the middle of the bearing an annular groove is formed in the middle of the outer circumferential face of the hub annular protruding parts are arranged on the two sides of the annular groove outer rings are arranged on the protruding parts in a sleeving mode inserts matched with the annular groove in shape are embedded in the annular groove and the inserts surround the annular groove by a circle The logistics wheel disclosedby the utility model has good corrosion resistance high temperature resistance and high compressive strength
