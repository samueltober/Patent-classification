 CNA     The present invention provides a chassis height automatic adjustment device based on the running state of the vehicle which includes wheels the wheels are fixedly connected to the wheel shaft the wheel shaft is fixedly connected to a first universal joint the first universal joint is fixedly connected to a drive shaft and the wheel shaft rotates Cooperating with the lifting power source the lifting power source is fixedly arranged on the chassis The lifting power source and the sliding mechanism are set to cooperate with the wheel shafts to realize the automatic lifting adjustment function of the automatic vehicle chassis and reduce the wear of the vehicle chassis caused by high obstacles on the road surface a buffer spring is added to the sliding mechanism to make the vehicle The antivibration effect of the vehicle is better the trigger lifting methods such as mechanical triggering and vehicle speed triggering are designed to meet the multiple needs of vehicle driving stability
