 CNA The invention discloses a mining dump truck hub The mining dump truck hub comprises a hub body a hub bearing a half axle sleeve an adjusting nut an outer key plate and a bolt with a hole The hubbearing is installed on the half axle sleeve the hub is installed on the hub bearing the hub bearing is located between the half axle sleeve and the hub and the adjusting nut is installed on an external thread on the half axle sleeve through an internal thread and attached to the outer end face of the hub bearing The outer key plate is located on the outer side of the adjusting nut the lowerportion of the outer key plate is inserted into the outer spline of the half axle sleeve and the upper portion of the outer key plate is fixed to the adjusting nut through the bolt with the hole The antiloosening effect on the adjusting nut is achieved through toothshaped meshing of the half axle sleeve spline and the outer key plate and the mining dump truck hub has the advantages of beingsafe reliable convenient to adjust and simple in structure
