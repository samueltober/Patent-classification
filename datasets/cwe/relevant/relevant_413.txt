 CNA The invention provides a muddywaterproof oil seal for a wheel hub The oil seal solves the problem that in the prior art a common combined type oil seal cannot meet the waterproof requirement Themuddywaterproof oil seal for the wheel hub comprises a plurality of spliced frameworks a containing cavity is formed among the frameworks an inner rubber body is arranged in the containing cavitya plurality of independent chambers are defined between the inner rubber body and the inner surface of the containing cavity sealing oil is stored in each chamber and outer rubber bodies for installation are arranged on the outer surfaces of the frameworks The muddywaterproof oil seal for the wheel hub has the advantages of being simple in structure convenient to use capable of effectivelyachieving the isolating function on muddy water and good in waterproof effect and practicability
