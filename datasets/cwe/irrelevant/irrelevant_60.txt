 CNU The utility model provides an automobile hub flange plate structure and a display device The automobile hub flange plate structure comprises a flange plate body A plurality of slotted holes are formed in the end face of the flange plate body and the flange plate is installed on the outer sides of different types of automobile hubs through combination of the different slotted holes The flange plate body is provided with a center hole and the axis of the center hole coincides with the center line of a hub shaft The flange plate structure is adaptive to automobile hubs with different numbers of screw holes and is high in practicability and wide in application range
