 DEA The invention relates to a wheel bearing device  and a vehicle equipped therewith The wheel bearing device  comprises a first wheel bearing part  and a second wheel bearing part  which are rotatably mounted relative to one another via a bearing  The bearing  is sealed at least in some areas from the surroundings of the wheel bearing device  by means of a bearing seal  arranged between the two wheel bearing parts   The bearing seal  has a sealing plate  on one side with a sealing lip  arranged thereon On the other hand the bearing seal  has a stop plate  for the sealing lip  the stop plate  being designed as a ferromagnet and being held magnetically on one of the two wheel bearing parts
