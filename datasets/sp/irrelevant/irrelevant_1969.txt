 CNU     The utility model discloses a rear longitudinal beam assembly for fixing a torsion beam belonging to the technical field of automobile assembly comprising a beam two supporting beams two shockabsorbing devices and two shockabsorbing devices The beam is horizontal The two support beams are symmetrically arranged on both ends of the cross beam the two shockabsorbing devices are symmetrically arranged on the two support beams and the two shockabsorbing devices are respectively arranged on the two shockabsorbing devices One end of each support beam is provided with a first installation device and the other end of each support beam is provided with a second installation device The utility model can adjust the installation position of the torsion beam according to actual installation requirements through the cooperation of the first installation device with the second installation device and has strong practicability
