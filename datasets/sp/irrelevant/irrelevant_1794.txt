 CNA The invention discloses a rear axle structure of an electric cleaning vehicle The rear axle structure comprises a shell a cushioning mechanism a main shaft and a motor the shell is a hollow cylinder with two open ends a through groove communicated with the interior is formed in the upper end of the middle of the outer ring face of the shell a protective shell is fixedly installed in the middle of the outer ring face of the shell the interior of the protective shell is hollow and a partition plate is fixedly installed in the middle of the interior of the protective shell The protective shell for sealing a transmission mechanism is provided and the situation that dust or sundries enter the transmission mechanism and the transmission mechanism is damaged or the transmission efficiency is reduced can be prevented and meanwhile personnel can be prevented from directly contacting the transmission mechanism the rear axle of the electric cleaning vehicle is in a bilateral power mode and the problems of unbalanced power transmission and unstable running during running of the whole vehicle are solved and simple cushioning mechanisms are arranged at the two ends of the rear axle so that the use comfort of the electric vehicle is improved under the condition of considering the manufacturing cost
