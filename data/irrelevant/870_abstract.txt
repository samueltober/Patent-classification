The invention discloses a power supply system and a control method based on a space thermionic power generation device The system includes a thermionic power generation device a bus converter a power regulator a shunt resistor a solar cell array a shunt regulator a battery pack Charge and discharge controller and load cell By introducing a power regulator and a shunt resistor into the thermionic power generation device the output electric energy of the thermionic power generation device can be effectively regulated at the same time the proposed control method can realize the bus converter and the output voltage of the thermionic power generation device by constant control of the thermionic power generation device in different domains The power regulator dynamically adapts to work and forms different supporting control loops to achieve maximum output power generation The new power system architecture and control method proposed by this invention provide reference and help for the application of space power technology in the thermionic power generation device
