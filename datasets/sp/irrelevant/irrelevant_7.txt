 CNB     The invention relates to a method for manufacturing a guide rod  for a motor vehicle in particular a multipoint guide rod preferably a transverse guide rod The guide rod is basically composed of a fiberplasticcomposite structure In the step a preformed structure with fiber orientation matching the load is manufactured the preformed structure is introduced into a molding mold and the preformed structure solidified in the mold is conveyed by means of pressure and  or temperature and the guide rod is removed and continued to be processed
