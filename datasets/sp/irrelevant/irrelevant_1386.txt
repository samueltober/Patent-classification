 CNU The utility model provides a vehicle suspension displacement detection device based on piezoelectric effect and a vehicle the vehicle suspension displacement detection device comprises an Lshaped connecting plate one side of the Lshaped connecting plate is fixedly connected with a vehicle suspension one end of the movable connecting rod is connected with the other side of the Lshaped connecting plate the piezoelectric detection device is connected with the other end of the movable connecting rod and is fixed on a vehicle frame and the piezoelectric detection device generates a corresponding electric signal when the vehicle suspension moves and the controller is connected with the piezoelectric detection device and the controller is used for receiving the electric signal and taking the electric signal as a displacement signal of the vehicle suspension According to the utility model the displacement of the vehicle suspension can be accurately detected the linearity and the dynamic frequency response of the displacement detection of the vehicle suspension can be improved the structure is simple and the cost is low
