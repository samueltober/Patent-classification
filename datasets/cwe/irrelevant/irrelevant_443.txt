 CNU The utility model belongs to the technical field of hubs and particularly relates to an embedded tough magnesium alloy type light alloy hub which comprises a left half hub a and a right half hubb The opposite centers of the left half hub a and the right half hub b are respectively connected with two end surfaces of a cylindrical connecting piece  magnesium alloy reinforcing assemblies  are embedded in the left half hub a and the right half hub b The magnesium alloy reinforcing assembly  comprises a main magnesium alloy frame  and an auxiliary magnesium alloy frame  located in the main magnesium alloy frame  Wherein the auxiliary magnesium alloy frame  is connected with the main magnesium alloy frame  through a plurality of waveshaped rods  The hub has the advantages that the strength is improved and the shock absorption effect is also achieved
