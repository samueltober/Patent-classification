 CNA The invention relates to an intelligent electric control suspension damping system of an automobile The system comprises an information acquisition unit a data analysis control unit and an executionunit wherein the data analysis control unit comprises an ECU and a PWM module the execution unit comprises an air suspension and the air suspension comprises an air spring an air compressor an air distribution valve and a CDC damper the information acquisition unit comprises a vehicle body vertical acceleration sensor a wheel vertical acceleration sensor an air spring pressure sensor andan air spring height sensor the PWM module is in communication connection with the ECU and the CDC shock absorber and the ECU the vehicle body vertical acceleration sensor and the wheel vertical acceleration sensor are in communication connection and the air spring height sensor and the air spring pressure sensor are in communication connection with the ECU Rigidity damping force vehicle body height and other items are automatically adjusted according to different vehicle speeds driving states loading mass and changes of the number of passengers
