 CNA The invention discloses an electricity increasing agent for a powdery coating material the powdery coating material and an automobile wheel hub wherein the electricity increasing agent for the powdery coating material adopts monoglyceride and is used for electricity increasing and powdering of the powdery coating material during electrostatic spraying so as to eliminate and reduce the Faraday cage effect of the powdery coating material during electrostatic spraying According to the invention monoglyceride conventionally used as a surfactant or a dispersing agent is used as an electricity increasing agent of a powdery coating material so that a excellent electricity increasing and powdering effect can be realized and monoglyceride has an excellent migrationresistant precipitation function in a melting and curing process so as to further ensure the antislip function after powdering
