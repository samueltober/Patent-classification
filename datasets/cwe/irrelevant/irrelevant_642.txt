 CNA The invention relates to the field of automobile accessories in particular to an automobile hub bearing unit The automobile hub bearing unit comprises a flange shaft wherein the flange shaft comprises a shaft body provided with a bearing set in a sleeving manner and a shaft disc arranged at one end of the shaft body and extending in the radial direction and a first hub mounting portion and a second hub mounting portion are arranged on the shaft disc According to the automobile hub bearing unit the mounting universality of an automobile hub is greatly improved and an automobile can adapt to various complex road conditions conveniently
