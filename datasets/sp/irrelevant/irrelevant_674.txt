 CNU The utility model belongs to the technical field of automobiles and particularly relates to a novel automobile rear suspension support The utility model discloses a novel automobile rear suspensionsupport The device comprises a rack a vertical beam assembly a stabilizing arm assembly a hydraulic lock assembly and a shock absorber assembly the shock absorber assembly comprises an upper bearing platform a lower bearing platform a buffer block a buffer seat a first permanent magnet a second permanent magnet and a limiting mechanism a buffer block is arranged at the bottom of the upper bearing platform a buffer seat matched with the buffer block is arranged on the top face of the lower bearing platform the first permanent magnet and the second permanent magnet are fixedly connected with the upper bearing platform and the lower bearing platform respectively and the first permanent magnet and the second permanent magnet are the same in magnetic pole The automobile shock absorber is reasonable in structure good in shock absorption effect and excellent in silence effect
