 CNA The invention provides a central shaft container transport cart which comprises a loading platform a suspension mechanism is arranged at the bottom of the loading platform and three sets of rollingwheels are installed on the suspension mechanism At least two traction mechanisms are arranged at the front end of the loading platform each traction mechanism comprises a plurality of connecting bases installed on the loading platform at intervals and traction hooks with the front ends stretching out of the loading platform are installed on the connecting bases A plurality of locking mechanisms which are positioned on the edge of the loading platform and are used for locking the container body are mounted on the loading platform at intervals lifting supporting mechanisms located at the two ends respectively are arranged on the loading platform The situation that a loading platform structure in an existing trailer can only transport standard containers is improved
