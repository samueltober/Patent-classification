 USA An arrangement for connecting a link  of a chassis to a wheel carrier  by way of a ball sleeve joint  The link  has an link eye a the ball sleeve joint  has a ball sleeve  and the wheel carrier  has two bearing eyes   A sensor is located on the link eye a and a signal generator is located on the ball sleeve  The sensor and signal generator form an angle measuring device The ball sleeve  is connected to the bearing eyes   via a threaded pin  An antirotation unit   is provided between the ball sleeve  and the wheel carrier  in order to fix a defined angular position of the ball sleeve  with respect to the wheel carrier
