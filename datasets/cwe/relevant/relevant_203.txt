 CNA The invention discloses a detection device  for a wheel hub assembly  provided with a rolling bearing  and the detection device  is provided with a sound wheel  made of a magnetized material and mounted on a rotating ring  of the bearing  a shaped support element  angularly constrained to the rotating ring  of the bearing  and arranged directly between the sound wheel  and the rotating ring  such that the sound wheel  and the rotating ring  are angularly locked together a sensor  facing the sound wheel  and a protective member  integral with the rotating ring  of the bearing  so as to simultaneously protect both the sound wheel  and the sensor from external contaminants
