 CNA The invention relates to a bearing arrangement in particular a wheel bearing arrangement and preferably a wheel bearing arrangement of a loadcarrying vehicle comprising a bearing inner ring  having a first end face  and a structural element  having a second end face  which rests on the first end face  It is proposed that the bearing arrangement has at least one ring  which is arranged on the bearing inner ring by means of a first press fit and on the structural element by means of a second press fit
