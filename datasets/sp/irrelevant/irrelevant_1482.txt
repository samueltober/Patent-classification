 CNU An automobile chassis integrated module system in the technical field of automobile chassis comprises a tire a hub a hub motor a damping device a dust cover a speed reducer a steering motor andan automobile body connecting plate and the tire is arranged on the hub the driving end of the hub motor is connected with the hub and is used for driving the hub to rotate the lower end of the damping device is connected with the hub motor shell and the upper end is connected with the speed reducer the dust cover is arranged on the outer side of the damping device one end of the speed reducer is connected with the damping device and the other end is connected with the vehicle body connecting plate and the steering motor is arranged in the speed reducer and is connected with the damping device According to the invention the hub motor the damping device and the speed reducer are highly integrated into a whole to form a highefficiency and highpowerdensity driving module the overall weight is light and the unsprung mass of the whole vehicle is effectively reduced The device is reasonable in design is quickly connected with a vehicle body interface and is convenient to quickly disassemble and assemble
