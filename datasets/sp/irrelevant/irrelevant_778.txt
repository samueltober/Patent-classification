 WOA The invention relates to a ball joint  for a chassis of a vehicle comprising a joint inner part  and a slide bearing  the joint inner part  being movably mounted in the slide bearing  and a closure element  for closing a joint housing the slide bearing  and the closure element  being connected together To reduce the number of components andor assembly steps the ball joint  is characterised in that the slide bearing  for the joint inner part  is formed from a single onepiece plastic part
