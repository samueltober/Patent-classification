 CNA The invention discloses a distributed drive electric automobile wheel edge integrated system The distributed drive electric automobile wheel edge integrated system is composed of an independent suspension mechanism an independent turning mechanism a power drive mechanism and a braking mechanism In the independent suspension mechanism one end of an upper connecting rod assembly one end of a middle swing arm assembly and one end of a lower connecting rod assembly are hinged to the top the middle portion and the bottom of a knuckle assembly correspondingly and the other end of the upper connecting rod assembly the other end of the middle swing arm assembly and the other end of the lower connecting rod assembly are connected to an automobile body and a transverse stable rod assemblyand a spring damper assembly are connected to the middle swing arm assembly In the independent turning mechanism a turning motor and a worm and gear speed reducer are connected to the automobile body one end of a turning rocker is fixedly connected with a worm gear shaft and the two ends of a turning transverse pull rod assembly are hinged to the other end of the turning rocker and the knuckleassembly The two ends of a center pull rod are hinged to the turning rocker middle portion corresponding to wheels on the two transverse rods of an automobile and a locking hydraulic cylinder is arranged on the center pull rod in the axial direction By means of the distributed drive electric automobile wheel edge integrated system highspeed stability and high maneuverability of the automobile can be combined
