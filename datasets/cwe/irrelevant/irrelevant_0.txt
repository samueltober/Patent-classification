 CNA The invention discloses a wheel for stabilizing an automobile body and preventing the automobile body from deviating during automobile tire burst The wheel comprises  a tire body  an air storagebin  an inflation nozzle  a hub  tire patterns  a shared tire wall  an air pressure balance valve  a movable block  a shared toe and  a toe groove The wheel is advantaged inthat the vehicle direction the vehicle posture lane keeping and the like can be controlled during and after automobile tire burst so that the automobile body can take measures after running for a certain distance in a balanced and stable manner and traffic accidents caused by improper operation of a driver on the automobile during and after tire burst can be prevented
