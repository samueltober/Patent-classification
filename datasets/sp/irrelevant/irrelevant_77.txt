 KRB     BACKGROUND OF THE INVENTION  Field of the Invention The present invention relates to a stabilizer bar mounting structure of a vehicle More particularly the stabilizer bar may be mounted to a bearing unit in order to obtain a rotation angle measurement for calculating a vehicle height change without mounting a height sensor in a narrow space By inserting and mounting a rotation angle sensor to measure the rotation angle of the stabilizer bar in the bearing portion it relates to a stabilizer bar mounting structure capable of measuring the height of the stabilizer bar to measure the rotation angle
