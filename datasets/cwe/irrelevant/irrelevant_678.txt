 CNA The invention relates to a hubdriven integrated wheel for a baby carriage The hubdriven integrated wheel comprises a hub and a gearbox fixing mechanism installed in the hub A tire is arranged on the periphery of the outer side of the hub and a gear box is assembled in the gear box fixing mechanism The gear box is clamped with the hub so as to drive the integrated wheel to move the gear transmission mechanism adopts a multistage variable gear so that when the baby carriage moves under the condition that a driving motor adopts high power the gear transmission is stable the transmission ratio is accurate the working reliability is high the transmission efficiency is high the transmission torque is large and the service life is long the method is an improved and innovative mode of existing products wheels and gear boxes the wheels are directly driven the installation is convenient and the noise is reduced
