 CNB The invention provides a light emitting source and a driving chip for driving the light emitting source to realize constant current dimming control The driving chip comprises a rectifier module a constant current module a DC module a power factor module a first resistor a second resistor a third resistor a first transistor a first capacitor a first diode and a transformer The output terminal of the rectifier module is respectively connected with the input terminal of the DC module and the primary input terminal of the transformer A primary output terminal of the transformer is connected to a positive terminal of the first diode and a drain terminal of the first transistor respectively An output terminal of the power factor module is connected to a gate terminal of the first transistor The negative terminals of the first diodes are connected to the positive terminals of the light emitting sources respectively Compared with the related art the multichannel dimming driving system of the invention has high power factor small input voltage influence and good stability
