 KRA According to the present invention a wheel bearing assembly may comprise an outer ring coupled to a suspension system of a vehicle a wheel hub which rotates relative to the outer ring and includes a cylindrical portion and a joint portion extending from the cylindrical portion and having a thread formed on an outer circumferential surface an inner ring pressed into the outer circumferential surface of the cylindrical portion a transmission device having a rolling element interposed between the wheel hub to which the inner ring is coupled and the outer ring a nut engaged with the thread of the joint portion to provide a preload to the inner ring and a ring configured to prevent loosening of the nut in the axial direction The ring comprises a first ring portion inserted into the nut a second ring portion extending from the first ring portion along an axial inner crosssection of the nut and a third ring portion extending from the second ring portion and configured such that the tip contacts the wheel hub
