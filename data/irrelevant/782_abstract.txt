The invention relates to a novel threelevel Delta type connection rectifier which comprises a threephase alternating current power supply a filter circuit a Delta type connection rectification circuit and a threelevel circuit The AC input side of the rectifier is provided with a triangular connection and the DC link current can be shared by a plurality of devices at a given time so as to reduce the conduction loss A multilevel power converter is widely concerned in recent years due to the characteristics of high power and high voltage Compared with a traditional threelevel rectification circuit structure the novel threelevel Delta type connection rectifier provided by the invention has the advantages that the use of switching devices is reduced and the reverse recovery loss is eliminated
