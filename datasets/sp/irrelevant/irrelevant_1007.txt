 CNB The invention provides a C product AGV material transfer trolley and a C material transfer method and comprises a C product AGV material transfer trolley According to the C product AGV materialtransfer trolley provided by the invention the problem that an existing AGV is insufficient in stability when used for conveying fragile materials such as C products is solved through the structureof an AGV trolley main body a driving module a tray jacking and positioning mechanism a tray limiting and stopping mechanism and a tray conveying mechanism The purposes that the AGV material transfer trolley can still stably run on an uneven road surface the advancing direction can be changed without moving the AGV trolley main body by rotating the driving module and the tray containing space is utilized to the maximum extent are achieved
