The invention provides a DC wind turbine generator set and also provides a mediumvoltage DC direct gridconnected system realized based on the DC wind turbine generator set and a control and protection system matched with the mediumvoltage DC direct gridconnected system The DC wind turbine generator set provided by the invention can realize shortcircuit protection and fault ridethrough ofa DC transmission line under the condition that a DC circuit breaker is not configured for each fan and has a selfstarting function According to the mediumvoltage DC direct gridconnected system provided by the invention an offshore booster station is omitted and the system cost is further reduced According to the control and protection system provided by the invention the mediumvoltage DC direct gridconnected system can have a shore power grid lowvoltage fault ridethrough capability on the premise of not depending on communication and a shore converter station unloading device
