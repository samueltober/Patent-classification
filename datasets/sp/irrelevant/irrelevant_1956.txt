 DEA The invention relates to a bush bearing for a motor vehicle in particular an axle carrier bearing for supporting a vehicle axle against a motor vehicle body comprising a bearing core an elastomer body which at least partially surrounds the bearing core and an outer sleeve which surrounds the elastomer body and rests on a boundary surface of one of the bush bearings assigned bearing receptacle is formed The bush bearing according to the invention is characterized in that the outer sleeve has at least one latching element extending radially to an outer circumferential surface of the outer sleeve and elastically deflectable in the radial direction to produce a form fit between the outer sleeve and a latching element of the bearing receptacle that is complementary to the latching element of the outer sleeve The invention also relates to a storage system comprising such a bush bearing and an associated bearing receptacle
