 CNU The utility model relates to a lowcost largeload AGV moving chassis which comprises a chassis frame and is characterized by further comprising a carrying platform a slewing bearing a slewing bottom plate a differential steering mechanism and a plate spring Slewing bearings are connected to the front end and the rear end of the bottom face of the carrying platform in the chassis frame throughbolts a rotating bottom plate is installed on the lower portion of each slewing bearing plate springs are connected to the two sides of each rotating bottom plate in a hanging mode and the middleof each plate spring is limited on a connecting shaft where wheels are installed And wheels on the connecting shaft are driven by a differential steering mechanism The AGV is scientific and reasonable in structural design has the advantages that the manufacturing cost is reduced the gravity of a loaded object can be buffered the shock absorption function is achieved operation is stable andimplementation is easy and is high in innovativeness
