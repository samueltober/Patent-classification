 KRA The present invention relates to a direct current DCbased elevator power system According to an embodiment of the present invention the DCbased elevator power system comprises a DC power conversion device converting alternating current AC power supplied from a system into DC power a common DC power unit to which the DC power converted from the DC power conversion device is supplied and one or more inverters receiving the DC power from the common DC power unit and converting the same into the AC power to supply the same to one or more winding machines for driving an elevator According to the present invention the power supplied to the winding machine of the elevator is supplied from the common DC power unit to reduce the number of conversions of AC and DC thereby reducing power loss due to the conversion
