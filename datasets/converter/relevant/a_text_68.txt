 USA The present document relates to a power converter comprising an inductor a first stage and a second stage The first stage may be coupled between an input of the power converter and the inductor and the first stage may comprise a first flying capacitor The second stage may be coupled between the inductor and an output of the power converter and the second stage may comprise a second flying capacitor A second terminal of the first flying capacitor may be connected to a first terminal of the inductor and a first terminal of the second flying capacitor may be connected to a second terminal of the inductor
