 CNU The utility model discloses a balanced suspension type driving wheel device for an AGV Automatic Guided Vehicle which comprises a fixed seat a balance plate a damping mechanism and a driving wheel assembly and is characterized in that the fixed seat is connected to the AGV the balance plate is provided with a hinged end and a movable end and the hinged end is rotatably connected to the fixed seat the damping mechanism is mounted between the movable end of the balance plate and the fixed seat and the damping mechanism is connected with the fixed seat and the movable end of the balanceplate the driving wheel assembly is installed at the movable end of the balance plate so that the damping mechanism can buffer jolt borne by the driving wheel assembly The shock absorber has good shock absorption and shock absorption effects can adapt to rugged road surfaces and is compact in structure small in size and large in provided power
