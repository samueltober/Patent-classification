 CNA The invention discloses a directcurrent converter for seabed power supply and a control method thereof The directcurrent converter comprises a bypass part a highfrequency switch part a transformer part a rectifying part and a filtering part the bypass part comprises an input inductor and a plurality of same bypass circuits the highfrequency switch part comprises a plurality of same highfrequency switch circuits and the transformer part comprises a plurality of same highfrequency transformers The rectifying part comprises a plurality of same rectifying circuit groups the filtering part comprises a plurality of same filtering circuits and the filtering circuits are connected with the rectification circuit groups which are in series output in a onetoone correspondence manner According to the present invention an automatic redundant switching and selfadaptive voltage regulation control method can realize the functions of quick automatic switching input voltage selfadaption and the like and is particularly suitable for the seabed power supply occasions
