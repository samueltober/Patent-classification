 CNA The invention relates to the field of vehicle drive axle structures in particular to a drive axle flow guide device and a drive axle which can reduce power waste caused by oil stirring of a gear on the premise of not reducing the amount of gear oil and ensuring normal operation of a main speed reducer The drive axle flow guide device comprises a flow guide cover the flow guide cover extends in the arc direction the cross section of the flow guide cover is in the shape of a groove with an opening in one side the opening of the groove points to the center of the arc a connecting part is arranged on the side face of the flow guide cover and the flow guide cover can form blocking on the outer side of a main speed reducer a differential mechanism and other mechanisms When the gear rotates and gear oil is thrown a blocking effect can be achieved and power waste caused by stirring of the thrown gear oil and other gear oil is prevented
