 CNU The utility model relates to a novel pretightening structure of a fullfloating axle hub bearing Wherein the outer bearing is arranged on one side of the bearing nut the hub is sleeved on the outersides of the bearing nut and the outer bearing an inner bearing is arranged on the inner side of the hub the outer bearing and the inner bearing are sleeved on the outer side of a shaft head a hubspacer bush is arranged between the outer bearing and the inner bearing and a hub spacer bush adjusting gasket is arranged between the outer bearing and the hub spacer bush According to the structure the inner rings and the outer rings of the outer bearing and the inner bearing are simultaneously extruded and pretightened through the tapered rollers so that the bearing is pretightened to beused in a reasonable design state and the service life of the bearing is better guaranteed
