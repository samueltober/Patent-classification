 EPA The invention relates to an additional spring  for a shock absorber  of a motor vehicle and to a damper bearing  for a shock absorber  of a motor vehicle Here the additional spring  comprises a first spring body  which has a central hole  for a piston rod  of the shock absorber  to be led through According to the invention the first spring body  is formed spherically on one end face  The damper bearing  according to the invention comprises a cylindrical receiving space  in which the first spring body  of the additional spring  is partly held and is distinguished by the fact that the receiving space  has a spherically shaped bottom face  formed so as to correspond with the end face  of the first spring body
