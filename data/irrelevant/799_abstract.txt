The invention relates to a multishafting mechanical transmission system quality evaluation method based on spectral analysis and can be applied to product quality grading after factory inspection or maintenance of large and mediumsized multishafting mechanical transmission systems and belongs to the field of state monitoring and fault diagnosis of mechanical equipment Aiming at the defects of application of a traditional detection method in multistage mechanical transmission equipment quality evaluation and factory grading the invention provides the multishafting mechanical transmission system quality evaluation method based on spectral analysis and the method can effectively and accurately identify fault feature information in a composite signal generated by multistage transmission Meanwhile a certain feature comparison and anomaly grading method is established and used for overall quality evaluation of the multistage mechanical transmission equipment
