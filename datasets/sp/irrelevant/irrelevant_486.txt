 CNA The invention relates to the field of axle manufacturing and discloses a motor external suspension type axle assembly The motor external suspension type axle assembly comprises an axle housing a differential mechanism arranged in the axle housing and a driving motor arranged on the axle housing Ushaped bolts for fixing a steel plate spring to the axle housing is connected to the axle housinga first fixing rod is provided with an adjusting convex portion a second fixing rod is provided with an adjusting groove the second fixing rod is rotationally connected to an adjusting nut the second fixing rod is provided with a limit retaining ring and the adjusting nut is rotated to move the first fixing rod away from the second fixing rod to apply outward thrust to the two Ushaped boltsto restrict the two Ushaped bolts from bending inwards The motor external suspension type axle assembly has the following advantages and beneficial effects that the driving motor is arranged on theaxle housing the length of a transmission chain is reduced and the energy consumption is reduced and fixing components are arranged on the Ushaped bolts periodic deformation of the Ushaped boltsis limited the fatigue of the Ushaped bolts is reduced and the safety of an axle is improved
