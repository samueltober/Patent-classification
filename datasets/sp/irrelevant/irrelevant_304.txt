 CNB The invention discloses an automobile electronically controlled semiactive suspension system based on magnetorheological technology and a control method thereof The system comprises an assembly module for a suspension assembly and a magnetorheological shock absorber a detection module configured to detect vertical acceleration information of the vehicle body relative travel information of thewheels and the vehicle body and temperature information of the magnetorheological damper a suspension control module is configured to identify a current traveling road state of the vehicle according to the vertical acceleration information of the vehicle body the relative travel information of the wheels and the vehicle body and the temperature information of the magnetorheological damper and to control a damping state of the magnetorheological damper according to the current traveling road state The system can realize realtime electronic control of suspension damping force without making major changes to the vehicle and effectively improve the comfort and ride comfort of the vehicle in the process of driving
