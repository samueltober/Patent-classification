 CNA The invention discloses a vertical buffering integrated suspensionsteering mechanism and belongs to the field of mechanical design and manufacturing The suspensionsteering combined mechanism comprises a steering claw a cross beam connecting piece a piston an upper spring a lower spring a stop component a sleeve bolts and a gear train chassis wherein the sleeve is fixed with the gear train chassis through the bolts the piston moves in the sleeve the lower spring on the inner bottom surface of the sleeve and the stop component are used for limiting the maximum axial movement of thepiston in the sleeve the piston and the stop component are buffered by the upper spring and the cross beam connecting piece the steering claw and the piston are connected The suspensionsteeringcombined mechanism can be applied to the field of wallclimbing robots the hidden dangers of impact and wall surface falling caused by falling of the robot body on the wheel train in the posture changing process of the wallclimbing robot are avoided the wheel train provided with the suspensionsteering mechanism has the independent steering capacity and the wallclimbing robot can safely and flexibly work on the curved wall surface
