 CNA The invention discloses a front stabilizer bar mounting seat assembly structure based on a meter fullloadbearing structure lowentrance city bus which comprises an upper fixing seat lower hoops forming rubber sleeves and a stabilizer bar the two ends of the stabilizer bar are sleeved with the two forming rubber sleeves and the outside of each forming rubber sleeve is fixedly connected to the bottom of the upper fixing seat through the lower hoop which is sleeved with the forming rubber sleeve The leftright center distance of the base of the front stabilizer bar mounting seat assembly is large so that the roll stability of the whole vehicle is greatly improved layout adjustment in the height direction of the upper fixing seat is flexible and installation and layout of chassis brake pipelines and wire harnesses are facilitated
