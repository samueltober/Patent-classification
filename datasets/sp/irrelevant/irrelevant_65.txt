 DEB     Method for producing a connecting component for a vehicle wherein a plastic rod  and two plastic bodies  firmly connected to one another via the plastic rod  are produced characterized in that To produce the plastic rod  a tube  is made from a thermosetting plastic and a thermoplastic is poured into the interior  of the tube  which after it has solidified a through the tube  forming a core  extending out from the ends of the tube  with a respective connection area  and To produce the two plastic bodies  firmly connected to one another via the plastic rod  each connection area  is encapsulated with a thermoplastic material and forms a material bond with it which forms one of the plastic bodies  after it has solidified
