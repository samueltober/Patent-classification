 KRA Disclosed is a ball joint for a vehicle that can contribute to weight reduction According to the present invention a ball joint for a vehicle comprises a casing a ball stud portion in which a ball portion is formed so as to be accommodated in the casing a plug portion fitted to the casing to support the ball portion and having a grease accommodating portion formed therein to receive grease and an insert molding portion formed by injection of molten resin between an inner surface of the casing and an outer surface of the ball portion and surrounding an outer surface of the plug portion
