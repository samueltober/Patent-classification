 KRB     The present invention relates to a regenerative active suspension device equipped with a dual bidirectional pump According to the present invention a cylinder for receiving a working fluid therein a piston for separating the inside of the cylinder into a tension chamber and a compression chamber a pump connected to the cylinder to generate kinetic energy by receiving a working fluid and in the pump In the regenerative active suspension device equipped with a dual bidirectional pump comprising a motor for converting the generated kinetic energy into electrical energy the pump is provided on both sides of the rotation axis of the motor the pump is connected to the tension chamber It provides a suspension device including a pump and a second pump connected to the compression chamber
