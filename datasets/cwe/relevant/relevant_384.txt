 KRA The present invention relates to a seal for a wheel bearing for preventing foreign matter from entering the wheel bearing The object of the present invention is to provide the seal for a wheel bearing wherein a structure of a main branch having magnetic force in a lip unit of a rubber unit and a multiple branch protruding from one end of the main branch is applied Accordingly when one of the multiple branches in close contact with the surface of the seal outer race unit wears out to lose a sealing function the other one of the multiple branches can perform the sealing function while automatically coming in close contact with the surface of the seal outer race unit
