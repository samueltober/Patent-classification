 WOA The invention relates to a stabilizer bar  for a chassis of a vehicle comprising a curved stabilizer bar body  that forms a torsion spring a pendulum support   and a connecting part     the connecting part     is made of a metal and connects the stabilizer bar body  to the pendulum support   said connecting part     and said stabilizer bar body  being interconnected by means of a snapfit andor detent connection In order to simplify assembly and to make the connection of the stabilizer bar body  to the pendulum support   both secure and simple the stabilizer bar  or the pendulum support   is characterised in that the connecting part     has an opening  provided with a bearing shell  made of plastic for movably supporting a joint ball  of the pendulum support
