 CNU The utility model discloses a tractor rear axle halfshaft driving shell which comprises a half shaft and a halfshaft driving shell body connected with the half shaft in a rotating mode through a tapered roller bearing and a lowtemperature heating device and a hightemperature cooling device are arranged in the halfshaft driving shell body According to the utility model the lowtemperature heating device and the hightemperature cooling device are arranged so that the halfshaft driving shell can be always in a proper temperature range under the temperature control of the first temperature control sheet and the second temperature control sheet no matter the halfshaft driving shell is used in a lowtemperature or hightemperature environment and the influence on the transmission efficiency of the half shaft is avoided
