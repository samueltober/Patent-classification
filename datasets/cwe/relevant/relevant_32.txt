 KRA The present invention relates to a hub bearing comprising a diskshaped wheel joint joined to a wheel a hub having a bearing mounting part which hollows the wheel joint and protrudes to have a first face spline on the protruding surface and a bearing the bottom of which is fixated to the bearing mounting part to come in contact with one side of the wheel joint the top of which is fixated to a vehicle body and has a second face spline on one side of the bottom A face spline is separately arranged in the hub and the bearing to synchronize the rotation of a driving shaft the hub and the bearing with each other thereby preventing a slip between the hub and the bearing against the rapid acceleration of a stationary vehicle
