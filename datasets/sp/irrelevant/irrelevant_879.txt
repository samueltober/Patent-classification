 CNA The invention discloses a disconnected airport ferry axle and relates to an axle The disconnected airport ferry axle comprises a left hub assembly and a right hub assembly which are symmetrically arranged and disconnected wherein the left hub assembly and the right hub assembly are respectively installed on a Cshaped beam of an air suspension and a left wheel and a right wheel are respectively and independently installed on the left hub assembly and the right hub assembly Each of the left hub assembly and the right hub assembly comprises a hub brake drum assembly a disconnected axle housing a gatetype structure adjusting device a brake air chamber and an adjusting arm assembly the hub brake drum assembly is installed at one end of the disconnected axle housing and the other endof the disconnected axle housing is fixedly connected with the gatetype structure adjusting device the gatetype structure adjusting device is fixed on the Cshaped beam of the air suspension andthe brake air chamber and the adjusting arm assembly are respectively arranged on the gatetype structure adjusting device According to the invention the space of a chassis can be increased the left wheel and the right wheel can move independently and the disconnected airport ferry axle has the advantages of light weight simple structure high universality good driving stability reliable performance convenient maintenance and the like and is suitable for airport ferry vehicles
