The invention discloses a nearzero carbon emission distributed energy supply system and method the energy supply system comprises an energy storage and supply unit and a photovoltaic power generation unit a wind power generation unit an external power grid access unit and a fuel cell power generation unit which are respectively connected with the energy storage and supply unit and the fuel cell power generation unit is connected with a carbon capture unit Compared with the prior art the invention has the positive effects that a set of nearzero carbon emission distributed power thermal power and hydrogen supply scheme is provided a technology integrating multiple new energy sources such as photovoltaic wind power hydrogen energy fuel cells carbon capture and the like is provided for the first time and finally multienergy complementation and supply coupling are realized and the invention discloses a distributed energy system for supplying electric energy hydrogen energy and heat energy under capture and recovery of carbon dioxide and provides an implementable technical scheme for realizing a carbon neutralization target
