 CNU The utility model discloses an automobile hub bearing which belongs to the technical field of automobile parts and is characterized in that the automobile hub bearing comprises a flange plate and a bearing arranged on the flange plate the bearing comprises a bearing ring the bearing ring abuts against the flange plate the bearing ring comprises a locking device and the locking device comprises a transmission rod a first gear and a stop block The bearing ring is provided with a first groove for containing the transmission rod and a second groove for containing the first gear and the stop block the first groove is communicated with the second groove the transmission rod is provided with a second gear meshed with the first gear the stop block is provided with first gear teeth meshed with the first gear and the flange plate is provided with a third groove The stop block penetrates through the second groove to be connected with the third groove in a clamped mode The automobile hub bearing can be fixedly locked between the bearing and the flange plate
