 KRA A disclosed seal assembly for a wheel bearing includes a metal core facing the inside of a wheel bearing assembly a slinger facing the outside of the wheel bearing assembly a sealing member fixed to the metal core and having a plurality of sealing ribs pressed to the slinger and a deformation unit having a first spring and a second spring which are mounted on the slinger and allowing deformation of the slinger according to a vehicle speed The first spring and the second spring are arranged to be parallel to each other
