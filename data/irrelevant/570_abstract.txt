The invention relates to a novel cascaded BuckBoost highgain converter and relates to a highpower boost converter which belongs to the technical field of power electronics and comprises a first power switch tube S a second power switch tube S a third power switch tube S a fourth power switch tube S a first boost inductor L a second boost inductor L a boost capacitor C an input capacitor Ci an output capacitor CO an input port Vi and an output port VO The converter has the advantages of simple structure low cost low input current ripple high gain and bidirectional energyflow
