 CNU The utility model belongs to the technical field of automobile hub assembly The transverse mounting structure for the automobile hub comprises a transverse mounting plate the hub unit assembling device comprises an axle an axle fixing support a sliding device and a positioning device the axle is fixed to the axle fixing support in the horizontal direction the sliding device is arranged belowthe axle the positioning device is connected to a sliding block of the sliding device a hub unit to be assembled is arranged on the positioning device and the hub unit and the axle are concentricThe hub unit the sliding device and the Vshaped positioning device are combined into a whole it is guaranteed that the whole and an axle share the same benchmark and the hub unit is prevented from being not concentric with the axle during installation Therefore largebatch installation can be guaranteed frequent adjustment is not needed and the problem that hub units are difficult to install on heavy axles is solved
