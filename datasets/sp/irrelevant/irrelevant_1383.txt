 CNA The invention aims to provide a mobile robot The mobile robot comprises a chassis a weighing assembly and suspension assemblies the weighing assembly is arranged above the chassis and the two suspension assemblies are arranged on the two sides of the chassis correspondingly the weighing assembly comprises a weighing bottom plate a pressure sensor and a bearing connecting piece the pressuresensor is arranged below the weighing bottom plate and the weighing bottom plate is connected with the chassis through the bearing connecting piece the suspension assembly comprises a driving wheeland a driving device thereof The driving device comprises a spring an adjusting plate located above the spring and used for compressing the spring and a rotating motor The weight of goods can be effectively detected and a result is fed back to the rotating motor so that the compression amount of the spring is changed and it is ensured that the mobile robot can still effectively operate in different load states
