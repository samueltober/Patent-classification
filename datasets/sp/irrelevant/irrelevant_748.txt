 DEA     The invention relates to a suspension strut bearing  for a motor vehicle with a guide ring  and a cap  rotatably mounted relative to the guide ring  a roller bearing  between the guide ring  and the cap   is arranged with a first bearing ring  resting on the guide ring  and a second bearing ring  resting on the cap  wherein a plurality of rolling elements  are arranged between the first bearing ring  and the second bearing ring  and a labyrinth seal  is formed between the guide ring  and the cap  It is provided that the cap  is fastened to the guide ring  of the suspension strut bearing  by means of a snap lock
