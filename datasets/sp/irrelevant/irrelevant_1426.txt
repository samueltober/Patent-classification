 CNA The invention relates to a walking device and a robot The walking device comprises a moving chassis used for bearing and installing a robot main body a first wheel train module and a second wheel train module wherein the moving chassis can be detachably assembled and connected with at least one of the first wheel train module and the second wheel train module and the first wheel train module is used for enabling the walking device to be suitable for indoor walking occasions and the second wheel train module is used for enabling the walking device to be suitable for outdoor walking occasions According to the scheme the first wheel train module and the second wheel train module in the walking device share one moving chassis the universality of the walking device is improved and an effect of reducing design manufacturing and use cost is achieved
