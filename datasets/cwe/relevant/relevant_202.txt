 WOA The invention relates to a wheel support  for a vehicle having at least one seat opening  in which a wheel bearing  via which at least one wheel hub of the vehicle can be mounted rotatably on the wheel support  can be seated at least partially and having a centring seat  which is arranged in the seat opening  and via which the wheel bearing  can be supported in the radial direction on the wheel support  and can be centred relative to the wheel support  wherein the centring seat  has a plurality of centring surface segments  which follow one another in the circumferential direction  of the seat opening  are spaced apart from one another and between which respective recesses  of the wheel support  are arranged
