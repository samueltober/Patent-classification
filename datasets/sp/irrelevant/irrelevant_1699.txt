 KRA Disclosed is a strut bearing assembly The strut bearing assembly according to the present invention comprises an insulator portion having a receiving portion formed in the center thereof And a strut bearing unit coupled to the receiving unit wherein the strut bearing unit includes an upper case unit pressfitted to the inner surface of the receiving unit to be elastically deformable And a lower case portion rotatably mounted on a lower side of the upper case wherein the upper case portion comprises an upper case body portion inserted into the receiving portion And a protrusion formed on an outer surface of the upper case body and elastically deformable while being pressfitted into the inner surface of the receiving unit and the protrusion is formed in the upper case body by double injection
