 CNB The invention relates to a shock absorber assembly and a vehicle The shock absorber assembly comprises a shock absorber and a knuckle bearing The knuckle bearing comprises an inner ring the inner ring is used for being matched with the outer circumferential face of the upper end of the shock absorber to be installed on the shock absorber and the knuckle bearing is used for being connected between the shock absorber and a frame or between the shock absorber and a vehicle body By using the shock absorber assembly the height of the vehicle bottom plate can be effectively reduced
