 CNA     The invention provides a steering drive device in the technical field of automobile carrier accessories The steering drive device includes a base with a through hole A roller is rotatably connected to the upper part The lower end of the roller is below the mounting frame At least three sets of shockabsorbing mechanisms are arranged between the tooth plate and the base The shockabsorbing mechanism includes a shockabsorbing tube and an end cover and one end of the shockabsorbing tube It is hinged on the base the other end of the shockabsorbing cylinder is provided with a containing cavity and a sliding rod is slidably connected in the containing cavity On the end cover the outer side of the shockabsorbing cylinder is sleeved with a spring one end of the spring is connected to the shockabsorbing cylinder and the other end of the spring is connected to the end cover
