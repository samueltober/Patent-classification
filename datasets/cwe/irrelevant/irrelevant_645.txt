 CNU The utility model discloses an automobile hub with a compressionresistant structure relates to the technical field of automobile hubs and aims to solve the problem that the compressionresistant and shockabsorbing effects are not good enough when the existing automobile hub is used An inner steel ring is installed on one side of the interior of the automobile hub a reinforced compressionresistant inner steel ring is installed in the inner steel ring a connecting hole is formed in the reinforced compressionresistant inner steel ring a reinforced compressionresistant arm is installed outside the front end of the reinforced compressionresistant inner steel ring and a fixed supporting wheel shaft is installed in the middle of the interior of the automobile hub And a damping buffer block is mounted on one side of the interior of the reinforced compressionresistant inner steel ring
