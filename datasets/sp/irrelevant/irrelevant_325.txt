 CNB The invention provides a variablerigidity variabledamping semiactive suspension The variablerigidity variabledamping semiactive suspension comprises a main magnetorheological damper an auxiliary magnetorheological damper a main spring and an auxiliary spring The main magnetorheological damper is connected with the main spring in parallel and the auxiliary magnetorheological damper is connected with the auxiliary spring in parallel The main magnetorheological damper and the auxiliary magnetorheological damper are coaxially connected in series and the main spring and the auxiliary spring are coaxially connected in series By means of the structure the rigidity and damping force of the semiactive suspension can be accurately adjusted according to acceleration signals in the vertical direction and therefore a good damping effect on a car is achieved and the comfort of car driving and riding is effectively improved
