The invention discloses a staged battery energy storage capacity configuration method considering full life cycle economy The method comprises the steps building a staged battery capacity degradation model by considering the relation between the discharge depth of a staged battery and the cycle life determining benefit and cost factors of staged battery energy storage economy evaluation according to the characteristics of staged battery energy storage and performing economy evaluation on staged battery energy storage considering the influence of the battery capacity retention rate on theeconomy and the cycle life of the battery energy storage system establishing a staged battery energy storage capacity configuration model taking the optimal economy in the whole life cycle as a target and setting a constraint condition equation set of the capacity configuration model and finally carrying out optimization solution on the capacity configuration model by utilizing a firefly algorithm and obtaining an optimal capacity configuration scheme of staged battery energy storage The method can effectively improve the economy and accuracy of a capacity configuration result providessupport for the planning of a staged battery energy storage system and has a good application prospect
