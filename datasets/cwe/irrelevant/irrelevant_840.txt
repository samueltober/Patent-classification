 CNA     The name of the invention is an automobile front axle hub bearing unit It belongs to the technical field of commercial wheel hub bearing units It is a wheel hub bearing unit provided for the ton front axle of SAIC Hongyan Automobiles National VI platform Its main features are including wheel hub inner bearing outer bearing ABS gear ring seal  and seal seat  etc there is a spacer sleeve between the inner bearing and the outer bearing which can compensate for bearing wear during use The resulting clearance maintains the stability of the bearing clearance thereby ensuring the reliability and long life of the hub bearing and the unit The invention has the characteristics of improved loadbearing performance maintenancefree no environmental pollution long service life and improved transmission efficiency and is mainly used for the wheel hub bearing unit of the ton front axle of the SAIC Hongyan Automobile National VI platform
