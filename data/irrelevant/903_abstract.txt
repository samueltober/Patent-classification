The invention provides a modular wearable phototherapy instrument which includes a power supply module a control module a display module a drive module an equipment casing an LED flexible circuit module a temperature detection module and a homogenization module The modular wearable phototherapy instrument has a simple structure and is easy to use It can freely assemble the lightemitting LED circuit to achieve light physiotherapy on multiple parts of the human body such as chest waist elbows knees legs etc suitable for hospitals And family personal use At the same time the power supply is externally installed and the safety of users during the use of the product can be further guaranteed
