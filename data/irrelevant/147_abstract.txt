The invention belongs to the technical field of electric distribution and safety protection and particularly relates to a multifunctional integrated type V electrical distribution and protection device for an electricdrive special vehicle Compared with the prior art the multifunctional integrated type V electrical distribution and protection device suitable for the electricdrive specialvehicle is provided The electrical distribution and protection device is mainly suitable for occasions where compound power supplies are applied for vehicle highvoltage grids reliable electric energy transmission and distribution are conducted on V loads of all power grades of the whole vehicle and complete highvoltage safety protection measures are provided for personnel on the vehicle and the highvoltage loads The electrical distribution and protection device is currently at an engineering design stage and can be verified together with an electromechanical system rack after assembling and adjusting are completed and verified together with a certain real vehicle
