 KRA The present invention provides a bearing assembly of a wheel driving device capable of stably applying a preload in a state in which a nut member is directly in contact with a bearing without mediating a washer or the like and an assembly method thereof According to the present invention the bearing assembly of a wheel driving device comprises a rotary shaft in which an insertion groove is formed a bearing mounted around the rotary shaft a lock nut which is fastened around the rotary shaft and preloads the bearing and a loosening prevention member which has one end inserted into the insertion groove and the other end extending in the radial direction
