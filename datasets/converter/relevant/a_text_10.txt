 EPB A seriesconnected multilevel power conversion device  A B according to an aspect of embodiments includes a multiwinding transformer  and a power conversion unit  The multiwinding transformer  has a relationship that n secondary windings a to f respectively connected to n singlephase power converters a to f in the same output phase have a voltage phase difference of n degrees and a relationship that the m secondary windings a to f respectively connected to the m singlephase power converters a to f corresponding to the m output phases have a voltage phase difference of m degrees
