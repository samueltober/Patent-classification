 CNA The invention discloses a digital electronic control device of a gas turbine generator set The device is used for collecting and processing signals such as a rotor rotating speed signal an air inlettemperature signal an exhaust temperature signal a lubricating oil temperature signal and a switching value signal of the gas turbine generator set and further outputting a control signal to control the working states of all accessories of the gas turbine generator set Therefore digital electronic automatic control is achieved accurate and stable control is provided the operation state ofthe gas turbine generator set can be monitored in real time the safety and reliability of the gas turbine generator set are improved a solution is provided for achieving multivariable multitask and complex state control the whole device is of a frame type stacked structure all plate assemblies are convenient to assemble and disassemble the overall structure is compact the size is small and carrying is convenient
