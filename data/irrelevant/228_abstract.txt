The invention discloses a test power supply and a cascade static synchronous compensator converter valve test system The test system comprises a test power supply with adjustable output voltage and frequency a tested converter valve a control device and a manmachine interaction system wherein the test power supply is connected to a primary coil of a multiple phaseshifting transformer throughan alternating current power supply each secondary coil of the transformer is connected to a rectification module and is output to a direct current bus and the secondary coils pass through an inversion module and are sequentially cascaded and are sequentially output through connection with a reactor The control device synchronously adjusts the test power supply and the tested converter valve through optical fiber communication The converter valve test system has the characteristics of integration universality voltage and frequency adjustability high automation degree applicability tosystem tests and the like The invention further discloses a test method for the cascade static synchronous compensator converter valve test system
