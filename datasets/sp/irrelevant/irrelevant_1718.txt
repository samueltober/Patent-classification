 CNA The invention relates to a rhythm chassis a rhythm control method thereof and an automobile Therhythm chassis comprises a chassis body a central control processor directly or indirectly mounted on the chassis body a voice recognition sensor directly or indirectly mounted on the chassis body and electrically connected with the central control processor and a rhythm execution system arranged on the chassis body electrically connected with the central control processor and used for driving the chassis body to rhythm Therefore the rhythm execution system can drive the chassis body to have rhythm with corresponding frequency and amplitude such as jumping swinging and the like so that the effect that the automobile can dance along with music is achieved and the automobile is endowed with a new role due to the capability of rhythm along with the music therefore the automobile brings visual auditory tactile and olfactory multisense experience to the user and the service capability and the application scene of the automobile are improved
