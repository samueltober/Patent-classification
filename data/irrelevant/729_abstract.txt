The invention relates to a multicomputer display control switching method for a VPX case and belongs to the technical field of VPX module display control According to the invention a computer module is connected with an exchange board module a DP display switching module and a USB switching module in the exchange board are utilized to realize a function of multiinput and oneoutput of display control signals and the output single display control signal can be connected to display control equipment a keyboard a mouse and a display for testing and debugging so that the debugging and operation convenience of multiple computer modules of the VPX case is improved By means of the method the use amount of display control equipment in the debugging process of the multicomputer moduleVPX case is reduced and the demand for display control debugging interfaces of the VPX case is greatly reduced
