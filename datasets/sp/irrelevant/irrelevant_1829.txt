 CNU     A damping wheel for a wheelchair comprising a roller mounted on a roller frame the roller frame is fixedly connected with a rotating shaft the rotating shaft is rotatably arranged in a shaft sleeve and a friction block is arranged in the shaft sleeve and the friction block is connected with The rotating shaft is in contact and matched and a spring is pressed on the friction block and the spring is bound up and down in the sleeve The damping wheel generates damping force for the steering of the roller through the friction plate The size of the damping force can be adjusted by adjusting the bolt The spring can automatically compensate the damping force after the friction block is worn The application of the damping wheel on the wheelchair makes the steering process more gentle and the patient feels it is good
