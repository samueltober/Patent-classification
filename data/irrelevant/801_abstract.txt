The invention relates to a multiview video processing device and method based on a PAL system The invention provides the multiview video processing device and method based on the PAL system for solving the technical problem that frontend video acquisition equipment  is difficult to upgrade due to the limitation that backend computer equipment can only process standard PAL system images in the prior art Video acquisition equipment  and a comprehensive processing unit  are newly added and the video acquisition equipment  comprises at least one degree panoramic camera andor at least one key area monitoring camera An image processing module  of the comprehensive processing unit  can carry out preprocessing distortion correction stepless zooming region windowing clipping picture recombination and video coding processing on received video information and then an existing standard PAL system video channel and an existing rearend processing unit are used for obtaining a degree panoramic image and a key region monitoring image around a carrier
