 CNU The utility model discloses a structure based on a rear air suspension of a fullloadbearing urban passenger car which comprises a rear suspension fixing arm assembly an air bag assembly a rear axle housing and a tie rod assembly the main body of the rear suspension fixing arm assembly is of a frame structure and two ends of the rear part of the rear suspension fixing arm assembly incline outwards and extend upwards to form an air bag bracket The air bag assembly comprises a left air bag assembly body and a right air bag assembly body the left air bag assembly body and the right air bag assembly body are installed on the air bag bracket respectively the rear axle housing is installed at the position close to the rear end of the rear suspension fixing arm assembly body the transverse pull rod assembly is arranged between the left air bag assembly body and the right air bag assembly body and one end of the transverse pull rod assembly is connected with the right air bag assembly body And the other end is connected with the left airbag assembly According to the utility model the overall design structure is more compact simple convenient and practical and meanwhile the urban passenger car with the fullloadbearing type car body air suspension structure also obtains higher maneuverability and driving stability
