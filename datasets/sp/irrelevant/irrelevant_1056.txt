 KRA The present invention relates to a fastening device between a lower arm and a knuckle for an automobile capable of simultaneously obtaining antidive performance and impact improvement performance That is the present invention is to provide a fastening device between a lower arm and a knuckle for an automobile which may simultaneously obtain antidive performance and impact improvement performance by improving a fastening structure between the lower arm and the knuckle to a screw structure which can move the knuckle backward during bump operation so that the amount of farward movement of a wheel is reduced as the knuckle is moved backward by the pump operation during braking even if the angle  between a line connecting both ends of a lower arm of a front wheel and the ground is increased
