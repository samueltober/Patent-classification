 CNA The invention belongs to the field of vehicle engineering and discloses an unmanned vehicle wheel hub motor modular driving system which is characterized in that a first axle a second axle and a third axle are single trailing arm axles a fourth axle is a double cross arm axle and each single trailing arm axle comprises a tire rim assembly a trailing arm a trailing arm mounting frame a hydropneumatic spring and a wheel hub motor the doublewishbone bridge comprises an upper wishbone a lower wishbone a steering knuckle and a hub motor According to the invention the distributed driving hub motor is matched with the hydropneumatic spring for independent suspension so that the vehicle realizes ultrahigh geometric obstacle passing capacity and highspeed crosscountry road maneuvering capacity The system adopts a fullpoint driving mode so that the power density is high the used space is small and the work is stable and reliable besides the system components are designed in a modular mode interchangeability is good manufacturing cost is low and maintenance is easy
