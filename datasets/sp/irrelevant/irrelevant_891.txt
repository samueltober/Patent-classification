 CNA The invention discloses an electric racing car wheel rim system which is an assembly of all parts between the ground and a cross arm and specifically comprises a rim a stand column an angular contact ball bearing a hub a hub nut a dustproof gasket a wheel speed gear ring nut a cotter pin a transmission pin an adjustable gasket and the like which are installed and connected On the premisethat the wheel rim system has enough bearing strength the effects of being easy to assemble compact in structure and light in weight are achieved and therefore the overall performance of the racing car is improved
