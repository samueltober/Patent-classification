 DEA The present invention relates to a semiactive pendulum support  for a motor vehicle in particular an offroad vehicle with two fastening points   each arranged at a distal end for fastening the pendulum support  to a chassis and a passive stabilizer of the motor vehicle  comprising a substantially hollowcylindrical housing  having the first fastening point  and an inner contour element  having the second fastening point  with an inner contour  axially guided in the housing  wherein the Inner contour element  can be coupled to the housing  by means of a locking device
