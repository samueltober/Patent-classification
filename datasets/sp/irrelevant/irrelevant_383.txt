 WOA The invention relates to a vehicle axle  in particular for a passenger car having two wheel carriers  which are guided on axle links     and to each of which a wheel is fixed and having a stabiliser  which connects the two wheel carriers  to each other The stabiliser  is connected directly to the wheel carriers  for which purpose the wheel carriers  are each equipped with a joint  to which one of the wheel carrierside ends of the stabiliser  is fixed
