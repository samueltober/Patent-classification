 CNU The utility model relates to the field of automobile starting lifters Specifically the present utility model relates to a battery the utility model relates to a middle axle lifting device The device comprises an inflatable leather bag an upper supporting plate a lower supporting plate an upper cover plate and a lower cover plate the upper end of the inflatable leather bag is connected withan upper supporting plate an upper cover plate is installed on the upper supporting plate the lower end of the inflatable leather bag is connected with a lower supporting plate a lower cover plateis installed at the lower end of the lower supporting plate the plate faces of the upper supporting plate and the lower supporting plate are annular concaveconvex faces an inflation hole is further formed in the upper supporting plate and an inflation groove is formed in the upper cover plate The middle axle lifting device solves the problems that in the prior art a plurality of air bags are combined and installed on the two sides of an axle cost is high debugging is difficult and a single independent air bag is poor in structural strength and poor in stability in the lifting process The lifting device has the advantages of being convenient to install and easy to debug the strength and bearing capacity of the lifting device can be greatly improved the structure of the liftingdevice is firmer the structure is simple and reliability is high
