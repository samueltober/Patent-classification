 CNA The invention discloses a safety automobile hub structure relates to the technical field of hub structures and aims to solve the problems that when an existing automobile hub is used a central exposed gap is large garbage and dirt or small animals are easy to drill in and safety is not facilitated An inner steel ring block is installed in the automobile hub a fixing hole is formed in the inner steel ring block a reinforcing rib block is installed in the middle of the interior of the inner steel ring block a center hub fixing bearing seat is installed at one end of the reinforcing ribblock and a supporting connecting rod is installed on one side of the reinforcing rib block A ventilation groove is formed in the supporting connecting rod and a protective safety cover is installed in the center hub fixing bearing seat
