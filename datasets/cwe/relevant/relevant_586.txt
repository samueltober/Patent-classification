 CNU The utility model relates to a pretightening type automobile hub bearing which belongs to the technical field of hub bearings and comprises a hub flange a bearing outer ring sleeved with the hub flange is arranged on the periphery of the hub flange and a left bearing inner ring and a right bearing inner ring which are symmetrically distributed are arranged between the bearing outer ring and the hub flange The left bearing inner ring and the right bearing inner ring are both fixedly connected with the hub flange in a clamped and embedded mode retainers are arranged between the left bearing inner ring and the bearing outer ring and between the right bearing inner ring and the bearing outer ring respectively and a plurality of balls fixedly connected with the retainers in a clamped and embedded mode are arranged in the retainers A hoop connected with the outer wall of the bearing outer ring in a nested mode is arranged between the inner side end of the bearing outer ring and the hub flange The brake device has the characteristics of compact structure high assembly precision good use effect good brake performance and long service cycle The lubricating oil seal performance of the hub unit is improved and the operation loss of parts is reduced
