 KRB     Disclosed is a strut of a suspension for a vehicle The disclosed strut of the suspension device for a vehicle includes an insulator member to which one end of a piston rod is coupled and the other end of the piston rod is movably inserted and coupled to an axle and uses flow resistance generated by movement of the piston rod to form a vehicle body The damper member for damping vibration the strut bearing member into which the insulator member is inserted the dust cover member into which the strut bearing member is inserted and the dust cover member are formed in a shape surrounding the dust cover member to fix the dust cover member to the strut bearing member It characterized in that it comprises an upper pad member one end coupled to the damper member and the other end portion in contact with the upper pad member to elastically support the upper pad member
