 CNU The utility model discloses an extraheavy rigid balance suspension device which comprises balance beams axles a frame bearings flanges first bolt and nut assemblies and second bolt and nut assemblies the balance beams are arranged on the front axle and the rear axle in parallel the bearings are arranged at the two ends of the balance beams and the second bolt and nut assemblies are arranged on the balance beams The axle is fixedly connected with the bearing through the first bolt and nut assembly and can rotate relative to the balance beam the flange plate is fixedly arranged in themiddle of the balance beam and fixedly connected with the frame through the second bolt and nut assembly and the balance beam is a box type bearing part formed by welding steel plates and is of a symmetrical structure The balanced suspension device is high in structural strength and bearing capacity the defect that the bearing capacity of an existing rigid balanced suspension is insufficient can be effectively overcome meanwhile the safety of related vehicles can be improved and the carrying level can be improved
