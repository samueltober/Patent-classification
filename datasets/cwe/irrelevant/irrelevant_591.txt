 WOA A grease tool for a unitized wheelend assembly is provided The grease tool may have a plug with a fluid conduit to place a lubrication source in fluid communication with an inner bearing and a cap with a fluid conduit to place the lubrication source in fluid communication with an outer bearing The grease tool may include a cylindrical body having an inner bearing support and an outer bearing support The cylindrical body has an inner rotor and disc and an outer rotor and disc where the inner rotor and the outer rotor cause the discs to move between an extended and retracted position
