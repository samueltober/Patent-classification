 WOA A wheel bearing according to one embodiment of the present invention can comprise a first seal member disposed between first outer peripheral surfaces of first cylinder parts of an outer ring and an inner ring so as to seal the gap between the outer ring and the inner ring and a second seal member pressfitted into a second cylinder part of the inner ring The second seal member can include a slinger having an axial outer end portion spaced from a first seal member and a first end surface a first seal lip which is coupled to the axial outer end portion of the slinger and which has a first seal part coming in contact with a portion of the first seal member and a second seal part coming in contact with a portion of the first cylinder part and a second seal lip coupled to the slinger so as to be spaced from the first seal lip in an inwardly axial direction
