 CNA The embodiment of the invention provides a vehicle a vehicle suspension control method and related equipment The vehicle comprises a first part a second part and a vehicle suspension wherein the vehicle suspension is located between the first part and the second part the first component is a component borne by a vehicle suspension the second component is used for bearing the vehicle suspension and the first component and the vehicle suspension comprises a variable damper connected between the first component and the second component The variable damper is used for providing first acting force for the first component according to the first acceleration of the first component so as to control relative displacement of the first component and the second component in the height direction of the vehicle By implementing the vehicle bumping of the vehicle in the driving process can be effectively reduced and the riding comfort of the vehicle is improved
