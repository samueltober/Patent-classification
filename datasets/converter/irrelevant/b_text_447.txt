 CNB The invention discloses a converter power assessment test system based on an isolation transformer The system comprises a power supply a primary isolation transformer and a plurality of tested converters a primary winding of the primary isolation transformer is connected with the power supply in a triangular connection mode the primary isolation transformer comprises a plurality of secondary windings and the plurality of secondary windings are connected with the plurality of tested converters in a star connection mode The converter power assessment test system based on the isolation transformer has the advantages of being simple in structure stable reliable high in universality high in test efficiency and the like
