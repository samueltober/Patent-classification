The invention discloses a frequency measurement method applied to a standard meter of an electric energy meter calibrating device wherein the method comprises the steps sampling input voltage of a power source performing digital directcurrent filtering processing on voltage sampling data caching multistage sampling filtering data judging zero crossing points according to a zero crossing condition and repeatedly counting two zero crossing points by using a counter and estimating a zero crossing point position by combining the sampling filtering data on the two sides of the two zero points and carrying out frequency calculation The frequency measurement of the standard meter of the electric energy meter calibration device does not need a hardware zerocrossing detection circuit and the hardware cost is saved meanwhile a reliable zerocrossing judgment mechanism and a simple and convenient calculation method improve the frequency measurement precision of the standard meter of the electric energy meter calibration device
