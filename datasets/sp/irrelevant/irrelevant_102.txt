 JPA To provide a steering knuckle which adapts to requirement of intensity and which can reduce weightSOLUTION A steering knuckle  comprises a wheel bearing storage part  at least one upper brake carrier coupling part  and at least one lower brake carrier coupling part  a transverse link coupling a steering arm  a damper storage part  a support rib  and a support web  The support rib is extended from one side of the damper storage part the upper brake carrier coupling part is arranged on the support rib the support rib is extended from another side of the damper storage part The steering knuckle has at least one opening  the opening is arranged in a region between the wheel bearing storage part and the damper storage part The opening is divided by a stay bolt  extended from the damper storage part or support web to the wheel bearing storage partSELECTED DRAWING Figure
