 CNU The utility model discloses a steel plate spring support of a stamping and welding structure belongs to the field of automobile axles and aims to solve the problems that in an existing automobile axle due to the fact that the steel plate spring support is of a cast steel structure the weight is increased and the cost is increased According to the utility model firstly a stamping welding structure is adopted to replace an original cast steel structure so that the weight and the cost are reduced energy is saved and pollution is reduced Meanwhile a stamping inclination angle structure is adopted and used for adjusting the installation angle of the axle housing assembly and the weight reduction advantage of a stamping structure is brought into play
