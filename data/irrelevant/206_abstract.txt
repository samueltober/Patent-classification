The invention discloses a circuit package for a power converter The power converter can comprises a plurality of power level modules Each power level module comprises a power level integrated circuit IC chip an output inducer and a set of capacitors The output inducer is coupled to the switch node of the power level integrated circuit IC chip and the set of capacitors forms the output capacitor of the power level module The output capacitors of multiple power level modules are close to each other and are symmetrically arranged on a substrate The output inducers of the power level modules can be installed on the side where the first surface of the substrate stays Power level integrated circuit IC chips of multiple power level modules are installed on the first surface or theside where the second surface of the substrate stays The second surface is opposite to the first surface The output capacitors of multiple power level modules are closely arranged so as to assist the interconnection among the output capacitors of multiple power level modules
