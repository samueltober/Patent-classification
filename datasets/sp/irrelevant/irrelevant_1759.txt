 CNU The utility model discloses a sixwheel walking system of a straddle carrier The sixwheel walking system comprises a frame wheels suspension wheels and a buffer structure The frame is connected with the front wheel and the rear wheel through a slewing bearing and is connected with the suspension wheel through the buffer structure the wheels and the suspension wheels are arranged on the left side and the right side of the frame and are symmetrical The utility model provides a sixwheel walking system of a straddle carrier and aims to reduce wheel pressure improve the shock absorption performance of the whole vehicle and guarantee the stability of the whole vehicle The device is simple in structure and convenient to use
