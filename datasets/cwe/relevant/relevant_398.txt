 CNU The utility model relates to a lightweight nondriving conical hub unit which mainly comprises an outer ring an inner ring a flange and an outer ring the inner ring is sleeved on the flange the inner ring is positioned inside the outer ring three rolling bodies are arranged between the outer ring and the inner ring a gear ring is fixed on the inner ring one end of the outer ring is connected with an end cover and a sealing ring is arranged at the other end contacted with the flange The automobile hub unit has the beneficial effects that a row of rolling bodies are additionally arranged in the middle of the existing automobile hub unit so that the hub unit is provided with three rows of rolling bodies and the bearing capacity of the hub unit can be greatly improved
