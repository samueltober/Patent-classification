 CNU The utility model discloses a front axle of a land leveler The front axle comprises a bridge frame hubs telescopic oil cylinders and inclination angle joints one end of the inclination angle jointis fixedly connected with the bridge frame through a pin shaft and a bearing and the other end of the inclination angle joint is connected with the telescopic oil cylinder through a pin shaft The telescopic oil cylinder is fixedly connected with the hub through a tapered roller bearing the telescopic oil cylinder is connected with the hub so that the wheel track of the front wheel can be changed freely the front tire and the rear tire of the land leveler can be interchanged for use after the wheel track is changed and the service life of the tires is prolonged by more than one time
