 CNA The invention discloses a pressure regulating type hub An air cylinder barrel is arranged on the hub the air cylinder barrel is communicated with gas in a tire supported by the hub through a hole inthe hub a movable piston is arranged in the air cylinder barrel a nut is arranged on the piston the nut is connected with a lead screw on a motor the motor is arranged on the hub the motor drives the lead screw to rotate so that the piston is made to move forwards or backwards in the air cylinder barrel and therefore the pressure of the air in the air cylinder barrel and the tire is increased or decreased
