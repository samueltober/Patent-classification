 WOA Disclosed is a hydraulic shock absorber comprising a shock absorber piston  supporting in the front a rod  which comprises a stop piston  penetrating into an end stop tube  and which further comprises on the front face of the stop piston  a first bore  comprising an outlet at the rear of the stop piston  on the side of the stop piston  a second bore  comprising an outlet at the rear of the stop piston  the shock absorber further comprising a sliding plug  which is equipped with a timing chamber  and which in a front position closes at least one outlet of the bores   and in a rear position connects said outlets the plug  being located between a front restoring spring  and a rear restoring spring  resting on the shock absorber piston
