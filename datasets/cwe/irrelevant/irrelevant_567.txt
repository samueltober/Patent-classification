 WOA The present invention relates to a decoupling device  for a wheel hub motor assembly comprising at least one motor  at least one fiberreinforced joint apparatus  which is arranged on one side of the motor wherein the at least one motor can be coupled to a vehicle wheel  via the at least one fiberreinforced joint apparatus for transmission of torque and at least one resilient apparatus  which is arranged in the axial direction on the other side of the motor
