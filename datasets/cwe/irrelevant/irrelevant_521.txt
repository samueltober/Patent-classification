 CNU The utility model relates to an aluminum alloy hub for a commercial vehicle drive axle which comprises a hub a rim and a brake drum the hub is concentrically connected with a large disc the outerside of the hub is connected with the brake drum and the rim through knurled bolts the inner side of the hub is connected with a shaft head through a bearing and the shaft head is connected with a half shaft through a doubleend stud the aluminum alloy hub has the advantages of being high in strength light in weight simple in process and low in production cost
