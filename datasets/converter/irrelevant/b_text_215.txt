 CNB The invention relates to a control method of a pure electric automobile modular multilevel converter and belongs to the technical field of control The invention aims to solve the problem in specialmodular multilevel converter control for pure electric vehicle driving and the control method of the pure electric automobile modular multilevel converter by designing a layered controller is disclosed The method comprises the following steps that  a modular multilevel converter mathematical model is established  a finite set prediction controller control target function is designedand  optimization and solving of a multistep finite set prediction control screening algorithm is carried out According to the method the optimal and suboptimal conditions are taken into consideration at the same time and the situations are considered in some research while all the switches are considered in the next time domain and the method of the invention has the advantages that theoptimal and suboptimal conditions and the left and right electric level numbers are used as alternative selection so that the calculation burden is further reduced
