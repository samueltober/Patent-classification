 CNU The utility model discloses a suspension system of a vehicle and the vehicle with the suspension system The suspension system comprises a stabilizer bar assembly the stabilizer bar assembly comprises a transverse rod two longitudinal rods and bushings the two longitudinal rods are arranged at the two ends of the transverse rod in the length direction each longitudinal rod is provided with the corresponding bushing and the bushings are connected with the auxiliary frame the spiral springs are used for bearing acting force in the vertical direction the two spiral springs are located on the left side and the right side of a symmetric line extending in the frontback direction of the transverse rod and the upper ends of the spiral springs are suitable for being connected with a vehicle body at least one of the bush and the spiral spring is adjustable in rigidity According to the suspension system of the vehicle the posture of the vehicle body can be controlled the driving stability is improved and then the riding experience of a user is improved
