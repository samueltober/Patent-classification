 CNU The utility model relates to the field of mechanical equipment in particular to an allterrain vehicle which comprises a vehicle body a third power generation device connected with the vehicle bodya second folding shaft connected with the third power generation device a tire fixing device connected with the second folding shaft and a tire connected with the tire fixing device The third powergeneration device can drive the second folding shaft to rotate so as to control the steering angle of the tire by arranging the third power generation device and the second folding shaft the problem that the allterrain vehicle cannot be driven obliquely and steered in situ is solved and therefore the allterrain vehicle is better suitable for certain terrains with narrow space and turning difficulty and the like
