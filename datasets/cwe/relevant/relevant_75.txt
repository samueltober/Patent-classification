 CNB     The invention relates to a wheel bearing unit which has a hub  at least one first rolling bearing  and at least one fixing unit  the fixing unit  is designed separately from the wheel hub and is axially end of the wheel hub The parts  are arranged at intervals wherein the fixing unit holds at least one component  of the rolling bearing to prevent the center of gravity  of the wheel bearing unit from disengaging from the hub It is recommended that the fixing unit contacts the hub
