 CNU The utility model relates to a Mecanum wheel independent suspension mechanism The Mecanum wheel assembly device is applied to the technical field of wheel assembly and comprises a Mecanum wheel a mounting seat and a shaft seat the shaft seat is slidably connected into the mounting seat a nitrogen spring is arranged on the mounting seat a piston rod of the nitrogen spring penetrates into the mounting seat and abuts against the shaft seat and a driving part used for driving the Mecanum wheel to rotate is arranged on the shaft seat The Mecanum wheel has the effect of widening the application range of the Mecanum wheel
