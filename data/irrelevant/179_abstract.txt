The invention discloses a multitarget power quality comprehensive treatment device used for a medium voltage distribution network The multitarget power quality comprehensive treatment device comprises a threephase parallel transformer and three singlephase series transformers and three singlephase energy storage conversion units corresponding to each phase in A phase B phase and C phase each singlephase energy storage conversion unit comprises n first units and n second units each first unit comprises a singlephase fullbridge ACDC converter a power supply side bidirectional DCDC converter an energy storage device and a load side bidirectional DCDC converter which are connected in sequence and each second unit comprises a threephase sixbridge arm DCAC converter The multitarget power quality comprehensive treatment device is mainly used for the medium voltage distribution network of medium  kV or  kV voltage level the load side harmonic current can be inhibited the threephase current of a distribution transformer is balanced the utilization rate of the transformer is improved the reactive power of the load is compensated dynamically the power factor of the distribution network is improved the active power of the load is compensated quickly and the effects such as stability and reliability for user power supply voltage are improved
