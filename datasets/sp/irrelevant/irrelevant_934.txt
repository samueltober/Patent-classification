 CNU The utility model discloses a connecting structure of a shock absorber and a chassis system The connecting structure comprises a piston rod assembly a mounting disc assembly and a rubber metal sleeve The piston rod assembly is installed in the piston cylinder the open end face of the piston cylinder is fixedly connected with the installation disc assembly the end away from the piston cylinder of the installation disc assembly is connected with a vehicle chassis and the rubber metal sleeve is installed in the installation disc assembly and arranged at one end of the piston rod assemblyin a sleeving mode The installation disc assembly is in rigid connection with the automobile body and the rubber metal sleeve can generate elastic deformation when bearing lateral force to achieve the flexible connection effect
