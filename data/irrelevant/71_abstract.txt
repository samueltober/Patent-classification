A line commutation converter LCC for a highvoltage direct current power converter The LCC includes at least one LCC bridge circuit connected to at least one terminal of a DC system and each bridge circuit includes a plurality of arms Each arm is associated with a corresponding phase of the AC system and each arm includes one or more upper thyristor valves and one or more lower thyristor valves connected in series related branches extending between the upper thyristor and the lower thyristor and At least one thyristorbased capacitor module for each phase each module includes a plurality of modular thyristors each capacitor module is operable to insert the main capacitor into the bridge by triggering at least one or more of the modular thyristors In the corresponding arm of the circuit
