 WOA A motor vehicle  is disclosed having a chassis  with a longitudinal centre line  and a vehicle vertical plane through which the longitudinal centre line passes The vehicle  has first and second wheels LR on respective opposite sides of the longitudinal centre line  A coupling system  couples the first and second wheels L R to the chassis  in a manner to enable the wheel L and R to tilt in unison relative to the vehicle vertical plane and turn in unison with the wheels remaining parallel with each other
