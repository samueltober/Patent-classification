 USA A top mount assembly according to one aspect of the present disclosure may comprise an insulator comprising an elastic body and disposed between a vehicle body and a strut and a strut bearing comprising an upper housing comprising at least one hole through which a portion of the elastic body passes a lower housing and a bearing disposed between the upper housing and the lower housing The elastic body may comprise an elastic protrusion protruding downwards from the hole The lower housing may comprise a groove which extends along the circumferential direction such that the elastic protrusion is inserted into the groove and is positioned at the radial inner side of the bearing The elastic protrusion is inserted into the groove to seal between the upper housing and the lower housing at the radial inner side of the bearing
