 CNU The utility model discloses a highstability hub Relating to the field of electric vehicle hubs the problem that a hub is poor in stability during running is solved The key point of the technical scheme is that a hub comprises an outer wheel an inner wheel and spokes the spokes are connected with the outer wheel and the inner wheel a buffer wheel is arranged in the inner wheel the buffer wheel comprises a first steel ring a second steel ring and a rubber layer the rubber layer is arranged between the first steel ring and the second steel ring an axial through hole is formed in the rubber layer a plurality of clamping teeth used for connection are arranged on the inner wall of the rubber layer the second steel ring and the inner wheel are in transmission through double rows of conical balls and the excellent effects of being high in bearing capacity stable in running stable in transmission and high in hub strength are achieved
