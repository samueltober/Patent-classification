 WOA A direct drive long travel steering knuckle the knuckle having a hub with an expanded cavity wherein such cavity houses an articulated joint adapted to receive the outermost end of a mid axle shaft wherein such mid axle shaft is lengthened to extend from a differential of the vehicle through the hub cavity The expanded hub cavity accommodates an extended range of motion of the axle shaft and accordingly the articulating hub provides for increased vertical range of motion of the vehicles wheels especially when used in conjunction with an active controlling suspension system
