 WOA The present invention relates to a pneumatic suspension arm and assembly method preferably used in trucks buses trailers and semitrailers comprising a vehicle suspension comprising a pair of arms  positioned in the longitudinal direction of the vehicle in which one end of the arm is fastened in a swivelling manner to the front supports  the intermediate portion of the arm is fastened to the vehicle axle  and the rear end of the arm fastens the pneumatic springs  the suspension also including elements   for fastening the arm  to the axle  an articulated bearing  in the front supports  and other normal components of a pneumatic suspension The elements of the pair of arms  follow a sequence and assembly method with welds to join the parts applied at various different assembly stages so as to form a single block
