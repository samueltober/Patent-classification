 WOA The invention relates to a planetary transmission  for a roll stabiliser  of a motor vehicle comprising at least one first planetary stage  having a first sun gear  a first ring gear  multiple first planetary gears  engaging radially between these two a first planetary carrier  with play which can be shifted andor tilted in a radial direction within the scope of the available play and a spring mechanism  for noise reduction The spring mechanism  engages on the first planetary carrier  and shifts andor tilts same out of its centred neutral position in a springloaded manner in an unloaded state of the planetary transmission  such that the first planetary gears  thereof come into contact with the first sun gear  andor the first ring gear  The invention also relates to a roll stabiliser  having a planetary transmission  of this type
