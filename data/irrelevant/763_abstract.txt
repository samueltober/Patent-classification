The invention relates to the technical field of illumination and discloses an illumination monitoring method and equipment so as to realize remote regulation and control and improve the regulation and control efficiency The illumination monitoring method comprises the steps that an illumination system is deployed wherein the illumination system comprises a host and singlelamp controllers which are connected through a  bus and each singlelamp controller is connected with one path or one controlled lamp and the host acquires the state information of the controlled lamps from the single lamp controllers judges whether the corresponding controlled lamps have faults or not according to the acquired state information and starts corresponding fault processing plans when confirming that the corresponding controlled lamps have faults For example when an arbitrary controlled lamp breaks down other controlled lamps which are adjacent to the brokendown lamp and can work normally are indicated to supplement light by enhancing brightness
