The invention relates to a torsional damping electromagnetic antidancing device which is mainly composed of a discshaped excitation component and induction components The excitation component andthe induction components are arranged in sequence along the axis and there is an air gap between the excitation component and each induction component The excitation component is mainly composed ofa base plate and a sector permanent magnet Each induction component is mainly composed of a current conducting plate and a concentrating flux plate The torsional damping electromagnetic antidancingdevice of the invention has the advantages of simple structure stable operation safety and reliability low cost and good damping effect
