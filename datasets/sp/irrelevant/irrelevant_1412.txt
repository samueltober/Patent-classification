 CNA The invention discloses an automobile active suspension intelligent control method based on a deep reinforcement learning algorithm The automobile active suspension intelligent control method comprises the steps that automobile body parameters are used as training data autonomous training is carried out on the deep reinforcement learning algorithm a trained deep reinforcement learning algorithmis obtained and the damping force or rigidity of an automobile active control suspension is adjusted in real time through the algorithm According to the automobile active suspension intelligent control method based on the deep reinforcement learning algorithm the selflearning capability is introduced into the active suspension and possible conditions are continuously explored so that a control strategy which is more excellent than that of manual coding is learned and the implementation efficiency of the active suspension is improved
