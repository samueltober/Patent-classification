 DEA     The invention relates to a wheel flange  for a motor vehicle  having at least the following components A bearing holder  preferably an outer ring  of a rolling bearing  and A flange element  for fastening a rotatable body preferably a wheel rim  The wheel flange  is primarily characterized in that the flange element  has a plurality of load introduction elements  preferably screw sleeves which are each separately connected to the bearing receptacle  by means of a web  the Web  has a back surface  which extends axiallyradially inclined from an axially inner section  of the bearing seat  to the associated load introduction element With the wheel flange proposed here a heavyduty wheel flange with a very low weight is proposed which can be produced in very different manufacturing processes depending on the number of pieces
