A power distribution system  connected to a power source  and a plurality of loads  includes a converter  a power distribution unit  including a plurality of solid state power controllers SSPC and configured to provide power from converter  to plurality of loads  and a fault protection unit  operatively coupled to converter  power distribution unit  or both The fault protection unit  includes a sensor  to sense current at output terminal of at least one of power distribution unit  or converter  and a controller  to compare sensed current with a threshold value of current identify presence of a fault in at least one of plurality of loads  power distribution unit  and converter  based on comparison and activate a protection scheme to provide fault ride through during the presence of the fault
