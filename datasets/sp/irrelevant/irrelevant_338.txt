 CNB     The present invention discloses a vehicle rollover prevention control method device storage medium and vehicle The method includes acquiring displacement sensor data of an electronically controlled air suspension of the vehicle and determining the electrical system according to the displacement sensor data Control the load value of the air spring of the air suspension perform overload warning andor rollover warning according to the load value so as to realize the antirollover control of the vehicle The solution of the present invention can solve the problem that the risk of rollover is likely to occur during the running of the vehicle when the pressure sensor is not installed in the vehicle and achieves the effect that the risk of rollover is unlikely to occur
