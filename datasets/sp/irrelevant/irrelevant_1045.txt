 CNU The utility model discloses a lower cross arm connecting structure for a steerbywire vehicle The lower cross arm connecting structure comprises a steering knuckle the steering motor base is fixedly arranged on one side of the steering knuckle and a first shaft hole is formed in one side of the steering motor base two supporting lugs are arranged on one side of the lower cross arm and secondshaft holes are formed in the supporting lugs the pin shaft penetrates through the second shaft hole and the first shaft hole at the same time so that the lower cross arm is rotatably connected with the steering motor base the two shaft sleeves are arranged at the two ends of the pin shaft in a sleeving mode respectively inner rings of the two first bearings are correspondingly arranged at the other ends of the shaft sleeves in a sleeving manner respectively the two glands are correspondingly propped against the outer rings of the first bearings respectively and are fixedly connected with the second shaft holes two compression bolts The structure can prevent the motor base and the lower cross arm from generating a gap when the vehicle moves when the wheel jumps The utility model further provides a lower cross arm steering structure for the steerbywire vehicle
