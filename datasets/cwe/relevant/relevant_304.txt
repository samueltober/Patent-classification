 DEA     The invention relates to a wheel bearing arrangement  with a bearing axle  for a motor vehicle  comprising at least the following components A wheel hub  for a vehicle wheel  A multirow axially braced roller bearing  with an outer ring  rolling elements   and a first inner ring  and a second inner ring  the first inner ring  and the second inner ring   are only connected in a forcetransmitting manner by means of the rolling elements   The wheel bearing arrangement  is primarily characterized in that the second inner ring  is pushed onto the wheel hub  with a clearance fit  and secured against circumferential slippage and axially by means of a separate connecting means    the wheel hub  is fixedThe wheel bearing arrangement proposed here is secured against circumferential slippage and can be produced using a simple and inexpensive method
