 CNU The utility model discloses a middlerear axle structure of a commercial vehicle which comprises a middle axle a rear axle a balance suspension and a steel plate spring two ends of the steel plate spring are respectively connected with the front axle and the rear axle the middle axle and the rear axle are connected with the balance suspension through an upper thrust rod and a lower thrust rod the middle axle is provided with a driving structure and the rear axle is not provided with a driving structure A lifting mechanism is installed between the middle axle and the rear axle the lifting mechanism can be controlled through an instrument desk switch the middle axle and the rear axle are both matched with double tires and on the premise that the bearing capacity is guaranteed the weight of the whole vehicle is lighter cost is lower oil consumption is reduced the turning radius is reduced the passing ability of a curve is guaranteed and meanwhile the problem of tire abrasion is solved
