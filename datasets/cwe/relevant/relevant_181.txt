 FRA         The invention relates to an actuator assembly for wheel drive assistance comprising a support  connected to a chassis or suspension arm  a hydraulic motor  a hub  supporting a rotatably mounted wheel a bearing  which provides rotational guiding of the hub  a bearing bearing bearing   and a shaft  which establishes a rotational drive connection between a rotating element  of the motor and the hub  characterized in that the support bearing  is formed of two portions   a first portion  of which is secured to the support  and which forms a sealed portion of the motor housing  and a second portion  attached to the first portion  and which serves as a bearing support
