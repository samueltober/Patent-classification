The invention provides a control method of a singlephase chain power electronic energy storage converter The control of the whole energy storage converter is divided into four links an energy storage battery SOC control loop an energy storage unit cascaded connection inverting current control loop power grid voltage phase locking and energy storage unit SOC balance control The invention aimsto provide an energy storage converter control method after cascade connection of energy storage battery units suitable for different voltage levels and different residual capacity so as to improvethe utilization ratio of energy storage batteries make the battery selection of energy storage devices more flexible and make it possible for a lowvoltage battery to be connected into a mediumvoltage power grid for use
