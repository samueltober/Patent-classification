 KRA The present invention relates to a suspension thrust bearing device  for use with a suspension spring  in a suspension strut of a vehicle The device  includes a bearing having upper and lower annular bearing members in relative rotation while a lower bearing member has a lower cup  having at least one protruding indexation element    In addition the device  also includes a damping element  made of a resilient material and interposed between the lower cup  and the suspension spring  The indexation element    is made of a different material than that of the lower cup  the lower cup  being made of a rigid plastic material and the indexation element    being made of a resilient material The present invention is relatively inexpensive has good operational reliability and has an increased service life
