 CNA The invention relates to a modular AGV independent suspension device and application thereof The modular AGV independent suspension device comprises a driving motor a driving wheel a driving support a spring a connecting guide shaft a damping support and a fixing support the driving wheel is fixedly installed in the driving support and the driving motor is installed on the outer side of the driving support and connected with the driving wheel one end of the damping support is connected with the driving support through the connecting guide shaft the connecting guide shaft is sleeved with the spring and the other end of the damping support is connected with the fixed support The compression amount of the damping spring is adjusted through an adjusting nut damping parameters areadjusted the device adapts to various complex road surfaces and is good in damping effect and the suspension device is independent of the overall structure simple in structure and convenient to disassemble assemble and maintain
