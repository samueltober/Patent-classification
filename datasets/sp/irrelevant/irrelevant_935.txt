 CNA The invention discloses a suspension system for generating electricity through the dead weight of a new energy automobile The suspension system comprises a connecting main pin and a sliding guide rail Wheels are arranged at the two ends of the connecting main pin A first bearing arm and a second bearing arm are sequentially arranged on the surface of the connecting main pin and located on the inner sides of the wheels in a sleeving mode The sliding guide rail is arranged on an automobile body A negativepressure damping cylinder is slidably connected to one side of the sliding guide railand one end of the first bearing arm and one end of the second bearing arm are rotationally connected with the two ends of the negativepressure damping cylinder correspondingly The invention relates to the technical field of automobiles According to the suspension system for generating power through the weight of a new energy automobile kinetic energy of upanddown floating generated by suspension in the automobile running process is transmitted to the negativepressure damping cylinder through the two bearing arms and when the vibration is absorbed and buffered through the negativepressure damping cylinder the kinetic energy can be transmitted to a power generation device through a transmission piece the mechanical energy is converted into electric energy through the power generation device to be used by an automobile the mechanical energy generated by automobile floating is greatly utilized and energy conservation and environmental protection are better achieved
