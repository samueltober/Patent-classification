 CNU An automobile front swing arm assembly comprises a swing arm body a ball head connecting station is arranged on the swing arm body the ball head connecting station can be connected with a swing armball head and the swing arm ball head is in interference fit with the connecting position of the ball head connecting station Wherein the swing arm main body is provided with a rubber bushing and ashock absorption mounting hole and the rubber bushing can be used for mounting the bushing the swing arm body is provided with a swing arm reinforcing area recessed backwards and lightening holes are formed in the swing arm reinforcing area The automobile front swing arm assembly has good bending resistance and tensile strength the electrophoresis layer on the surface of the swing arm body can improve the load impact bearing capacity of the swing arm and therefore the swing arm is not prone to breakage when bumping for a long time or being impacted by loads
