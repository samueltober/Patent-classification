 CNA     A damping force constrained linear segmented control method of magnetorheological semiactive suspension belongs to the technical field of magnetorheological semiactive suspension It presents a linear segmented control method of semiactive suspension considering the energy dissipation characteristics of magnetorheological damper It includes the following steps S test the nonlinear characteristics of the magnetorheological damper to obtain the outer envelope of the adjustable range of the damping force S obtain the linear segmented damping force constraint S construct the linear segmented damping force constraint S Calculate the damping force control law S According to the damping force control law use the realtime vibration signal obtained by the sensor to calculate the damping force required for vibration reduction S Use magnetic current The variable damper inverse model converts the calculated damping force into the actual drive current
