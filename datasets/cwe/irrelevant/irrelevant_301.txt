 CNU The utility model belongs to the technical field of automobile axles and particularly relates to a hub sealing structure The oil seal comprises an oil seal seat ring an oil seal seat and an oil seal assembly wherein a first annular groove is formed in the right side of the oil seal seat ring the oil seal seat and oil seal assembly comprises an oil seal and an oil seal seat a second annular groove is formed in the left side of the oil seal seat the right side of the oil seal seat is connected with the left side of the oil seal seat in a matched mode and the oil seal is arranged betweenthe first annular groove in the right side of the oil seal seat and the second annular groove in the left side of the oil seal seat A single oil seal structure is adopted and compared with an original oil seal rotating along with the hub the sealing reliability is improved and two oil baffle discs in the original structure are cancelled so that the cost is reduced
