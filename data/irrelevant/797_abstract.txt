A power converter circuit of the invention comprises a primary side circuit a secondary side circuit and a coupling network arranged between the primary side circuit and the secondary side circuit The coupling network comprises a first inductor a second inductor a first capacitor a second capacitor and a third capacitor The first inductor the first capacitor and the second inductor are sequentially connected between a first output end of the primary side circuit and a first input end of the secondary side circuit in series One end of the third capacitor is connected to a second output end of the primary side circuit and the other end of the third capacitor is connected to a second input end of the secondary side circuit One end of the second capacitor is connected between the first inductor and the first capacitor and the other end is connected to the second input end of the secondary side circuit
