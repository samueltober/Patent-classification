The utility model discloses a split type power end device of a plunger pump which comprises a power input shaft a connecting mechanism and several power units the power units are arranged side by side the power input shaft is arranged in a forward and backward direction passing through all Power unit each power unit includes a housing and a transmission mechanism located in the housing the transmission mechanism is used to convert the rotary power of the power input shaft into linear reciprocating power delivered to the piston rod of the plunger pump The connecting mechanism is used to connect the power units together The utility model adopts a split structure the power end assembly is divided into several independent power units to realize a modular design On the one hand the size of the main components is reduced the processing difficulty is reduced and the operation is convenient At the same time different quantities can be changed according to needs The power unit is quickly combined into plunger pump products of different power
