 EPB The purpose of the present invention is to provide a thrust bearing for a vehicle such that the number of components for assembling an air spring used for an air suspension of a vehicle is reduced thereby simplifying the assembling work This thrust bearing  for the vehicle is provided with an upper case  which is attached to the body side of the vehicle which has an air suspension comprising an air spring AS and a lower case  which overlaps with the upper case  in a rotatable manner with respect thereto In a bottom area BA of the lower case  the lower case  is provided with a tube upper end attachment part  to which a rubber tube RT constituting the air spring AS is attached
