 KRA The present invention relates to a wheel bearing with improved tone wheel and tone wheel mounting structure According to one embodiment of the present invention provided is the wheel bearing for rotationally mounting and supporting an automotive wheel on an automotive body The wheel bearing according to one embodiment of the present invention may comprise a rotation element on which the automotive wheel is mounted and rotates with the wheel a nonrotation element mounted on an automotive chassis part and fixed to the automotive body and equal to or more than one rolling elements which are interposed between the rotation element and the nonrotation element to support the rotation element so as to be rotatable with respect to the nonrotation element According to one embodiment of the present invention the rotation element is provided with a wheel mounting flange used to mount the wheel In addition a tone wheel or an encoder used in measuring a rotary speed of the wheel is mounted on the end face of the wheel mounting flange on the automotive body side
