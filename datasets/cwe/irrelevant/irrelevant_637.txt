 CNA The invention discloses a lowgloss powder coating for hub primer and relates to the field of powder coatings In the application the lowgloss powder coating for the wheel hub primer comprises the following components in parts by weight  parts of epoxy resin  parts of polyester resin  parts of a leveling agent  parts of a matting agent  part of benzoin  part of a degassing agent  parts of a pigment and  parts of filler The powder coating is used for solving the problems that the curing temperature of an existing powder coating is generally kept at  DEG C or above embrittlement of the surface of an aluminum hub is caused the impact resistance is reduced and the leveling property of the powder coating is reduced when the powder coating is sprayed on the surface of the aluminum hub through lowtemperature curing
