 CNB The invention belongs to the technical field of suspensions and discloses a suspension twolevel limiting device The suspension twolevel limiting device comprises an integrated support a movable limiting block and an elastic buffer block An oilgas spring fixing portion and a limiting fixing portion are arranged on the integrated support and the movable limiting block is detachably fixed tothe limiting fixing portion The elastic buffer block is fixed to the movable limiting block The device can adapt to lifting a suspension to rigidly support a vehicle body when a special vehicle is in a special scene and achieve elastic limiting in a conventional scene and impact damage of parts is reduced
