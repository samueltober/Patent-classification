 EPA     The invention relates to a spring axle for a road vehicle  with an axle tube  an axle block  firmly connected at the end to the axle tube  and with a suspension  in particular rubber suspension integrated in the axle tube  wherein a rocker arm  connected to the suspension  is rotatably arranged on the axle tube  The rocker arm  has a pivot bearing  that is pulled up and fastened to the axle block The pivot bearing  is preferably arranged and fastened on the outside  of the axle block
