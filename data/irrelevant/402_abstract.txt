The invention discloses a prevention and control method considering the control mode of an IPFC Various control modes of the IPFC are considered and power system power flow calculation including a main control mode and an auxiliary control mode is performed by utilizing the power flow calculation iteration format under each control mode of the IPFC a multiobjective optimization function integrating practicability and safety is constructed and operation constraints of the power system and the IPFC are provided and prevention and control power flow optimization of the power system is realized based on a particle swarm optimization algorithm According to the invention the static control potential of the IPFC can be fully mined the optimization of power flow distribution during steadystate operation of the system is realized and the practicability and the static safety level of the system are significantly improved
