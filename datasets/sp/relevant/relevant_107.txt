 DEA     Support bearing and strut bearing arrangement for a spring strut  of a motor vehicle in which the support bearing  forms a bearing for an upper end of a shock absorber  a lower race  of a strut bearing  is supported on the upper end of a spring  surrounding the shock absorber and an upper race  of the strut bearing  both via a radially extending elastomer track  and is also coupled to a body part via an essentially axially extending elastomer track  the axially extending elastomer track  is arranged between a part of the upper race  and a substantially axially extending peripheral surface of the support bearing  the radially extending elastomer track  between another part of the upper race   and a substantially radially extending surface of the support bearing  and the upper race  carries a plastic ring  on the outer peripheral edge which seals directly or indirectly on the lower race
