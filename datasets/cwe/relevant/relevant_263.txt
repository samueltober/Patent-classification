 WOA A wheel bearing used for rotatably mounting a wheel of a vehicle to a vehicle body is provided according to one embodiment of the present invention A wheel hub according to one embodiment of the present invention comprises a hub flange extending radially outward wherein the hub flange may be configured such that a crosssectional tooth is provided in a cross section located on a wheel side so as to be engaged with a corresponding crosssectional tooth formed on a wheel The crosssectional teeth according to one embodiment of the present invention provided on the hub flange of the wheel hub is configured such that a plurality of teeth are arranged to be spaced apart from each other in a circumferential direction and the teeth of the crosssectional teeth provided on the hub flange of the wheel hub may be configured to have a smaller volume than the teeth of the corresponding crosssectional teeth provided on the wheel
