 WOA The invention relates to a wheel suspension  for a motor vehicle comprising a wheel support  which supports a wheel and is mounted on the motor vehicle body via a lower control arm  and a partially wheelguiding suspension strut  The invention is characterised in that the suspension strut  is mounted on the wheel support  via a first bearing  and a second bearing  and in that in relation to the wheel centre  of the wheel and viewed in the vehicle vertical direction FH the first bearing  is arranged below the wheel centre  and the second bearing  is arranged above the wheel centre
