The invention relates to the field of flexible directcurrent power transmission in particular to a modular multilevel converter power decoupling control method based on disturbance estimation and the method comprises the following steps S designing a secondorder disturbance observer based on a dynamic equation of a bridge arm current differential mode component S estimating disturbance quantity in real time according to a secondorder disturbance observer and S realizing cancellation according to the estimated disturbance quantity so as to control AC side power decoupling of the modular multilevel converter The method does not need an accurate mathematical model is easy to implement good in effect and high in robustness and has high application value
