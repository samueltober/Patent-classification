 KRA According to one embodiment of the present invention provided is a wheel bearing assembly capable of supporting a vehicle wheel by mounting the vehicle wheel on a vehicle body to be rotatable According to one embodiment of the present invention the wheel bearing assembly includes a wheel hub including a vehicle wheel mounting flange at least one inner wheel mounted on the wheel hub an outer wheel including a vehicle bodyside mounting flange and at least one driving body supporting the wheel hub and the inner wheel to the outer wheel to be rotatable According to one embodiment of the present invention a receiving space for receiving a constant velocity joint is formed inside a vehicle bodyside end part of the wheel hub and at least one recess receiving a rotary component of the constant velocity joint is provided in a circumferential direction on the inner circumference surface of the receiving space According to one embodiment of the present invention the inner wheel is formed to be fixed onto the wheel hub by plastically deforming the vehicle bodyside end part of the wheel hub the vehicle bodyside end part of the wheel hub includes an extension part extended to be closer to the vehicle body than the inner wheel before plastic deformation and an undercut extended outward in a radial direction is provided on the inner circumference surface of the vehicle bodyside end part of the wheel hub
