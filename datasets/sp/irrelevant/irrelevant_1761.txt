 CNU The utility model provides an upper mounting seat structure of an automobile front pillar assembly which relates to the technical field of automobile suspension systems and comprises an upper mounting seat framework upper plate an upper mounting seat framework lower plate and a rubber bushing which are fixedly connected together in a detachable mode The rubber bushing is arranged in a towershaped cavity formed by combining the upper mounting seat framework upper plate and the upper mounting seat framework lower plate and the rubber bushing is in interference fit with the towershaped cavity According to the utility model the technical problem of insufficient rigidity of the vehicle body mounting base due to easy abrasion of rubber in the longtime movement process of the vehicle is solved the shock insulation buffer and riding comfort are improved and the bearing force transmission condition of the front pillar when the front pillar is compressed by the lower end of the upper mounting base in the movement process of the vehicle can be optimized
