The present disclosure relates to a bias voltage generator for an Nchannel based linear regulator A device and method for a bias power supply circuit supporting a power supply are provided including a switchmode voltage converter cascaded with an nchannelbased linear regulator In one example the cascaded power supply system may include a switchmode DCDC power converter which includes an input voltage node a firststage output voltage node a bootstrap floating bias voltage node and a linear regulator circuit The linear regulator circuit may include an nchannel field effect transistor NFET pass transistor which includes a drain terminal coupled to a first stage output voltage node a source terminal configured to provide a second stage output voltage and a gate driver The circuit includes a driver output coupled to the gate terminal of the NFET pass transistor and a highside power supply node configured to receive a bias voltage generated from a bootstrap floating bias voltage node
