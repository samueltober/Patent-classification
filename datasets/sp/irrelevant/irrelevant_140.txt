 KRB     A vehicle bump stop device is disclosed A bump stop device for a vehicle according to an embodiment of the present invention includes a bump stopper fixed to a vehicle body a lower arm that is disposed under the bump stopper and is rotatable about a horizontal rotation axis formed at one end and a rotation axis It includes a guide plate installed on the upper surface of the lower arm so as to be movable in a vertical direction and when the lower arm rotates upwardly toward the bump stopper the bump stopper contacts the guide plate and is compressed in the vertical direction
