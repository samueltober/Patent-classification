 CNA The invention provides a wheel swing device which is arranged on a transport vehicle the wheel swing device comprises wheel assemblies fixing frames a cantilever and a first driving piece one end of each fixing frame is connected with the corresponding wheel assembly and the cantilever is hinged to the fixing frames and used for being mounted on the transport vehicle the first driving part is connected to the other end away from the wheel assemblies of each fixing frame the first driving part can drive the fixing frames to swing and the swing fixing frames are used for providing supporting force for the wheel assemblies The invention further relates to the transport vehicle by adopting the wheel swing device swing of the wheel assemblies can be achieved the stability of the wheel swing device is improved and meanwhile the transport vehicle is suitable for some special road conditions
