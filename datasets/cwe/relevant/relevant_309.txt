 CNA The invention discloses a fourthgeneration hub bearing unit and an assembling method thereof The problem that when an existing hub bearing unit is connected with a universal joint the structure iscomplex is mainly solved The fourthgeneration hub bearing unit is characterized in that an inner flange is axially provided with a through hole  a universal joint shell  is further includedand comprises a bellshaped shell  and a shaft rod  the shaft rod stretches into the through hole to be buckled and pressed to the inner wall of the inner flange the bellshaped shell abutsagainst the front end of the inner flange and the bellshaped shell is provided with a rolling path  which is in rolling fit with a ball The fourthgeneration hub bearing unit is provided thehub bearing unit and the universal joint are connected into a whole a half inner ring is omitted the integral structure is compact and the weight is small
