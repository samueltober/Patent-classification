 CNA The invention discloses a robot suspension mechanism and a robot relates to the technical field of Internet of Things and mainly aims to reduce the gravity center of a robot so as to improve the motion performance of the robot The robot suspension mechanism comprises a top plate a floating support and an elastic piece a guide shaft is connected to the top plate a first hole is formed in thetop plate the floating support floats relative to the top plate along the guide shaft a second hole is formed in the floating support and the first hole and the second hole are opposite to form anaccommodating space and the elastic piece is arranged in the accommodating space and is used for applying acting force to the floating support The mechanism is mainly used for improving the motionperformance of the robot
