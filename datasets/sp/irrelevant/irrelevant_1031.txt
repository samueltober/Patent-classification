 CNU The utility model relates to the field of vehicles The utility model discloses a shock absorber and a suspension system The shock absorber comprises an oil storage barrel  a bearing part  anelastic piece  and a vehicle body connecting piece  wherein the oil storage barrel  comprises an oil storage part  and a stopping part  arranged on the periphery of the oil storage part  the bearing part  is installed on the oil storage part  and is positioned through the stopping part  and the elastic piece  is connected between the bearing part  andthe vehicle body connecting piece  According to the shock absorber provided by the utility model the bearing part is sleeved on the oil storage barrel and is positioned through the stopping part so that the fixing strength of the bearing part and the oil storage barrel is increased the probability of strength failure is reduced and the universality of all parts of the shock absorber is increased
