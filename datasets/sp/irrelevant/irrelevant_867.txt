 CNU The utility model discloses a wheel damping running mechanism for an AGV and relates to a wheel damping balance mechanism for the AGV The device comprises a connecting plate connected with a wheel shaft the wheel shaft is sleeved with a wheel the wheel shaft is arranged on a wheel plate and the wheel shaft is connected with a servo motor through a gear motor a second lateral fixing assemblyis arranged at one end of the connecting plate a spring fixing assembly connected through a pin shaft is arranged at the other end of the connecting plate and a first lateral fixing assembly is arranged on the connecting plate close to the pin shaft The utility model achieves the purposes of simple structure and good trafficability and ensures that the wheels are always in contact with the ground
