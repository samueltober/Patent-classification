 CNA     The invention discloses an antiroll bar structure for a double wishbone suspension of a FSAE racing car The rods are vertically distributed with the joints at both ends and a limit mechanism is arranged between the antiroll rod and the joint the connecting rods are vertically distributed with the joint and the position of the connecting rod can be adjusted along the vertical direction of the joint The invention improves the mechanical performance and durability of the existing structure improves the stress concentration phenomenon in the working process and has multiple relative positions adjustable which is beneficial to the use installation and positioning of the antiroll bar The joint is limited and the lateral position of the antiroll bar is determined by the joint sleeve between the frame connecting lugs and the joint By changing the thickness of the left and right joint sleeves the lateral position of the antiroll bar can be changed
