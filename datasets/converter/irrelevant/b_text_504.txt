 CNA The invention belongs to the technical field of control of a plasma excitation power supply and particularly relates to a control method of an extensible controllable plasma excitation power sourceThe method comprises the following steps that the controllable plasma excitation power source includes a controller and a plurality of excitation power sources connected with the controller the plurality of excitation power sources are cascaded one excitation power source is connected with one antenna subarray and each antenna subarray includes a plurality of plasma antennas the plurality of antenna subarrays form an antenna array the controller adjusts an excitation frequency and excitation power of each excitation power source and the excitation power source excites the antennas in theplasma antennas connected with the excitation power source according to the excitation power so that each plasma antenna is dynamically reconstructed and the connected antenna subarrays can work invarious different working modes
