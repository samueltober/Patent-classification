 CNU The utility model provides a suspension system of an electric kart which solves the technical problems that the kart is not provided with a suspension system and is poor in driving comfort on the premise of ensuring the driving controllability The suspension system of the electric kart comprises a pair of front suspension assemblies and a pair of rear suspension assemblies which are symmetrically installed on the two sides of a frame The front wheels are connected with the frame through the two front suspension assemblies respectively and the rear wheels are connected with the frame through the two rear suspension assemblies respectively The front suspension assembly and the rear suspension assembly are both of an unequallength doublecrossarm type suspension structure And the damping assemblies are arranged between the front suspension assembly and the vehicle frame and between the rear suspension assembly and the vehicle frame According to the utility model the driving comfort is improved the lightweight design is adopted the weight is light the size is small competition and entertainment can be better met and the device can also be used for adolescent automobileeducation the ground clearance can be adjusted according to the road surface condition and more field requirements can be met while the control fun of the electric kart is ensured
