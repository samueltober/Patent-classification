 WOA The invention relates to a support bearing of a suspension spring between a structure of a vehicle and a wheelguide element of the vehicle comprising at least two bearing parts   which can be moved relative to one another by means of an actuator at least approximately in the vertical direction of the vehicle and of which a first bearing part is secured to the vehicle structure and the second bearing part is and forms a support for the suspension springThe actuator is formed by an electric motor  having a downstream strain wave gearing  or cycloidal gearing which permits the bearing parts supported on one another in the form of a threaded spindle to rotate relative to one another
