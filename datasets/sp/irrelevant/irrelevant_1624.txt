 CNA The invention discloses a vehicle height adjusting method and device a vehicle and a storage medium The method comprises the steps of obtaining vehicle gear information and door lock state information judging whether the gear information and the door lock state information meet a preset condition or not obtaining current vehicle body height information of the vehicle judging whether the current vehicle body height meets the preset condition or not and controlling the vehicle body height to reach a preset height through the active suspension according to a judgment result According to the method for automatically adjusting the height of the vehicle the height of the vehicle body is automatically controlled when the door is opened when passengers get on or get off the vehicle or a trunk tail door is opened so that the inout convenience of the passengers and the convenience of taking and placing luggage are improved and compared with a traditional mode the method is more intelligent and humanized and the comfort degree and satisfaction degree of the user in the static scene can be improved
