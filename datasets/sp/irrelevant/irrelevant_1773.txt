 CNU The utility model relates to a cavel structure for a vehicle the number of the cavel structures is four each wheel is connected with one cavel structure and each cavel structure comprises a swing arm a connecting arm a big arm an air cylinder connecting rod and a damping system the first end of the swing arm is movably connected with a rotating shaft of one wheel the second end of the swing arm is connected with one end of the connecting arm the other end of the connecting arm is connected with the first end of the large arm and the second end of the large arm is connected with a vehicle body the air cylinder connecting rod is arranged between the large arm and the connecting arm and the damping system is further arranged on the connecting arm The steering knuckle structure is simple in structure and has a good damping effect the steering knuckle structure is composed of a plurality of parts and maintenance is easy and convenient
