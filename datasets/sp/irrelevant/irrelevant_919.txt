 DEA     The invention relates to a device for connecting a shock absorber which has a spring and a hydraulic shock absorber to a body the device comprising a first coupling section for coupling to the body and a second coupling section for coupling to the hydraulic shock absorber of the shock absorber and a third coupling section for coupling to the spring of the shock absorber The device is characterized in that the device includes an adjusting device by means of which the distance of the first coupling section relative to the second and third coupling section can be set and the distance of the second coupling section from the third coupling section remains constant during an adjustment process
