 EPA The invention relates to a roll stabilizer  for a motor vehicle comprising two stabilizer parts   which are connected to each other for rotation relative to each other about a longitudinal axis of the stabilizer  and an actuator  for the relative rotation of the two stabilizer parts   According to the invention the roll stabilizer  comprises at least one first sensor   arranged in a first stabilizer section  of the first stabilizer part  and one second sensor   arranged in a second stabilizer section  of the second stabilizer part  by means of which sensors a state in particular static andor dynamic state of the associated stabilizer section   of the associated stabilizer part   can be sensed
