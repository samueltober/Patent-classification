 CNA     The invention discloses a control method of an antiroll system an activation method of the antiroll system and a vehicle The control method of the antiroll system includes judging whether the vehicle has a tendency to roll if the vehicle has a tendency to roll adjusting the left wheel avoidance The shock absorber damping force orand the shock absorber damping force of the right wheel shock absorber to restore the vehicle to a stable state According to the control method of the antirolling system of the present invention the vehicle speed sensor lateral acceleration sensor steering wheel angle sensor left wheel pressure sensor right wheel pressure sensor and other equipment are used to monitor the vehicle driving state in real time and when it is judged that the vehicle has a tendency to roll over At the same time properly adjust the damping force of the left wheel shock absorber and the right wheel shock absorber to restore the vehicle to a stable state and prevent the vehicle from rolling over
