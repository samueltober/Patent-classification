 CNU     This application relates to an allterrain cable positioning vehicle which includes a vehicle body and also includes multiple sets of walking components installed on the vehicle body and a power source installed on the vehicle body to provide power for the walking components The multiple groups of walking components are divided into two groups and each group is installed on opposite sides of the vehicle body The walking components include a driving device a reducer installed in the vehicle body and connected to the motor and a universal motor connected to the reducer A shaft a connection structure connected between the cardan shaft and the vehicle body and a wheel installed on the end of the cardan shaft away from the reducer The present application has the effect of enabling the positioning vehicle to travel stably on uneven roads
