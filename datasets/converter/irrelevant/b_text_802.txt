 CNA The invention discloses a multiobjective optimization method and optimization system for a regional integrated energy system The method comprises the following steps firstly determining a multidimensional optimization objective of the regional integrated energy system then on the basis of a known regional integrated energy system optimization target constructing a model framework of the regional integrated energy system and carrying out modeling on energy conversion equipment distribution equipment transmission equipment electricity storage equipment and other equipment in the regional integrated energy system meanwhile setting constraint conditions including energy balance constraint energy conversion equipment output constraint tie line transmission power constraint and energy storage constraint And finally solving the established multiobjective optimization model to obtain an iterative optimization process of multiobjective optimization and a final multiobjective optimization solution set According to the method a multiobjective optimization scheme of the regional integrated energy system is provided and the requirement of the regional integrated energy system for coordinating multiple optimization objectives is met
