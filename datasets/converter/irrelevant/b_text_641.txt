 CNA The invention provides a data composite transmission method and device based on a modular multilevel converter The method comprises the steps generating a modulation wave according to a control modeof each converter station and electrical characteristics of a flexible DC power grid wherein the electrical characteristics include a voltage actual value and a current actual value performing frequency modulation on a carrier according to communication data between the converter stations obtaining a trigger pulse according to an amplitude of a modulated wave and the amplitude of the carrier after frequency modulation and sending the communication data in the trigger pulse to a highvoltage directcurrent line According to the invention synchronous transmission of energy and data between modular multilevel converter stations of the flexible DC power transmission system can be realized without increasing communication lines
