 KRA The present invention relates to a suspension thrust bearing device  for use with a suspension spring  in an automotive suspension strut of a vehicle The device comprises a bearing    having upper annular bearing members   and lower annular bearing members   in relative rotation and a damping element made of a resilient material which is interposed between lower annular bearing members   and the suspension spring  The damping element  includes at least one deflecting flange  for reducing any ingress of water and other pollutants between the upper and lower annular bearing members The damping element is provided with an annular groove  that opens axially towards the suspension spring and the annular groove  has a spring support surface  on an inner side and a deflecting flange  on an outer side
