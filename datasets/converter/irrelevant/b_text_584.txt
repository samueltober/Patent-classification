 CNA The invention discloses a receivingend power grid power receiving and power supply margin interval evaluation method under multichannel limit constraint and belongs to the technical field of powersystems and automation thereof The method comprises the steps considering stable limits of a plurality of power receiving channels of a receivingend power grid establishing a linear programming calculation model of the maximum power limit and the minimum power limit of the receivingend power grid obtaining the maximum power limit and the minimum power limit of the receivingend power grid meeting the requirement that all channels do not exceed the limit by solving the linear programming model and calculating the power supply margin interval of the receivingend power grid based on the maximum power limit and the minimum power limit of the power receiving channels By calculating the receivingend power grid power supply margin interval of multipowertransmissionchannel power supply a basis is provided for improving the power supply capacity of the receivingend power grid and guaranteeing safe operation
