 CNA The invention discloses a power generation system and a power generation method for a multisource system railway freight vehicle The power generation system comprises a first power generation device which is an axle end power generator mounted at the end part of an axle and further comprises more than one auxiliary power generation device mounted on a vehicle body and used for generating power and a power supply conversion and management device used for converting and managing the electric power generated by the first power generation device and the auxiliary power generation device and completing power supply output to an application object The power generation method is a method based on the power generation system The system has the advantages of simple structure easy realization continuous and stable output multimode compatibility and the like
