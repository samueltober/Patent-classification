 CNU The utility model belongs to the technical field of automobile hubs and accessories thereof The utility model provides a hub a hub bolt and a bolt cap Each bolt hole is designed into a semicircularfirst end a rectangular middle section and a semicircular second end which are sequentially connected in the radial direction of the wheel the pitch of the bolt holes can be changed within a certain range gaskets matched with the bolt holes are designed the gasket is provided with a through hole for the hub bolt to penetrate through an outer side limiting piece and an inner side limiting piece The positions of the through holes are arranged the positions of the hub bolts are fixed through the outer side limiting pieces and the inner side limiting pieces the bolt hole distance of the hub can be fixed the fixing tightness of the hub bolts is ensured in other words the universality of individual wheels is achieved by replacing different gaskets and the refitting cost is saved
