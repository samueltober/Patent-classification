 CNB The invention discloses a multichannel RS communication network The multichannel RS communication network is used for realizing multichannel RS communication by utilizing limited serial port resources The multichannel RS communication network comprises a DSP digital signal processor a channel switching control circuit RS interface circuits and RS communication terminals and is characterized in that the RS communication terminals are connected with the same serial communication port and after passing through the channel switching control circuit a channelselection signal output by the DSP digital signal processor is used for enabling the RS interface circuits According to the multichannel RS communication network disclosed by the inventionmultichannel RS communication can be realized through a single serial port without programmable devices such as an FPGA and thus the technical problems of relatively high cost relatively high expansion and change cost complex design and inconvenient transplantation in the prior art can be solved
