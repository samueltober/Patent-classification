 CNA The invention discloses a service robot moving chassis based on Mecanum wheels The service robot moving chassis based on the Mecanum wheels comprises a chassis body a vibration reduction system anda driving system wherein the chassis body comprises an upper chassis a lower chassis a supporting column and a supporting column connecting piece the vibration reduction system comprises a vibration reduction device and a parallel mechanism the driving system comprises a motor the Mecanum wheels a transmission shaft a support a flange a shaft sleeve a bearing and Mecanum wheel connecting pieces the vibration reduction device is connected with the upper chassis and the lower chassis the parallel device is connected with the upper chassis and the lower chassis and the driving system is connected with the vibration reduction system through the support By controlling different steering and rotating speeds of four motors omnidirectional movement is achieved
