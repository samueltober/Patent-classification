 CNU The utility model provides an automatic leveling device of a solar cleaning machine The vehicle comprises a vehicle body a chassis an electronic controller and a hydraulic pipeline wherein the vehicle body is connected with the chassis through a universal hinge joint a left leveling oil cylinder and a right leveling oil cylinder a tilt angle sensor is arranged on the vehicle body and the electronic controller receives a signal of the tilt angle sensor and controls the left leveling oil cylinder and the right leveling oil cylinder to act through a hydraulic pipeline so that the postureof the vehicle body is adjusted In the aspect of the structure leveling in any direction in the horizontal plane can be achieved through the universal joint and the two leveling oil cylinders symmetrically arranged on the longitudinal axis of the vehicle and therefore it is guaranteed that the cleaning machine can conduct automatic leveling in the walking process and the cleaning effect of thesolar photovoltaic panel is guaranteed
