 CNA The invention relates to an intelligent hub bearing unit system The system mainly comprises a wheel a shock absorber a brake disc a hub bearing unit a driving shaft a steering knuckle a pull rod and a lower arm The wheel and the brake disc are in locking connection with the hub bearing unit through bolts the hub bearing unit is in locking connection with the steering knuckle through a bolt the hub bearing unit is in locking connection with the driving shaft through a center nut the steering knuckle is in locking connection with the shock absorber through a bolt the steering knuckleis in locking connection with the pull rod through a bolt and the steering knuckle is in locking connection with the lower arm through a bolt therefore a wheel assembly is formed The beneficial effects of the invention are that according to the structure through integration of a plurality of sensors omnibearing monitoring of the speed acceleration temperature load rigidity and the likeof a vehicle wheel end is met so that the running state of the vehicle is recognized control over safety early warning state monitoring chassis power state optimization and the like of the vehicleis achieved the safety of the vehicle is higher and the controllability of the vehicle is better
