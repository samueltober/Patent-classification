 KRA An active suspension pump is disclosed According to one embodiment of the present invention the active suspension pump comprises a motor a pump assembly of which one side is disposed while coming in contact with the motor receiving rotational force from a rotational shaft of the motor to transfer fluid and having a casing and a cover a motorpump assembly fastening member penetrating the pump assembly on the other side of the pump assembly to be coupled to the motor in order to fasten the motor and the pump assembly and a pump assembly fastening member penetrating the cover to be coupled to the casing to fasten the pump assembly without being coupled to the motor
