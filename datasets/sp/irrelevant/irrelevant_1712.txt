 WOA An electromechanical vehicle height adjustment unit  comprises a control arm  a lower spring pad  operative to support a lower end of a vehicle spring  and a displacement mechanism supported on the control arm  At least a portion of the displacement mechanism is integrated into the control arm The displacement mechanism is coupled to the lower spring pad  and is operative to displace the lower spring pad  relative to the control arm  The displacement mechanism comprises a rotarytolinear motion conversion mechanism   and an electric motor
