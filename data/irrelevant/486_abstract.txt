The invention provides a multipurpose intelligent electric fence used for a power supply company Functions of warning drone patrol and the like can be achieved better reference can be provided foranalysis and decision making of a manager the multipurpose intelligent electric fence comprises a conical pile body and a fastening device used for connecting with the ground surface the conical pile body further comprises a pile body locking device connected with the fastening device used for connecting with the ground surface the pile body locking device further comprises a bolt device a limiting device a locking device and a supporting device the bolt device comprises a thread structure fixedly mounted on the conical pile body an inner ring hole of the thread structure is in threaded connection with a telescopic bolt a rotating connector is arranged on the telescopic bolt and the rotating connector is connected with the limiting device through a lifting rod
