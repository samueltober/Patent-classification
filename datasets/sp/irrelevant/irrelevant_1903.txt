 CNU     The present application discloses an air suspension system with multiple airbags and an automobile and relates to the technical field of automobile manufacturing The air suspension system includes a lifting bridge two loadbearing airbags which are arranged at opposite ends of the lifting bridge and a lifting airbag It is centrally arranged above the two loadbearing airbags and communicates with the two loadbearing airbags through switches a lifting mechanism one end of which is connected with the lifting bridge and the other end is connected with the lifting airbag The lifting mechanism drives the lifting bridge to move up or down when the lifting airbag is inflated or deflated The embodiment of the present application can realize gas storage through the communication between the loadbearing airbag and the lifting airbag reduce the requirement on the capacity of the air storage cylinder and facilitate the spatial arrangement of the air suspension
