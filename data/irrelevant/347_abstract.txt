The invention relates to a power converter arrangement  with a modular multilevel converter MMC which comprises a series circuit of switch modules  which each have a plurality of semiconductor switches and at least one stored energy source some of the switch modules being switch modules of a first type  and some other of the switch modules being switch modules of a second type  During operation of the power converter arrangement a positive switch module voltage a negative voltage module voltage or a null voltage can be generated at connection terminals of the switch modules of the first type and a positive switch module voltage or a null voltage can be generated at connection terminals of the switch modules of the second type The invention is characterised in that the power converter arrangement also comprises a support structure  having a number of levels  which each have receptacles  in which the switch modules are arranged with both switch modules of the first and switch modules of the second type being arranged in each level of the support structure
