 CNU The utility model discloses a highstrength locomotive hub The wheel comprises a bearing a rim and spokes wherein the rim is fixed with the bearing through the spokes a reinforcing frame is arranged on an outer ring of the bearing the outer circle of the reinforcing frame is fixed with the spokes the two spokes are matched to form a supporting structure the two spokes the rim and the reinforcing frame form two supporting structures with opposite inner triangles and the reinforcing frame is fixed on the bearing in a cross shape According to the highstrength locomotive hub the two spokes are matched to form the two supporting structures with the opposite inner triangles and the crossshaped reinforcing frame is arranged on the outer ring of the bearing so that the whole hub ishigher in structural strength lighter in weight and more reasonable in structure
