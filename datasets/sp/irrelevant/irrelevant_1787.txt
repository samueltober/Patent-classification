 CNU The utility model relates to the technical field of automobiles in particular to a bearing type new energy automobile chassis platform which comprises an automobile body the automobile body is provided with a longitudinal beam a battery pack is detachably arranged at the middle end of the longitudinal beam and a lowvoltage communication interface a first discharging socket and a second discharging socket are arranged at the front end of the battery pack The quick charging seat is electrically connected with the second discharging socket and the alternating current controller is electrically connected with the first discharging socket according to the utility model the new energy automobile is designed into a bearing type and the battery pack and the automobile body are separately designed so that the new energy automobile is convenient to replace
