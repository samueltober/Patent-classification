The invention discloses a new energy electric vehicle driver control board which uses a ZYNQ SOC fully programmable device as the main control chip and integrates two parts of ARM and FPGA The ARM part is used for advanced motor control algorithms and CAN communication and the FPGA part is used for Realize the closedloop control of the motor including the communication circuit module the processing circuit module and the memory circuit module The communication circuit module is used to isolate the externally connected communication lines the processing circuit module is used to receive the engine control instructions issued by the automobile ECU and perform the underlying control of the motor And generate the driving signal of the motor and the memory circuit module is used to buffer the data of the processing circuit module Through this heterogeneous processing method the complexity of the control board circuit is reduced the bus communication speed of the internal algorithm unit is improved and the cost is reduced
