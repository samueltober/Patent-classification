 CNA     A system includes a first power conversion device and a second power conversion device The first power conversion device and the first power conversion device are configured to be connected between the ac source and the load The second power conversion device includes a power factor correction device The power factor correction device is configured to generate an output voltage that varies in a wide range and the output voltage of the system is mainly adjusted by adjusting the output voltage of the power factor correction device The power flowing from the ac source to the load through the second power conversion device is a fraction of the power flowing from the ac source to the load through the first power conversion device
