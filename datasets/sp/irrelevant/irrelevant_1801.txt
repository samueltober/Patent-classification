 CNU The utility model belongs to ground towing devices for aircrafts and relates to a trailer for the aircrafts in particular to a trailer for carrying an undercarriage Comprising a vehicle body the vehicle body comprises two side beams and a main beam the two side beams are arranged in parallel at intervals the main beam is installed between the ends of the same sides of the two side beams an opening is formed in the area between the other ends of the two side beams opposite to the main beam and the opening is used for containing an aircraft A plurality of hanging rings are installed in the end faces of the same sides of the two side beams rigging and an aircraft are arranged in the hanging rings in a penetrating mode for fixing and a winch and a winch assembly are installed in the main beam
