 CNU The utility model discloses an air spring with an elastic reinforcer The utility model relates to a tire which comprises a leather bag the inner part of the leather bag is provided with a closed leather bag space for storing compressed gas the wall body of the leather bag is formed by integrally vulcanizing inner layer rubber a first layer of cord fabric a second layer of cord fabric and outer layer rubber from inside to outside and cord threads on the first layer of cord fabric and the second layer of cord fabric are arranged in an oblique crossing manner and the elastic reinforcing piece is arranged in the leather bag space and the telescopic direction of the elastic reinforcing piece is the same as that of the leather bag On the basis the utility model further discloses an automobile suspension device The air spring is high in bearing capacity compact in structure small in size and capable of breaking through the limitation of narrow application space
