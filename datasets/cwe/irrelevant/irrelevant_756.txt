 CNU     The utility model relates to an integrated wheel hub bearing unit which comprises a secondgeneration wheel hub bearing unit a mandrel and an end cover One end of the mandrel is provided with a spin riveting edge and the mandrel is connected and fixed with the secondgeneration wheel hub bearing unit through the spin riveting edge  The end cover is pressed into the inner hole of the rim leading end of the secondgeneration hub bearing unit The beneficial effects of the utility model are the structure of the utility model simplifies the installation process only needs to install the hub unit mandrel to the twist beam which greatly simplifies the installation process at the same time the number of parts to be managed by the OEM is divided from four cores Shaft secondgeneration bearings nuts end caps become one reducing the number of parts managed by OEMs reducing quality management and assembly time costs
