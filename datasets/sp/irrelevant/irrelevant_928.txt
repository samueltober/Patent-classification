 CNB The invention discloses an active suspension assembly and a vehicle The active suspension system comprises a detection part and a control controller The detection part is suitable for detecting a front driving road condition to generate and send a driving signal the controller is suitable for receiving the driving signal judging the front road condition according to the driving signal and adjusting the height of a shock absorber of the suspension assembly in advance when the front road surface fluctuates According to the active suspension system provided by the embodiment of the invention the driving road condition namely the road surface information in front is detected through the detection part Thus the active suspension system can sense front road surface information in advance so that the active suspension system can adjust the height of the suspension assembly in real time the response speed of the suspension assembly is increased the retentivity of the active suspension system is reduced and the adjusting effect of the active suspension system is improved
