 CNA A wheel hub bearing  for motor vehicles includes a rotatable hub  provided with a flange  for the engagement of the hub to a wheel of a vehicle and provided with a radially outer free portion  and a bearing unit  provided with a radially outer ring  for the engagement of the wheel hub bearing to a knuckle  of the vehicle and a radially inner rotatable ring  angularly connected to the hub  Furthermore the wheel hub bearing  has a device for detecting a vehicle parameter and provided with an encoder  and a sensor  in communication with the encoder  The encoder  is ridgidly coupled to the radially outer free portion  of the flange  to jointly rotate with the hub
