 CNU The utility model discloses an automobile control arm assembly Swing arm body wherein a mounting hole is formed in the swing arm body a ball pin assembly is mounted in the mounting hole the ball pin assembly comprises a ball pin and a rubber ring A bearing is installed in the rubber ring a narrow channel is formed in the middle of an inner hole of the bearing an annular arcshaped clampinggroove is formed in the inner wall of the narrow channel the ball head portion of the ball pin is arranged in the arcshaped clamping groove in a rotatable mode and the portions between the narrowchannel and end openings in the two ends of the inner hole of the bearing of the inner hole wall of the bearing are conical Therefore the technical effect of simple and reliable structure is achieved
