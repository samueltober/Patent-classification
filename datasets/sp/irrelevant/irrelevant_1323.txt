 KRA Disclosed is an insulator for a suspension device The insulator according to an embodiment of the present invention includes a main plate fixed to a vehicle body a rubber member provided to cover the main plate and having a central hole through which a strut rod passes a cup member that is coupled to the upper side of the central hole of the rubber member and fixes the strut rod a rubber seat member that is coupled to the main plate while being spaced apart from the cup member at the lower side of the central hole of the rubber member and supports bumper rubber and a bearing plate that is coupled to the lower side of the rubber member while being spaced apart from the rubber seat member and supports a strut bearing
