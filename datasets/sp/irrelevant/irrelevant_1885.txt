 CNA     This application relates to the field of transportation tools and in particular to an active suspension device a control method and a mobile chassis of a mobile chassis The active suspension device of the mobile chassis includes a detection device an automatic control device a vertical movement structure the detection device Connect the drive motor and the automatic control device the automatic control device is connected to the vertical movement structure the vertical movement structure is connected between the drive wheel and the plane chassis the detection device is used to detect the current change of the drive motor and determine The terrain where the mobile chassis is located is fed back to the automatic control device the automatic control device outputs corresponding torque according to current changes to control the vertical movement structure to move in the vertical direction the vertical movement structure pushes the driving wheels Perform up and down movements This application can solve the problem of the mobile chassis adapting to complex terrain
