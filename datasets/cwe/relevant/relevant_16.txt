 KRA Disclosed is an axle apparatus for a vehicle The disclosed axle apparatus for a vehicle comprises a drive shaft transmitting the power of an engine a tone wheel part coupled to the drive shaft a wheel bearing part supporting the drive shaft to be rotatable and an antislip part rotating the tone wheel part and the wheel bearing part to be engaged with each other The axle apparatus according to the present invention is capable of preventing a slip generated between a tone wheel and a wheel bearing when the vehicle is driven thereby preventing noise from being generated by the slip and preventing damage to and abrasion in the tone wheel the wheel bearing and peripheral components
