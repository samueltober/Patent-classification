The invention discloses a nanosecond pulse power supply for underwater pulse discharge The nanosecond pulse power supply is powered by AC power The nanosecond pulse power supply includes a switch module a low voltage voltage regulating module a low voltage rectifier module an inverter module and a high voltage connected in sequence Voltage regulating module high voltage rectifier module high voltage energy storage module high voltage discharge control module and current limiting protection module The main components and functional modules of the nanosecond pulse power supply of the present invention adopt a modular design which can be easily replaced and has low manufacturing cost
