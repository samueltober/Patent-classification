The invention discloses a photovoltaic air conditioner a control method and device of the photovoltaic air conditioner a directcurrent power grid and a readable storage medium The photovoltaic airconditioner comprises a grid side converter and a photovoltaic air conditioner control device wherein the photovoltaic air conditioner is used for opening a directcurrent bus to serve as a directcurrent energy source for use by other directcurrent loads and the photovoltaic air conditioner control device is used for limiting the power output of the grid side converter under the power utilization condition of the multidirect current loads According to the invention through power control the gridside power output of the gridside converter is limited in the power utilization conditionof the multidirect current loads so that it can be guaranteed that the grid side converter is output within the rated power
