The invention discloses a distributed electric energy system multibus network topology structure which comprises at least two direct current buses each direct current bus is connected with a power supply input module and each power supply input module is composed of distributed electric energy and a converter power supply group Each direct current bus is connected with a load through an electric energy conversion module and a voltage stabilization module The solar energy is connected with a DCDC module and an MPPT module to form a solar power supply input module and the wind energy is connected with an ACDC module and the MPPT module to form a wind energy power supply input module
