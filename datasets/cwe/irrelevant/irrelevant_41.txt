 CNA The invention relates to an automobile hub unit shell The automobile hub unit shell comprises an inner wheel hub  an outer wheel hub  a guide sleeve  a meshing groove  a meshing plate an inner pressing plate  and an outer pressing plate  wherein a guide sleeve  is arranged between the inner hub  and the outer hub  a circular connecting end plate  is arranged between the guide sleeve  and the inner hub  and the circular connecting end plate  is arranged along an outer side surface of the inner hub  The automobile hub unit shell is advantaged in that through protection of the inner hub and the outer hub automobile parts can stably and safely operate in an automobile hub unit the automobile hub unit can be used more efficiently meshing is conducted through meshing grooves in the meshing plates and the automobile hub unit can be connected with other devices more conveniently
