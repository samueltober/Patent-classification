 KRA Provided is a wheel bearing assembly to reduce manufacturing costs The wheel bearing assembly according to one embodiment of the present disclosure may include an outer race coupled to a suspension unit of a vehicle a wheel hub which rotates relative to the outer race about an axis an inner race coupled to the wheel hub and rotating relative to the outer race a plurality of rolling elements disposed between the outer race and the wheel hub and between the outer race and the inner race a wheel located at the axially outer side of the wheel hub and configured to rotate with the wheel hub by splinecoupling with the wheel hub and a fastening nut fastened to the wheel hub and coupling the wheel to the wheel hub
