 KRA The present invention relates to a ball joint for a vehicle suspension and a method for assembling the same According to the present invention the ball joint for a vehicle suspension comprises a case part a bearing part inserted into the case part a ball stud part which is rotationally inserted into the bearing part and connected to a stabilizer and a dust cover part which is coupled to the ball stud part and inserted between the case part and the bearing part to prevent foreign substances from entering the ball stud part and the bearing part Accordingly by maintaining airtightness it is possible to prevent the inflow of foreign substances and reduce costs
