 DEA The invention relates to a wheel bearing unit for the rotary bearing of a wheel of a vehicle and to a vehicle in particular an electrically drivable vehicle comprising a wheel bearing unit according to the invention A wheel bearing unit  for the rotary bearing of a wheel of a vehicle comprises a speed sensor  in particular an integrated inductive speed sensor   for generating analog signals  with respect to an applied speed and a converter unit  for converting the analog signals  into digital signals  which are in a control unit coupled with the converter unit  for the purpose of outputting the signals corresponding information and  or control of at least one unit of a vehicle equipped with the wheel bearing unit  can be used the invention providing that the converter unit  is arranged in close proximity to the speed sensor  With the wheel bearing unit according to the invention s Like the vehicle equipped with it a speed of a wheel bearing can be measured reliably and precisely
