 EPA A sealing device used for a wheel bearing is provided The sealing device is inserted into the wheel bearing and comprises a first insert body and a second insert body The first insert body has an annular first frame and a first sealing part coupled to the first frame The second insert body has an annular second frame having a diameter smaller than that of the first frame and a second sealing part coupled to the second frame The second insert body rotates relative to the first insert body around a rotation axis The first insert body and the second insert body are arranged such that a gap is formed between the first sealing part and the second sealing part At least one of the first sealing part and the second sealing part comprises a lip having an annular shape based on the rotation axis The lip comprises at least one groove on the surface thereof in the radially outward direction
