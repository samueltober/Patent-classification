 KRA The present invention relates to a suspension thrust bearing device  to be used together with a suspension spring  in a suspension strut of a vehicle The device  comprises a bearing having an upper annular bearing member   and a lower annular bearing member   that rotate relative to each other whereas the lower annular bearing member   has a radial body  The device further comprises a damping element  having a damper radial portion  made of an elastic material and overmolded to the radial body  of the lower annular bearing member The damping element  has a lower support surface  for axially supporting an end coil of the suspension spring  The damper radial portion  has a material having a shore A hardness between  and an axial thickness D between  mm According to the present invention the suspension thrust bearing device has excellent operation stability
