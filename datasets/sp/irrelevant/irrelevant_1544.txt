 CNU The utility model relates to the technical field of main cable maintenance equipment in particular to a walking device and a main cable maintenance platform The walking device comprises a connectingframe a rotating shaft assembly an adapter frame a wheel frame and a walking wheel assembly and the connecting frame is rotationally connected with the adapter frame through the rotating shaft assembly so that the adapter frame can rotate in the horizontal direction relative to the connecting frame the transfer frame is rotatably connected with the at least two wheel carriers so that the wheel carriers can rotate in the vertical direction relative to the transfer frame the wheel carriers are sequentially arranged from front to back and the at least two walking wheel assemblies are installed in the wheel carriers respectively According to the traveling device provided by the utility model the centering adjustment of each traveling wheel assembly on the main cable is convenient to realize so that each traveling wheel assembly can fall in the center of the main cable the problem that the traveling device deviates from a main cable track during traveling is prevented and thestable operation of the traveling device is ensured
