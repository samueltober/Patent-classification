 DEA     The invention relates to an elastic connecting component  with variable stiffness for connecting two components in particular for a roll stabilizer  or an active chassis  of a motor vehicle comprising a cylinder element  and one inside the cylinder element  between a first and second end stop   axially guided guide element  further comprising a compression spring  which is arranged preloaded axially between a first contact surface a on the guide element  and a second contact surface b on an at least partially sleeveshaped piston rod  guided within the cylinder element  and wherein a coupling rod  is axially guided within the piston rod  between a third and fourth end stop   The invention also relates to a method for operating a connecting component a roll stabilizer  and an active chassis  for a motor vehicle with at least one such connecting component
