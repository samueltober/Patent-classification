 CNA The invention belongs to the technical field of vehicle damping devices and particularly relates to an integrated composite suspension actuator and a control method thereof The integrated compositesuspension actuator comprises an actuator body and an actuator control system The integrated composite suspension actuator is characterized in that the actuator body comprises a ballscrew pair assembly and an air spring assembly The invention further discloses an integrated composite suspension actuator and a control method thereof According to the method the height of a vehicle body is controlled to adapt to different driving conditions and meanwhile damping matching and energy recovery are conducted in different vehicle height modes The integrated composite suspension actuator is novel and reasonable in design capable of achieving better vibration reduction under different working condition modes and recycling vibration energy convenient to use wide in use prospect and convenient to apply and popularize
