 CNA     The invention discloses a new type of Mecanum wheel suspension damping mechanism which is composed of a car body mounting plate a suspension assembly and a chassis the mechanism is equipped with a suspension assembly at the front and rear and each suspension assembly It consists of a shock absorber and two Mecanum wheel sets The shock absorber is equipped with a buffer spring and is installed between the body mounting plate and the chassis the present invention is useful for the omnidirectional mobile platform based on the Mecanum wheel  Can realize the use of down pressure in a variety of different environmental conditions can be adjusted according to the load and ground conditions which can reduce the impact of the ground on the running process of the mecanum wheel and avoid the suspension of a certain group of wheels caused by uneven ground Phenomenon it has the advantages of stable operation of the platform strong ability to overcome obstacles and large carrying capacity
