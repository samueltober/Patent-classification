 CNU The utility model discloses a hub of a swing car The hub comprises a hub body a tire mounting part is arranged on the outer surface of the middle part of the hub body a shaft hole is formed in thecenter of one side of the hub body An end cover connecting part is arranged on the other side an iron ring is embedded into the shaft hole wherein an end cover connecting nut and a magnetic ring are arranged at the end cover connecting part the hub body is formed by onetime injection molding of a composite material the iron ring the end cover connecting nut and the magnetic ring are used asinserts to be put into a mold to be combined together during injection molding of the hub body and an inflating valve is arranged on the hub body on the side surface of the tire mounting part Compared with an original aluminum hub the aluminum hub is higher in strength lighter in weight and high in production efficiency and the manufacturing process is beneficial to environmental protection
