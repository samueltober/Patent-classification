The invention discloses a highefficiency photovoltaic inverter capable of sharing operation maintenance and the inverter comprises a detection module a GPS positioning module an inverter circuit module and a wireless communication module The data collected form the detection module and the GPS positioning module is transmitted to an operation and maintenance sharing platform through the wireless transmission module and the parameters of all elements or lines in an inverter circuit are also transmitted to the operation and maintenance sharing platform through the wireless transmission module The invention provides the highefficiency photovoltaic inverter and the inverter can be managed in a unified manner through a network thereby enabling the operation and maintenance to be moreconvenient reducing the operation and maintenance cost and improving the practicality
