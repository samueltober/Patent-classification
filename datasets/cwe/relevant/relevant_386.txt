 CNA The invention discloses a semifloating type drive axle hub end assembly which comprises a brake a brake drum a half shaft a hub bearing and an axle shell flange the brake is positioned between the hub bearing and the axle shell flange the brake is detachably fixed on the axle shell flange the brake drum is detachably fixed to the half shaft the brake and the brake drum rotate relativelythe half shaft is sleeved with the hub bearing in an interference fit mode an inner ring of the hub bearing is detachably and fixedly connected with the half shaft and an outer ring flange of the hub bearing is detachably and fixedly connected with the axle shell flange The semifloating type drive axle hub end assembly is high in integration degree and high in bearing capacity and sealing functionality the service life of a bearing is prolonged and then the user satisfaction degree is improved
