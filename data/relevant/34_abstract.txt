The invention relates to a high voltage DC power flow controller comprising two sets of modular multilevel converters The two sets of modular multilevel converters are connected in series and one end is accessed to the positive electrode and the negative electrode of a DC power grid and the other end is interconnected and grounded so as to form a neutral point According to the technical scheme an external power supply does not need to be connected so that the structure is simple the fault processing capacity is high and the operation efficiency is high
