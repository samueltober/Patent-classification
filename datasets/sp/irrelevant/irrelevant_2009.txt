 CNA     The invention provides an active suspension control system and method and relates to the technical field of automobiles The active suspension control method includes an information collection vehicle a current driving vehicle and a cloud platform wherein the information collection vehicle is used to collect first road smoothness information and send the first road smoothness information to the Cloud platform the cloud platform is used to receive the first road smoothness information sent by the information collection vehicle and send the first road smoothness information to the currently driving vehicle the current driving vehicle is receiving all After the first road smoothness information sent by the cloud platform the target damping force for controlling the active suspension system is determined according to the first road smoothness information The embodiment of the present invention controls the active suspension system through the road smoothness information obtained from the cloud platform which effectively solves the problem of control lag and ensures the driveability and comfort of the vehicle
