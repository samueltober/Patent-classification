 CNB     The present invention relates to a ball joint  especially for the chassis of a vehicle which comprises a hinged housing  and a hinged body  with a spherical part  wherein the hinged body  is in its spherical shape The part  is accommodated by the hinged housing  so as to be supported in a hinged motion relative to the hinged housing and the ball joint includes a sensor assembly which has a first assigned to the hinged housing  The sensor element  and the second sensor element  assigned to the hinged body  the second sensor element  and the first sensor element  work together to detect the hinged body  relative to the hinge The position of the housing  is characterized in that the second sensor element  is arranged in the area of the spherical part  of the hinged body
