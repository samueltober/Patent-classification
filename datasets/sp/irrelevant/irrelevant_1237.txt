 CNU The utility model discloses a damping device for a walking chassis of agricultural equipment The chassis comprises an upper chassis and a lower chassis which are mounted at the bottom of agriculturalequipment at least one upper groove and at least one lower groove are formed in the opposite faces of the upper base plate and the lower base plate A fixed base is arranged in the lower groove a pair of gears meshed with each other is arranged on the fixed base a first rotating arm is fixedly arranged on the gear a second rotating arm is arranged at the other end of the first rotating arm abearing block is arranged at the other end of the second rotating arm a spring is arranged between the bearing block and the inner wall of the upper groove a sliding groove is formed in the upper groove a sliding block is arranged on the upper portion of the bearing block and a third rotating arm is arranged on the upper portion of the fixed base and hinged to the hinge point of the second rotating arm and the bearing block According to the damping device for the walking chassis of the agricultural equipment through the four damping mechanisms buffering can be effectively conducted the weight in four directions can be balanced and excessive inclination is avoided
