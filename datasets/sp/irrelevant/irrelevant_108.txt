 KRB     A geometry variable active suspension for a vehicle is disclosed The geometry variable active suspension is provided at the rear end of each of the torsion beams arranged in the vehicle width direction both trailing arms arranged in the longitudinal direction of the vehicle body at both ends of the torsion beam and coupled with the knuckle of the wheels It includes a knuckle connection portion in which the camber angle is controlled by the camber angle adjustment unit The camber angle adjustment unit includes a rotating part having one end rotatably coupled to the rear end of the trailing arm and the other end fixed to the inner side of the knuckle connection part and an actuator rotating the rotating part
