 WOA This method of acquiring a contact angle of an angular contact ball bearing comprises a first detection step for detecting the rotational speed of one raceway ring  of an angular contact ball bearing  a second detection step for detecting from the outside the deformation of the other raceway ring  according to the revolution of a ball  of the angular contact ball bearing  and a calculation step for obtaining a contact angle  of the ball  by using the detection result of the first detection step the detection result of the second detection step and specification data of the ball
