 CNU The utility model discloses an automobile brake structure which is simple in structure and easy to achieve a brake disc is arranged on an inner half axle the weight of an outer half axle is reducedand wheels on the outer half axle can steer conveniently The first mounting seat is provided with a first bearing for mounting the inner half shaft so that the first mounting seat and the inner half shaft can synchronously move up and down and the first mounting seat is connected with the main bracket through a first swing frame so that the first mounting seat cannot rotate along with the inner half shaft and the brake disc when moving up and down
