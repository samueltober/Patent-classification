 CNU The utility model discloses a hub unit which is rotationally installed on a steering knuckle or a vehicle axle tube and comprises a hub body a first bearing chamber and a second bearing chamber are arranged in the hub body an outer bearing is installed in the first bearing chamber and an inner bearing is installed in the second bearing chamber The inner ring of the outer bearing is longer thanthe outer ring of the outer bearing the inner ring of the inner bearing is longer than the outer ring of the inner bearing the end face of the inner ring of the outer bearing abuts against the endface of the inner ring of the inner bearing and a fixing piece used for fixedly connecting the inner ring of the outer bearing with the inner ring of the inner bearing is installed in the hub body Compared with a traditional hub unit the hub unit is compact in structure light in weight convenient to maintain and long in service life
