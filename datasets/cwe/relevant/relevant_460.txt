 KRA According to an embodiment of the present invention provided is a wheel bearing assembly for rotationally mounting and supporting a vehicle wheel with respect to a vehicle body The wheel bearing assembly according to an embodiment of the present invention can include a wheel hub having a hub flange for mounting a wheel one or more inner races that are pressfitted to the wheel hub and mounted an outer race mounted and fixed to a chassis member of the vehicle and one or more rolling elements that rotationally support the wheel hub and the inner race with respect to the outer race According to an embodiment of the present invention a constant velocity accommodation groove capable of accommodating a rolling element of a constant velocity joint can be provided at a vehicle body side end of the wheel hub and the wheelside axial section of the hub flange on which the wheel is mounted can have a surface runout characteristic of a predetermined level while the constant velocity joint is inserted and mounted in the constant velocity joint accommodation groove
