 DEA     The invention relates to a vehicle damper comprising a protective tube  surrounding an axially movable damper tube  with a piston rod  with a head bearing  for attachment to a vehicle body and a foot bearing  for attachment to a wheel suspension In the case of a vehicle damper which can be used as a standing rearaxle damper and requires little installation space despite the wide tires and wide interior of the vehicle the damper tube  is designed as a singletube damper with a piston rod  underneath and at the upper end  of the Damper tube  a standing head bearing  arranged The damper tube  and a stop profile  formed below the head bearing  can be arranged in an axle area of the vehicle
