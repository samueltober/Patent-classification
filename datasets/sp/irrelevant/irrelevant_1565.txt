 CNA The invention relates to the field of robots and in particular relates to a multilegged intelligent obstacle crossing robot The robot comprises a main frame and obstacle crossing feet each obstacle crossing foot comprises a supporting frame electric push rods I and universal wheels the front end and the rear end of the lower end of each supporting frame are each fixedly connected with one electric push rod I the movable end of each electric push rod I is provided with one universal wheel the two obstacle crossing feet are arranged in a mirror symmetry mode and the two supporting frames are fixedly connected with the left end and the right end of the main frame respectively Driving wheels and driven wheels can be independently adjusted and are suitable for obstacle crossing of complex terrains
