The utility model relates to the technical field of lamp control and discloses a single lamp controller and a lighting system to realize remote control and improve control efficiency The singlelamp controller of the utility model includes a relay module arranged between the V mains input and the power supply module of the controlled lamp the relay module is connected with the singlechip microcomputer the singlechip connected with the host computer is used for  communication based on  bus communication Module a first power conversion module used to convert V mains input into V a second power conversion module cascaded with the first power conversion module to convert V to V the second power conversion module The power supply terminals are respectively connected to the power supply pins of the singlechip microcomputer and the  communication module the power supply terminals of the first power conversion module are also connected to the power supply pins of the internal control chip of the relay module
