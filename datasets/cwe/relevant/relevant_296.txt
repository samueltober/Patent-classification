 CNA The invention discloses a bub bearing unit sealing part The bub bearing unit sealing part comprises a first framework and a second framework A rubber sealing body is arranged on the first frameworkand a lip is arranged on the rubber sealing body The lip and the second framework cooperate to form a water storage space and the water storage space communicates the outside through a labyrinth sealing channel The water storage space is mainly arranged for accumulating water or impurities entering from the outside the external water or impurities are prevented from directly entering a hub bearing and the water storage space achieves a buffering function The water storage space communicates with the outside through the labyrinth sealing channel and the external water or impurities cannot enter the water storage space easily In the rotating process of the hub bearing the water or impurities located in the water storage space are discharged along the labyrinth sealing channel underthe action of centrifugal force A water storage trough is mainly arranged for storing the water or impurities and the water or impurities are prevented from entering the labyrinth sealing channel to optimize the sealing performance of a sealing part
