 KRA The present invention relates to a drive axle for a vehicle having a deformation prevention function in particular to a drive axle for a vehicle which prevents deformation of a constant velocity joint groove by forming a deformation prevention groove on one side of an axle hub In the drive axle for a vehicle including an axle housing a drive shaft and a wheel bearing the deformation prevention groove is formed at one side of a groove formed to receive a ball of the drive shaft coupled to the inside of the axle housing
