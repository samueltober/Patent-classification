The invention discloses a modular combined power supply and an electric welding machine The modular combined power supply includes a power frequency AC input rectifier circuit a DC filter circuit an overcurrent detection circuit an inverter conversion circuit and a The voltage isolation circuit the secondary rectification filter circuit and the output current detection circuit the control unit is connected with the inverter conversion circuit through an oscillation driving circuit circuit the control unit and the secondary rectification filter circuit are connected through an output voltage detection circuit circuit The invention adopts digital control technology to greatly reduce the dependence on the accuracy of hardware parameters and ensure the consistency of output under the condition of large fluctuations in the network voltage Through the software and hardware multiple compensation of the devices on the sampling and control loop multidimensional selfadjustment is ensured The output is consistent and regular verification is performed to correct the deviation caused by the aging of the components
