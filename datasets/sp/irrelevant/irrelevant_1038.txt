 KRA The present invention relates to a suspension thrust bearing device  which is used with a suspension spring  in a suspension strut of a vehicle The suspension thrust bearing device  includes a bearing having an upper annular bearing member   and a lower annular bearing member   performing a relative rotational motion and a damping element  which is inserted between the suspension spring  and the lower annular bearing member  and is made of an elastic material The lower annular bearing member  includes a flange  extended radially outward The flange  is embedded within the damping element
