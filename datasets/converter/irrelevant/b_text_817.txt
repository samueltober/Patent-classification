 CNA The invention provides an online input circuit an input method and an input device for a hybrid direct current converter valve group The online input circuit for the hybrid DC converter valve group comprises a first valve group switch a first bus switch a first bypass switch a second valve group switch a second bus switch and a second bypass switch wherein the first valve group switch is used for connecting a power grid commutation converter and a voltage source type valve group unit the first bus switch is used for connecting the power grid commutation converter with a direct current bus or a neutral bus the first bypass switch is connected in parallel with a series circuit of the first valve group switch and the power grid commutation converter the second valve group switch is used for connecting the voltage source converter and the current source type valve group unit the second bus switch is used for connecting the voltage source type current converter and the direct current bus or the neutral bus and a series circuit of the voltage source converter and the second valve group switch or a series circuit of the voltage source converter and the second bus switch is connected in parallel with the second bypass switch
