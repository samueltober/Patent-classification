 CNA The invention discloses a combined suspension device The device comprises a metal plate spring at least one composite material plate spring arranged below the metal plate spring a rigid support and an elastic bearing block arranged on the rigid support the rigid support is arranged in the middle of the metal plate spring when the vehicle is empty a gap is formed between the elastic bearing block and the vehicle frame and the elastic bearing block can abut against the vehicle frame along with the increase of the load on the vehicle frame or the rigid support is arranged on the vehicle frame a gap is formed between the elastic bearing block and the metal plate spring when the vehicle is empty and the elastic bearing block can abut against the middle of the metal plate spring along with the increase of the load on the vehicle frame The device is simple and reliable in structure light weight and bearing capacity are both considered high comfort is achieved when the vehicle is empty and good bearing capacity is achieved when the vehicle is loaded
