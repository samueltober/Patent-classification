 CNU The highwearresistance damping support for semitrailer suspension comprises an air suspension system installed at the lower end of a longitudinal beam at the bottom of a semitrailer the air suspension system comprises an installation frame installed at the lower end of the longitudinal beam the interior of the installation frame is connected with a support through a support damping device andan air bag is installed at the end away from the installation frame of the support The upper end of the air bag is fixed to the lower end of the longitudinal beam According to the highwearresistance damping support for suspension of the semitrailer the support damping device is arranged contact between the support and the installation frame can be avoided through the springs in the support damping device the direct connection position of the support and the installation frame is prevented from being abraded and meanwhile the connection position is prevented from being broken due todirect stress
