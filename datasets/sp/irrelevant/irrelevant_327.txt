 CNB The invention relates to a vehicle lateral wind estimation method and device and a vehicle Lateral wind can be estimated accurately The method comprises the steps of establishing N lateral wind estimation models corresponding to N lateral wind parameters according to the set N lateral wind parameters wherein N is a positive integer inputting the wheel state parameters of the vehicle into theN lateral wind estimation models to obtain N response values the wheel state parameters being used for representing the motion condition of wheels of the vehicle determining N identification errorscorresponding to the N lateral wind estimation models according to the N response values and an actual response value output by the vehicle based on actual lateral wind and determining a lateral windestimation value from the N lateral wind parameters according to the N identification errors
