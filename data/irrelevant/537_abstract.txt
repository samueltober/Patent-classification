The invention discloses a method for optimizing the output voltage waveform quality of a cascaded power quality control device The method specifically comprises the following steps outputting a voltage waveform quality optimization threshold through a preset device comparing the obtained current operation information of the device with the preset threshold value wherein the current operation information of the device comprises a device output current effective value a device output voltage harmonic content a cascade unit DC bus voltage a cascade unit filtered DC bus voltage a cascade unit DCDC converter current a cascade unit filtered energy storage system terminal voltage and energy storage system control signal flag bit information and calculating and generating a device cascade unit DCDC converter control signal according to a comparison result Thus the working state of the device cascade unit DC side energy storage system is adjusted the device cascade unit DC side voltage ripple is reduced and the waveform quality of the device output voltage is improved
