 CNA The invention discloses a charge pump circuit an ASIC chip and intelligent electronic equipment the charge pump circuit has a compensation working mode and a normal working mode and the circuit comprises a reference voltage generator connected with a power supply input terminal and used for converting an accessed power supply voltage into a reference voltage and outputting the reference voltage N stages of boosted circuits in cascade connection with the output terminal of the reference voltage generator and used for boosting a voltage value output by the reference voltage generator in a normal working mode so as to generate a driving voltage and a voltage reset circuit connected with the power supply input terminal and used for detecting the voltage output by the power supply and controlling the reference voltage generator and the N stages of boosted circuits to work in a compensation working mode when the detected power supply voltage is smaller than a first preset voltage value so as to generate a driving compensation voltage According to the invention the problems of low response speed and unstable initial value of the sensor are solved
