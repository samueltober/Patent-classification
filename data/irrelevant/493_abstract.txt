The invention discloses a threeterminal DC power transmission engineering fault power coordination control method and system The method aims at a twotoone operation mode or a onetotwo operationmode during threeterminal operation of a threeterminal directcurrent power transmission project A directcurrent power transfer method under the condition of singlepole and doublepole fault outage of a sendingend or receivingend converter station is provided reasonable interpole transfer and interstation distribution are carried out on directcurrent power and the stability of power transmission and the stability of a multiend directcurrent power transmission system are kept
