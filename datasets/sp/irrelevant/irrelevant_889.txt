 CNU The utility model relates to a highstrength lightweight steering knuckle The steering knuckle comprises a steering knuckle body and a control arm mounting part the control arm mounting part is connected with the steering knuckle main body through a transition section the area of the longitudinal section of the transition section is gradually reduced from one end close to the steering knucklemain body to one end of the control arm mounting part a first weight reduction groove extending from one end of the transition section to the other end of the transition section is formed in the upper surface of the transition section and the width of the first weight reduction groove is gradually reduced from the end close to the knuckle body to one end of the control arm installation part Thesteering knuckle is remarkable in light weight effect and high in strength
