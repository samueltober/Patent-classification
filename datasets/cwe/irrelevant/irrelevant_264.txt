 CNU The utility model discloses a vehicle and an axle thereof the axle of the vehicle comprises a housing a cavity is formed in the housing the transmission shaft comprises a shaft body and a drivingbevel gear the driving bevel gear is located at the axial end of the shaft body and at least part of the transmission shaft and the driving bevel gear are arranged in the cavity and the doublerowangular ball bearing is arranged in the cavity and is arranged on the shaft body in a sleeving manner Therefore by arranging the doublerow angular ball bearings the effects of supporting the axleand bearing the axial force are achieved the step of adjusting the clearance of a traditional tapered roller bearing is avoided accordingly the labor hours for production and maintenance are reduced and the assembly efficiency is improved
