 DEA     The invention relates to a device for connecting a shock absorber which has a spring and a hydraulic shock absorber to a body the device in a fastening plane a first coupling section for coupling to the body and a second coupling section for coupling to the hydraulic shock absorber of the shock absorber and a third coupling section for coupling to the spring of the shock absorberThe device is characterized in that the device includes an actuating device by means of which the distance of the first coupling section relative to the second coupling section and  or the third coupling section can be adjusted the actuating device being arranged at least partially above the fastening plane while the second coupling section and  or the third coupling section are arranged below the fastening plane
