 CNU The utility model relates to the field of automated guided vehicles in particular to a driving damping device The driving device is characterized by comprising a driving unit which comprises a master driving unit and a slave driving unit the damping unit specifically comprises a plurality of springs and the springs are in onetoone correspondence with the guide rods and penetrate through theguide rods to be arranged on the wheel carrier the driving frame is arranged between the fixing plate and the wheel frame a plurality of first guide through holes matched with the guide rods are formed in the driving frame and the guide rods penetrate through the first guide through holes from the support to the fixing plate and the bearing plate is arranged below the driving frame and is fixedly connected with the driving frame The technical scheme has the beneficial effects that the influence of the bumpy environment on the automated guided vehicle is reduced while the automated guidedvehicle is driven to move forwards
