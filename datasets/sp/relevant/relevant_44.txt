 FRA     A strut  comprises a telescopic damper  a coil spring  and a suspension stop  The rotation of the abutment  is ensured by a sliding or rolling bearing  comprising an upper washer  and a lower washer  the lower washer  forming a lower flat bearing annular bearing surface  for the telescopic damper  extending radially inwardly relative to the upper washer The abutment comprises a onepiece cup   having an upper face  having a contact zone  with the lower bearing washer  and a lower face  having an outer plane annular shoulder   and an at least partially cylindrical annular bearing surface  facing radially outwardly defining with the outer shoulder  a peripheral annular support zone  for the upper end of the coil spring  The upper face  of the onepiece cup  has reinforcing ribs  located radially outside the contact zone
