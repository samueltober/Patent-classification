A lighting system includes a power supply circuit an ACDC converter circuit and a wireless transmission module The wireless transmission module receives an outside command and generates an adjustment command according to the thus received outside command The wireless transmission module transmits the adjustment command to the power supply circuit via a transmission interface The adjustment command includes a luminance adjustment command and a driving power control command The power supply circuit includes a power stage and a conversion controller circuit The conversion controller circuit supplies a programmable output voltage to the wireless transmission module wherein the programmable output voltage is adopted to power the wireless transmission module The conversion controller circuit controls the power stage according to the luminance adjustment command to adjust an output current of an output power thereby adjusting luminance of a light emission device And the conversion controller circuit controls the programmable output voltage according to the driving power control command to regulate the programmable output voltage to a high voltage level or a low voltage level
