 EPB The invention relates to a singlewheel suspension arrangement of a twotrack vehicle having a lower leaf spring element  which is formed from a fiber composite material and which together with a damper  arranged in the manner of a damper strut performs the wheel control of the vehicle wheel  and which is connected at one side to a wheel support  and at the other side to an axle support or directly to the vehicle structure  Here the lower leaf spring element  is oriented at least approximately in a vehicle transverse direction and has at the vehicle body side a support base measured in the vehicle longitudinal direction the length of which support base corresponds to at least  times the length measured in the vehicle transverse direction of the lower leaf spring element  and wherein the lower leaf spring element  is arranged below the wheel axis of rotation as viewed in a vehicle vertical direction
