 CNU The utility model belongs to the technical field of riding chassis parts and particularly relates to a novel hub bearing assembly The hub bearing assembly comprises a connecting bolt a hub bolt a brake disc a hub bearing flange an outer side sealing ring an outer side retainer an outer side steel ball an inner side steel ball an inner side retainer a hub bearing outer ring a hub bearing inner ring and an inner side sealing ring The brake disc and the hub bearing are integrated into a whole and the hub bearing flange is provided with a flanging structure The novel hub bearing assembly is simple in structure the hub bearing is integrated with the brake disc to form the assembly a cast iron structure at the position of a mounting cap of the brake disc is replaced with a highstrength and highrigidity bearing steel structure the structural rigidity and strength reliability of a brake system are effectively improved and the defects of an existing hub bearing are overcome
