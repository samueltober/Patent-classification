 CNA The invention relates to a stabilizer assembly for a front wheel set or a rear wheel set of a motor vehicle equipped with at least an additional electric drive in which coupling elements  are provided for each wheel of the wheel set which coupling elements are connected to each other by means of an intermediate piece  characterized in that the intermediate piece  is fastened to a receiving container  of a battery wherein electrical energy for driving at least an additional electric drive of the motor vehicle can be stored in the battery
