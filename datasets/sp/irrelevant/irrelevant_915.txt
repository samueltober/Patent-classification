 CNU The utility model discloses a mobile chassis and a robot The movable chassis comprises a shell and at least two driving units Each of the at least two driving units comprises a swing rod a shock absorber a driving mechanism and a driving wheel the upper end of the shock absorber is hinged to the shell the lower end of the shock absorber is hinged to the first end of the swing rod the driving mechanism is fixed to the first end of the swing rod the output end of the driving mechanism is connected with a wheel shaft included in the driving wheel and the second end of the swing rod is hinged to the shell The first end and the second end of the swing rod and the upper end of the shock absorber are arranged in a triangular shape The technical problem that in the prior art the twoway advancing performance of a movable chassis is poor is solved
