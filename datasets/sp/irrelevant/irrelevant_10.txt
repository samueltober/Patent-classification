 EPB The invention relates to a steering unit in particular for commercial vehicles comprising an arm and a support element The support element has a support region and a supportside engagement region and is designed as a single piece and the arm has an armside engagement region A first engaging means is provided on the armside engagement region said engaging means being attachable to a second engaging means provided on the supportside engagement region and the support region has a securing portion for fixing an air spring bellows and a rolling surface
