 CNU The utility model discloses a safe automobile hub structure relates to the technical field of hub structures and aims at solving the problems that when an existing automobile hub is used a center exposed gap is large garbage and dirt or small animals are prone to being drilled in and safety is not facilitated An inner steel ring block is installed in the automobile hub a fixing hole is formed in the inner steel ring block a reinforcing rib block is installed in the middle of the inner portion of the inner steel ring block a center hub fixing bearing seat is installed at one end of thereinforcing rib block a supporting connecting rod is installed on one side of the reinforcing rib block and a center hub fixing bearing seat is installed at the other end of the center hub fixing bearing seat A ventilation groove is formed in the supporting connecting rod and a protective safety cover is installed in the center hub fixing bearing seat
