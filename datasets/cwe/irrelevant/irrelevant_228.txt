 CNU The utility model discloses an automobile balance bearing hub and belongs to the technical field of automobile accessories The utility model discloses an automobile balance bearing hub The device comprises an upper bearing hub and a lower bearing hub upper mounting plates are fixedly connected to the two sides of the upper bearing hub correspondingly lower mounting plates are fixedly connected to the two sides of the lower bearing hub correspondingly a plate spring seat is arranged at the bottom of the lower bearing hub a hole is formed in the top of the plate spring seat and a threaded cylinder is fixedly connected to the inner wall of the hole the utility model is advantageous in that the structure is simple an upper mounting plate a lower mounting plate a screw rod and a threaded cylinder are arranged the original integrated design is changed during installation only the guide strip on the lower bearing hub needs to be in butt joint with the guide groove in the platespring seat then the upper bearing hub the lower bearing hub and the plate spring seat are fixed under the action of the screw and the threaded cylinder the device achieves the effect of being convenient to disassemble and assemble and the situation that an internal bearing is damaged and consequently replacement is inconvenient for workers is avoided
