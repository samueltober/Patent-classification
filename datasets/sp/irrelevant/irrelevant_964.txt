 CNU The utility model discloses a composite air suspension for a light load vehicle The vehicle comprises a vehicle frame a vehicle axle and a plurality of bearing air bags arranged between the vehicleframe and the vehicle axle Characterized in that it is characterized in that it comprises a balance beam is arranged on the axle the balance beam is arranged to be arcshaped The balance beam comprises a fixing part and installation parts arranged at the two ends of the fixing part One end of the bearing air bag is fixed on the frame plate spring assemblies are arranged on the two sides of the frame each plate spring assembly comprises a plate spring body and spring eyes arranged at the two ends of the plate spring body elastic body shaft sleeves are arranged in the spring eyes compared with a traditional air suspension structures such as a thrust rod and a connecting support are omitted the structure is simple the damping performance is good and maintenance is easy
