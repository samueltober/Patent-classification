The invention belongs to the technical field of fruit and vegetable machines and in particular relates to a fruit and vegetable machine control circuit a fruit and vegetable machine and a fruit and vegetable machine control method The fruit and vegetable machine control circuit includes a power supply module for converting an input power supply into a preset power supply a signal conversion and control module  Connected to the power supply module a signal conversion and control module for converting a user preset adjustment signal into a control signal an action execution module connected to the signal conversion and control module and connected to the second power conversion unit The action execution module is used to switch the load according to the control signal The advantage of the control circuit of the fruit and vegetable machine is that it can perform stable power output and multilevel power output at the same time
