 CNA The invention discloses a suspensionsteering combined mechanism with a bidirectional buffering function and belongs to the field of mechanical design and manufacturing The suspensionsteering combined mechanism comprises a gear train chassis a sleeve a piston a stop mechanism a cross beam connecting piece a steering claw a sealing ring an upper spring a lower spring and bolts With application of the suspensionsteering combined mechanism the hidden danger that the wheel train is impacted by falling of the robot body and falls onto the wall surface in the posture changing process of the wallclimbing robot can be avoided the wheel train provided with the suspensionsteering combined mechanism has independent steering capacity and the wallclimbing robot can safely and flexiblywork on the curved wall surface
