 CNU The wheel assembly comprises a wheel a rolling bearing and swing control pieces the wheel is provided with an inner hole the outer ring of the rolling bearing is relatively and fixedly connected tothe inner hole of the wheel the swing control pieces are oppositely arranged on the opposite sides of the inner ring of the rolling bearing and a mounting space is formed between the two swing control pieces Corresponding connecting pieces can be connected in the installation space according to needs the design is scientific and reasonable the structure is simple the wheel has the deflection function under the condition that the center of gravity is not increased reversing and direction changing can be conducted conveniently and the center axis of the swing control piece is arranged on the front side of the center axis of the rolling bearing In the walking process the wheels can be effectively prevented from deflecting freely and stable walking of the wheels is guaranteed
