 CNU The utility model discloses a novel rear axle halfshaft structure and belongs to the technical field of transmission systems The novel rear axle half shaft structure comprises a bearing a rear axlepipe a transmission shaft and a shaft sleeve and the bearing is arranged on the transmission shaft in a sleeving mode abuts against a shaft shoulder on the transmission shaft and is connected withthe transmission shaft in an interference fit mode the rear axle tube is sleeved on the bearing the shaft sleeve is sleeved on the transmission shaft abuts against one side away from the shaft shoulder of the bearing and is in interference fit connection with the transmission shaft The stability of the rear axle half shaft structure can be guaranteed and therefore the operation stability of the electric mowing vehicle and the electric tricycle is improved
