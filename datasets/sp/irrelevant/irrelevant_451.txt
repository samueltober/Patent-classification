 CNU The utility model relates to an automobile swing arm Wherein the hollow swing arm body is formed by welding two sheet metal parts the brake claw is assembled on the hollow swing arm body and the hub shaft is assembled on the brake claw The hollow swing arm body comprises a first hollow arm a second hollow arm and a third hollow arm wherein the brake claw is provided with a shaft part matchedwith the end part of the first hollow arm the shaft part is fixedly connected with the first hollow arm in a welding manner the end part of the second hollow arm is fixedly connected with a first bushing mounting seat and the end part of the third hollow arm is fixedly connected with a second bushing mounting seat According to the utility model the processing cost and the maintenance cost ofthe automobile swing arm are reduced and the automobile swing arm has the advantages of compact structure and high integration level
