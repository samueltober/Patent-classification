A modular power conversion device includes at least one first type energy storage device ESD configured to induce a first direct current DC voltage and at least one active power link module coupled to the at least one first type ESD  APLM string The at least one APLM string includes a plurality of APLMs coupled to each other Each APLM of the plurality of APLMs has a plurality of switching devices including a first switching device and a second switching device electrically coupled to each other in series Each APLM of the plurality of APLMs also has at least one second type ESD coupled electrically in parallel with both the first switching device and the second switching device The at least one second type ESD is configured to induce a second DC voltage
