The invention discloses a power electronic transformer based on a square wave converter The power electronic transformer comprises an Aphase circuit a Bphase circuit and a Cphase circuit and each phase circuit comprises an alternating current filter inductor and X modules and is used for converting highvoltage alternating current into lowvoltage direct current Each module comprises Y cascaded Hbridge modules and a squarewave converter and each squarewave converter comprises Y highvoltage modules a common magnetic core type transformer and Y lowvoltage modules The modular degree is high automatic production is easy the reliability of the system can be improved meanwhile when the module breaks down the redundancy of the system is high high control flexibility is achieved and further application and popularization are facilitated
