 CNU The utility model discloses a loader drive axle hub and brake disc assembly which comprises a hub and a movable disc face The hub comprises a planet carrier mounting ring a rim mounting spigot a rim bolt mounting hole a spoke plate a left bearing mounting seat a bearing seat outer ring a right bearing mounting seat and an oil seal mounting seat the brake disc surface and the hub are integrally cast in order to prevent the bottom of the oil seal installation position from being too thin to affect the strength of the hub a degree oblique angle transition is adopted at the transition connection position of the bearing seat outer ring and the brake disc face And in order to avoid stress concentration chamfers R for transition are respectively arranged at the bevel angle and the outer ring of the bearing seat and at the root parts of the bevel angle and the brake disc surface The loader drive axle hub and brake disc assembly is simple in structure easy and convenient to machine and manufacture low in cost safe and reliable to use and capable of obviously improving the quality of a brake disc and reducing the manufacturing cost of the hub and the brake disc
