 CNA The invention relates to the technical field of hub motors of electric vehicles in particular to an inward swinging type flail block centrifugal clutch The inward swinging type flail block centrifugal clutch comprises a center shaft a motor and a hub arranged on the center shaft in a sleeving mode the left side of the motor is provided with an overrunning clutch the outside of the overrunningclutch is connected with a planetary assembly through a center gear the inner side of the planetary assembly is connected with the motor through a motor left inner cover and the outer side of the planetary assembly is connected with a brake part through a left end cover and the right side of the motor is connected with an inner swing type flail block mechanism through a right inner cover of the motor the outer side of the inner swing type flail block mechanism is connected with a right end cover and the right end cover and the left end cover are connected with the hub The inward swinging type flail block centrifugal clutch is adopted for driving power output and therefore hub rotation is achieved and compared with a traditional structure the inward swinging type flail block centrifugal clutch is simple in structure free in gear shifting and speed changing more stable in transmission high in transmission efficiency low in production cost and easy to popularize and use
