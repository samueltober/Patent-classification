 CNU     A sensor support column and a sensor protection structure using the support column include an annular cavity for accommodating a coil a hollow shaft is arranged in the middle of the annular cavity and a support column is arranged at one end of the hollow shaft The supporting cylinder is provided with a first cavity for accommodating permanent magnets on the side facing the hollow shaft The first cavity is connected to the second cavity formed by the hollow shaft There is also a third cavity on the upper side for arranging external connection components and the third cavity is open to the connection port on one side of the second cavity In the present application the first cavity and the second cavity formed by the hollow shaft of the winding coil are arranged continuously so that the permanent magnet and the iron core placed in the second cavity can be conveniently protected and then the second cavity is arranged With three cavities the components that can derive the signal are also fully externally protected
