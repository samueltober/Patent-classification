The invention discloses a method for designing a power electronic transformer converter with reactive power capacity The invention relates to a highfrequency power electronic transformer converter power supply which belongs to highfrequency power electronic transformers Voltage converter field It is designed to meet the demand for power supply of noload lightload or heavyload constantvariable loads It is equipped with a high turnon voltage that is higher than the traditional low saturation turnon voltage to greatly reduce the turnon Hourly carrier density is designed to design highfrequency power electronic transformer converter using the energy storage circuit composed of highspeed power electronic switching devices or modules and LC elements to increase the switching speed to provide reactive power capacity It can be used to replace various transformers of power frequency transformers including UHV transformers
