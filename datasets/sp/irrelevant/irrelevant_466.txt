 WOA The invention relates to an active roll stabilizer comprising a divided torsion bar  and an actuator  The actuator is arranged between the torsion bar portions   of the torsion bar  which torsion bar portions are arranged one after the other The actuator comprises a housing  connected to the first torsion bar portion  for conjoint rotation a motor  and a reduction gear  which is driven by the motor  and the output shaft  of which on the gear output side is connected to the second torsion bar portion  for conjoint rotation The second torsion bar portion  and the output shaft  are directly integrally joined to one another in particular by friction welding
