 CNA     The present invention is an active suspension control method based on road surface level recognition and multitarget dynamic optimization It is offline for the different requirements of vehicles under different road surface levels for ride comfort impact rubber limit safety and handling stability Optimized to obtain the LQR optimal Q and R weighting matrices with different weights for three evaluation indicators of vehicle body vertical acceleration ride comfort suspension dynamic deflection probability of hitting rubber limit and wheel dynamic load wheel attachment safety The vehicles vertical dynamic response is measured by onboard sensors and the intelligent clustering algorithm is used to identify the road level online so as to switch the optimal Q and R weighting matrices according to the current road level to achieve the multiobjective optimization of different weights under different road levels Excellent control
