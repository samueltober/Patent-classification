 CNA An integrated module  for a vehicle suspension includes a bearing element  and a suspension upright or knuckle  the bearing element is a rolling bearing outer ring  made of a first material and at least part of the suspension upright or knuckle  is made of a second material The second material is metallic is lighter than the first material and is comoulded on the external sideof the bearing element  in the radial direction The suspension upright or knuckle  is directly fixed to the bearing element  by means of an interface  for form coupling Opposite axial ends  of the bearing element  are configured to receive closing and sealing devices  for protecting corresponding races  of the bearing element during comoulding and for removing heat from the races during comoulding
