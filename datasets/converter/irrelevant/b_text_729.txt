 CNA The invention provides a power conversion system which comprises at least one power conversion circuit and is characterized in that each power conversion circuit comprises an input an output at least one resonant capacitor and two switching power conversion units and the resonant capacitor is coupled in series between the input and the output The two switch power conversion units are coupledbetween the input and the output in series each switch power conversion unit comprises a plurality of switches and windings and the plurality of switches are coupled in series and periodically actuate according to a switching period In any power conversion circuit the terminals of the two windings at the synonym ends are electrically connected to the output the two windings form a transformer and the resonant capacitor and the resonant inductor resonate by controlling the on or off of a plurality of switches
