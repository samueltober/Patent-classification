 CNA The invention relates to the field of unmanned equipment in particular to a suspension structure and unmanned equipment The suspension structure comprises rollers roller arms shock absorbers and racks The rollers are rotatably connected to the first ends of the roller arms the second ends of the roller arms are rotatably connected to the racks and the rotating axles of the rollers and the axles of the roller arms rotating relative to the racks are not superposed and one ends of the shock absorbers are connected to the second ends of the roller arms while the other ends of the shock absorbers are connected to the racks The suspension structure can improve the smoothness and the trafficability characteristic of the unmanned equipment
