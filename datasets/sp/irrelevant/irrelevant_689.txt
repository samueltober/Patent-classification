 KRB The present invention provides a hydraulic damper including a cylinder  having a cylindrical bore a piston  which penetrates at least a part of the cylinder  along the central axis of the cylinder  and is movable axially relative to the cylinder  and a damping fluid filled in the bore A blade  to allow relative rotation with respect to the piston  is installed in the piston  of the hydraulic damper The cylinder  and the piston  are equipped with an energy harvesting module for converting the rotational kinetic energy of the blade  into electrical energy
