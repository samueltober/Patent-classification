 CNU The utility model provides a hub assembly suitable for wheel edge driving and relates to the field of parts According to the hub assembly suitable for wheel edge driving a wheel bolt is annularly pressed and fixed to the outer surface of a hub a bearing unit is pressed and mounted in the axis of the hub a limiting cover plate is fixed to the side away from a brake disc of the hub through abolt and an induction gear ring is pressed and fixed to the inner ring of the brake disc According to the wheel hub assembly suitable for wheel edge driving the limiting cover plate is fixed to thewheel hub through the fixing bolt assembling is easy a bearing unit is used for replacing two traditional independent tapered rollers or ball bearings and meanwhile the wheel hub sealing mode is changed into a static pressure sealing mode from a dynamic rotation sealing mode an Oshaped ring used for static pressure sealing is integrated into the bearing unit bearing adjustment clearance andhub oil seal installation work are omitted in the assembling process the assembling link is greatly simplified and the assembling efficiency is improved
