 CNA The present invention addresses the problem of providing a vehicle wheel bearing device that can improve the ease of installing balls in a retainer while also increasing the number of balls This vehicle wheel bearing device  is equipped with a retainer  in which a pocket Pt is formed by adjacent pillar sections b and a base section a that is therebetween and which retains a ball  in the pocket Pt Formed in the pillar sections b are a claw portion e and another short claw portion g which project towards adjacent pillar sections as well as a notched section i cut toward the base section from the distal end radially inward of the claw portions of the pillar sections b When viewed from the axial direction the shortest length in the one claw portion eto the center of the ball and the shortest length in the other short claw portion g to the center of the ball are different
