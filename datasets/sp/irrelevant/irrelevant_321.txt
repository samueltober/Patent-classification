 CNB The invention discloses a wheel base adjusting device for a vehicle The wheel base adjusting device comprises a suspension mounting seat mounted on a cross arm of the vehicle a wheel base change driver mounted on a frame and used for pushing a suspension cross arm and wheels to move relative to the frame together guide wheel assemblies mounted on the both sides of the suspension mounting seat and a locking mechanism assembly mounted on the suspension mounting seat when the suspension cross arm moves the guide wheel assemblies are in rolling contact with the upper surface of a frame longitudinal beam and when the suspension cross arm is in a nonmoving state the suspension mounting seat and a longitudinal beam of the vehicle are locked through the locking mechanism assembly The wheel base adjusting device has the advantages of being simple in structure capable of adjusting the wheel base of the vehicle according to the road surface condition and the like
