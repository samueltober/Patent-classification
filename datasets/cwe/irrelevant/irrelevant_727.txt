 CNA The invention relates to a hub bearing sealing cover and a vehicle The hub bearing sealing cover comprises an outer end cover an annular side wall capable of extending into a port of a hub bearing flange a locking piece arranged on the annular side wall and capable of expanding outwards and a driving piece used for driving the locking piece and the outer end cover is provided with a via hole for the driving piece to extend into the annular side wall so that the locking piece is driven by the driving piece to expand outwards and after the driving piece retreats from the annular side wall the locking piece retracts inwards elastically The hub bearing sealing cover disclosed by the invention can be conveniently mounted and dismounted and a sealing coating is prevented from falling off to pollute grease of the bearing
