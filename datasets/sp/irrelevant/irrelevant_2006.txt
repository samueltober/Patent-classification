 CNA     A highly adaptable wheeled mobile robot relates to a robot Each of the tires is driven to rotate through a corresponding gear train transmission mechanism each of the gear train transmission mechanisms is connected to the main frame through a corresponding suspension mechanism and an oil is provided between the gear train transmission mechanism and the main frame Pressure buffer The invention is a wheeled mobile robot with high adaptability abilities such as climbing obstacle crossing antifalling and maneuvering It can be dropped at a height of up to m At the same time through modular installation it is easy to replace and repair with light weight simple structure and low cost
