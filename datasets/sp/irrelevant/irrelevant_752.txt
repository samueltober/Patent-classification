 CNA The invention relates to the technical field of auto spare parts and discloses a suspension structure used for distributed drive The suspension structure comprises a supporting frame A rocker arm is fixedly mounted in the middle of the upper portion of the supporting frame and a shock absorber is fixedly mounted in the middle of the bottom of the supporting frame A shock absorption spring isfixedly mounted on the surface of the shock absorber A generator is fixedly mounted on the upper portion of the shock absorber A dual connecting rod is arranged on the left side of the supporting frame According to the suspension structure used for distributed drive the suspension basic structure is arranged to be of a dual connecting rod parallelogram structure an upper connecting rod and alower connecting rod are driven by a bearing or universal joint to achieve free swing of the dual connecting rod meanwhile in order to control the upanddown swing speed of a suspension system better to achieve flexible swing an ejector rod is arranged on the lower connecting rod through upanddown movement of the rocker arm borne force is finally transmitted to the shock absorber and the shock absorption spring flexible buffering of the automobile structure is achieved and the comfort feeling of users is increased
