 DEB     The invention relates to a fastening arrangement for a stabilizer  of a motor vehicle comprising a stabilizer clamp  for mounting the stabilizer  and a motor vehicle component  to which the stabilizer clamp  is fastened by means of fastening means The invention is characterized in that that the motor vehicle component  has a first bearing bracket  for mounting a first chassis link  and a second bearing bracket  for mounting a second chassis link  and that the stabilizer clamp  is attached to the first by means of a first fastening means Bearing bracket  of the first suspension arm and is fastened to the second bearing bracket  of the second suspension arm by means of a second fastening means
