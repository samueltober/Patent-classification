 CNB The present invention relates to a washer coupled between a constant velocity joint  and a wheel hub  The washer is formed into a platelike annular body in which a copper alloy layer  and a Teflon layer  are formed on the surface of a steel base plate  and which has an opening  having an inner diameter exceeding the diameter of a shaft  of the constant velocity joint formed at the center portion thereof Through the present invention it is possible to tightly bind contact surfaces between the constant velocity joint  and the wheel hub  thereby minimizing vibrations and noise generated when running a vehicle
