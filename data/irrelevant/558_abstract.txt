A multipleinputmultipleoutput power supply apparatus and an integrated cabinet The apparatus comprises a power conversion unit  an output power distribution unit  and a monitoring unit  the power conversion unit  is used for converting a plurality of input power sources into preset direct current voltages and converging the preset direct current voltages onto a direct current bus the output power distribution unit  is used for taking electricity from the direct current bus and distributing power according to different loads and the monitoring unit  is used for managing controlling and scheduling respective modules of the power conversion unit  and the output power distribution unit
