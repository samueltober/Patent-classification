 WOA A safety hub  for a tire burst comprising a hub and a tire  The hub is formed by connecting inner and outer hubs the inner and outer hubs respectively comprise tire fastening portions and are connected to each other along the outer side of the inner hub  and the inner side of the outer hub  a shock absorbing tire  is mounted at the outer edge of the outer hub The advantageous effects of the present apparatus are a simple structure convenient mounting good stability low processing cost and easy popularization When a tire burst occurs in an automobile the present invention can replace wheels to continue to travel on the ground thereby effectively controlling the potential safety hazards such as rollover sideskidding and deflection generated by a vehicle at the moment of the tire burst avoiding a serious traffic accident of fatal car crash and improving the safety of the vehicle when the tire burst occurs moreover the vehicle can continue to travel to a safe place The present invention is very convenient to use
