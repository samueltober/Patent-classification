 KRA A stop disk  for an elastomer bearing  is integrally produced using a sheetlike metal plate  through a molding process and is provided to have a surface protection unit  In addition to a function as a movement limiter the stop disk has at least one of the following functions that is at least one of the functions of a screw andor antirotation device due to a structure integrated during the molding process of the sheetlike metal plate  In addition the elastomer bearing having such a stop disk  and a subframe having the same are embodied
