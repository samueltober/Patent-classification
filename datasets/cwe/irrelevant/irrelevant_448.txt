 CNA The invention discloses a hydraulic drive axle which comprises wheels a connecting sleeve a flange plate a cover plate a connecting body a bearing a hydraulic motor a drive axle frame and a drum brake the wheels are connected to the connecting sleeve the connecting sleeve is connected with an outer ring of the bearing in a matched mode an inner ring of the bearing is installed with the connecting body in a matched mode and the hydraulic motor is fixed to the connecting body The connecting body is sleeved on an output shaft of the hydraulic motor the flange plate is fixedly connected to the connecting sleeve an inner ring of the flange plate is fixedly connected to the output shaft of the hydraulic motor the drum brake is installed on the connecting body the cover plate is connected to the output shaft of the hydraulic motor and the drive axle bridge is connected to the connecting body The hydraulic drive axle can be suitable for shortdistance lowspeed transition transportation and longdistance highspeed transition transportation at the same time
