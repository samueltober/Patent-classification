 CNU The embodiment of the utility model provides a rack and a vehicle and relates to the technical field of vehicle parts The rack provided by the embodiment of the utility model comprises a body the body is provided with an inner cavity for the input shaft to penetrate through the inner cavity is internally provided with a first bulge and a second bulge the first bulge and the second bulge are arranged at an interval so as to form an oil storage space between the first bulge and the second bulge and lubricating oil entering the inner cavity can be limited in the oil storage space The oil return hole is formed in the body the oil storage space is communicated with the mounting space through the oil return hole so that lubricating oil in the oil storage space lubricates the bearing inthe mounting space through the oil return hole the lubricating effect is good and the service life of the bearing is prolonged
