 CNA The invention relates to a wheel device  The wheel device is provided with a hub  and the hub  is rotatably mounted about an axis of rotation X of the hub and is provided with at least one accommodating hole  wherein a rim  is arranged on the hub  and has an insertion opening  corresponding to the at least one accommodating hole  a braking device is arranged between the wheel hub  and the wheel rim  and a base body  of the braking device has a through hole  corresponding to the insertion opening  at least one wheel bolt  for fastening the wheel rim  to the wheel hub  is provided and it is proposed that the central axes X X of the accommodating hole  and of the insertion opening  are offset from each other in the radial direction with respect to the rotational axis X of the hub  parallel thereto
