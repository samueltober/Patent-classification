 EPB An internal gear constituting a transmission is supported and fixed in relation to a rotationside flange provided to the outer end of a hub in the axial direction and the hub is rotationally driven on the basis of the driving force inputted via the internal gear As a result it is not necessary to connect the driving shaft that rotationally drives the hub to the center hole of the hub and thus a generator and a wireless communications device are disposed in a housing space that has a large space provided to the inner side of the hub in the axial direction
