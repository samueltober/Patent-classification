 CNA The invention discloses a Tshaped spherical hinge and relates to the technical field of spherical hinges The Tshaped spherical hinge comprises a ball stud base a ball stud and a ball stud protective cover the head of the ball stud is rotationally arranged in a space defined by the ball stud base and the ball stud protective cover the Tshaped spherical hinge further comprises two rotating bearings and connecting arms are arranged on the two rotating bearings respectively Two mounting shafts are symmetrically arranged on the ball stud base and the two rotating bearings are respectively arranged on the corresponding mounting shafts The Tshaped spherical hinge is more reasonable in structure safer more reliable higher in strength and durability and higher in capacity of bearingforce and vibration transmitted by wheels and can completely meet the requirement for large vertical jumping stroke of the wheels so that a vehicle can adapt to more complex road conditions and wheel shimmy and abnormal noise cannot be caused
