 CNU The utility model discloses a fourthgeneration hub bearing unit The problem that when an existing hub bearing unit is connected with a universal joint the structure is complex is mainly solved wherein a through hole  is axially formed in the inner flange the universal joint shell comprises a bellshaped shell  and a shaft rod  the shaft rod extends into the through hole to be buckled and pressed on the inner wall of the inner flange the bellshaped shell abuts against the front end of the inner flange and a roller path  matched with the rolling ball in a rolling mode is arranged on the bellshaped shell According to the fourthgeneration hub bearing unit the hub bearing unit and the universal joint are connected into a whole a half inner ring is omitted and thefourthgeneration hub bearing unit is compact in overall structure and low in weight
