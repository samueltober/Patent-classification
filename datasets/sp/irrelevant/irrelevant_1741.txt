 CNU The invention relates to an independent suspension device and a vehicle The independent suspension device comprises a vehicle frame the suspension is connected with the frame and comprises a support arranged on the frame a lower swing arm arranged below the frame and a damping device arranged on the support the wheel assembly is connected with the lower swing arm and the damping device the steering system comprises a steering gear and a steering knuckle the steering gear is arranged at the bottom of the support and the steering knuckle is connected with the wheels the steering gear the lower swing arm and the damping device The independent suspension device and all parts of the vehicle are high in connection strength stability and reliability and meanwhile a large amount of space is saved for the head and the bottom of the vehicle
