 DEA     The invention relates to a suspension strut bearing for a suspension of a motor vehicle comprising a serving as strut connector guide ring  and a detachably connected thereto and serving as a body connection cap  and a coaxially disposed therebetween axial and radial bearings  for motion compensation said labyrinth sealant are provided to protect the axial and radial bearing  against contamination wherein the labyrinth sealing means comprise a plurality of outer edge side of the cap  integrally formed and radially extending cap blades  which cooperate with a plurality of outside edge of the guide ring  and formed to radially outwardly extending guide ring fins  to form the labyrinth seal
