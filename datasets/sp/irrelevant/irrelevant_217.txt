 CNA The invention relates to a wheel suspension arrangement for a vehicle The wheel suspension arrangement is used for suspending at least one wheel  The wheel is supported by a wheel frame  of the vehicle wherein the wheel  can rotate around a wheel shaft  The wheel suspension arrangement  comprises a transverse stabilizer  which comprises a stabilizer end  anda bearing  The bearing is fixed on the wheel frame  and is suitable for performing movable supporting on the stabilizer end  relative to the transverse direction  of the wheel shaft  of the wheel
