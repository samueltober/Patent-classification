The invention discloses a control method and system for suppressing highvoltage directcurrent commutation failure of a flexible power grid commutation converter and the method comprises the following steps cascaded Hbridge type controllable voltage sources are connected in series at a converter transformer valve side of a highvoltage directcurrent power transmission inversion side and thecontrollable voltage sources and the power grid commutation converter form a flexible LCC and the controllable voltage sources output capacitive voltage by adopting a continuous control method According to the invention the flexible LCC can realize respective control of the DC bus voltage and the actual extinction angle in a conventional operation state when voltage drop of different degrees occurs in the alternating current power grid the controllable voltage sources can effectively restrain the problem of commutation failure of the power grid commutation converter
