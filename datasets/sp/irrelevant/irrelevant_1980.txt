 CNA     The invention provides a rear wheel structure of a container cargo loader which belongs to the technical field of container cargo loader It solves the technical problems of large space occupied by the rear wheel of the existing loader and high maintenance cost The rear wheel structure of the container cargo loader includes a mounting bracket The upper side of the mounting bracket is provided with a hydraulic cylinder The hydraulic cylinder is installed vertically The end is fixedly connected to the middle of the mounting bracket Both ends of the mounting bracket are provided with small tires The center of the small tire is provided with a central shaft Both sides of the mounting bracket are provided with flanges The two ends of the central shaft and the installation The bracket is fixedly connected by a flange and a maintenancefree bearing is sleeved on the central shaft at the inner position of the small tire The invention occupies a small space does not require maintenance and has low use cost
